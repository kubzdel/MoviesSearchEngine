He becomes a student of Master Duang, a famous master musician. Master Duang owned the mysterious drum called [[poeng mang]], which is said to have been made by the skin of a beautiful woman named Thip. Ping falls in love with a beautiful girl who teaches him how to master drum skills but she is in fact the ghost living inside the drum. Meanwhile, an arrogant musician called Muan tries to convince all the students to leave Master Duang. Some students die horribly.
