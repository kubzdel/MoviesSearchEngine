It is the 17th of the 19 films in the ''Troublesome Night'' film series. ==Plot== Bud Gay is arrested for drug trafficking and sent to prison. When his mother, Mrs Bud Lung, visits him, she senses that a ghost is haunting him so she sends Bud Yan to help him. They learn that the spirit is a [[vengeful ghost]] of a woman, who had followed suit after her husband committed suicide when he was wrongfully imprisoned. All she wants is to see her husband's grave, which is located behind the prison.
