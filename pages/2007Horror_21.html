German }} '''''Barricade''''' is a 2007 [[horror movie|horror]]–[[splatter film|splatter]] feature film directed by [[Timo Rose]], written by Timo Rose and [[Ted Geoghegan]], and starring American actors [[Raine Brown]] and [[Joe Zaso]] and Germany's André Reissig, Thomas Kercmar, Manoush, and Andreas Pape. == Plot == The film follows three friends, Nina (Brown), Michael (Zaso) and David (Reissig), as they travel to the [[Black Forest]] of [[Germany]].
