[[Standard Cantonese|Cantonese]] | budget = | gross = }} '''''Red Nights''''' (''Les Nuits rouges du Bourreau de Jade'') is a 2010 French-Hong Kong film by Julien Carbon and Laurent Courtiaud. It is a thriller and a tale of erotic horror. This legends repeats nowadays when a French woman flees to Hong Kong after killing her lover and stealing from him an old artifact containing this poison.
