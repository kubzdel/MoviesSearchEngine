[[Dante Basco]] | studio = Upper Laventille Productions Inc. | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2014|06|13}} | country = United States | runtime = 90 minutes | language = English | budget = $1.5 Million }} '''''Murder 101''''' (also stylized as ''Murder101'' and known under the working title of ''C.L.A.S.S.'') is an American horror film that was directed by [[Michael Phillip Edwards]]. The film was released to DVD on June 10, 2014 and had a limited theatrical release on June 13, 2014. It stars [[Tom Sizemore]] and Paige LaPierre, and follows a female law student that gets caught up in a twisted game of cat and mouse.{{cite web|last=Serafini|first=Matt|title=First Look at Tom Sizemore in C.L.A.S.S.|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/35472/first-look-tom-sizemore-class#axzz338R5HALA|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=29 May 2014}} Filming took place in [[Los Angeles, California]] during 2010.{{cite web|last=Turek|first=Ryan|title=Indie Watch: Sizemore Enrolls in C.L.A.S.S.|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/13768-indie-watch-sizemore-enrolls-in-class/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=29 May 2014}} == Synopsis == Fiona (Paige LaPierre) is a beautiful and highly intelligent young law student that is eager to take on her latest class, a course in [[criminology]] by Professor Mark Sloan (Randy Irwin). She's horror struck when a series of brutal and seemingly random slayings begin around the campus community, leading Detective Caterson (Sheldon F. Robins) to suspect that it's the work of a [[mass murderer]]. Unwilling to watch the deaths keep piling up without doing anything, Fiona decides to investigate the murders- a decision made partially due to fact that her own father died at the hands of a mass murderer that was never caught or identified.
