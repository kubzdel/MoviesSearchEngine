English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Tragic Theater''''' is a 2015 [[Philippine|Filipino]] [[horror film|supernatural horror film]] based on the book of the same name written by G.M. Coronel, starring [[Andi Eigenmann]], [[Christopher De Leon]] and [[John Estrada]]. It was released on January 8, 2015 by [[VIVA Films|Viva Films]].{{cite news|url=http://www.philippineedition.com/2014/12/andi-eigenmanns-horror-movie-tragic.html|title=Andi Eigenmann's Horror Movie 'Tragic Theater' Marked As 'X-Rated' By MTRCB!|date=December 2014|work=Philippine Edition|accessdate=July 19, 2015}} == Plot == Many years ago, a group of construction workers were building a theater when the ceiling collapsed; many survived, but more than a hundred men died when the ceiling fell upon them. They were buried beneath the theater. Annie ([[Andi Eigenmann]]), an engineer, wants to consult a group of exorcists and spirit mediums led by a priest ([[John Estrada]]) to help her gather information about an old theater with a dark past before she starts her project there.
