It stars [[Paul McCarthy-Boyington]], [[Eddie McGee]] and [[Trista Robinson]] as a group of people who find themselves forced to race or die. == Synopsis == About eighty people are horrified to wake in a strange institutional setting, with the only common factor between them the knowledge that prior to their abductions they witnessed a sudden flash of white light. A voice in their heads demands that they all participate in a gruesome race to the finish. The rules are that they must all participate and that if they stray from the marked paths in any way or if they are lapped twice, they will die. Only one person will survive. Many die instantly, but others are forced to their deaths by other racers.
