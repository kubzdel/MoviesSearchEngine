{{Infobox film | name = The Dead Inside | image = File:The Dead Inside movie poster.jpg | alt = | caption = | film name = | director = Travis Betz | producer = | writer = Travis Betz | screenplay = | story = | based on = | narrator = | starring = [[Sarah Lassez]], Dustin Fasching | music = Michael Brake, Joel Van Vliet | cinematography = Shannon Hourigan | editing = Travis Betz | studio = Drexel Box Productions, Hundy Gilbert Media, Robot vs Dinosaur | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2011|12|20|Blue Whiskey Independent Film Festival}} | runtime = 98 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Dead Inside''''' is a 2011 musical horror film that was directed by Travis Betz.{{cite web|title=Travis Betz Goes Horror-Musical with ‘The Dead Inside’|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/17875/travis-betz-goes-horror-musical-with-the-dead-inside/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=5 May 2014}} The film had its world premiere on December 20, 2011 at the Blue Whiskey Independent Film Festival and was released to DVD on November 20, 2012. It stars [[Sarah Lassez]] and Dustin Fasching as a young couple that must deal with supernatural along with their own personal demons. Betz was inspired to make the film after he and his girlfriend had both been "creatively dead for quite some time" and he'd woken one night to hear her making strange noises while she slept.{{cite web|title=Shriekfest 2011: Exclusive Q&A with Filmmaker Travis Betz: The Dead Inside|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/47637/shriekfest-2011-exclusive-qa-filmmaker-travis-betz-dead-inside#axzz30oOcdMN2|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=5 May 2014}} This prompted him to come up with the idea for ''The Dead Inside'' and he chose to make it a musical after watching Sarah Lassez perform at a [[karaoke bar]]. ==Synopsis== Fi ([[Sarah Lassez]]) and Wes (Dustin Fasching) are deeply in love, but they find themselves forced to take unfulfilling jobs in order to make ends meet. Wes would rather take pictures of things that interest him, but has to work as a [[Wedding photography|wedding photographer]]. Fi has been suffering from [[writer's block]] and her steady job as the author of ''The Dead Survive'', a series of zombie-themed books, isn't helping her creative juices flow. Their tenuous existence is further threatened when Fi begins to develop signs of [[schizophrenia]] that are revealed to be possibly demonic in nature. ==Cast== *[[Sarah Lassez]] as Fi *[[Dustin Fasching]] as Wes ==Reception== ''[[Fangoria]]'' gave the film three out of four skulls and stated that the movie was "a gratifying, rarely predictable experience that works as a musical, relationship drama and horror film simultaneously."{{cite web|last=Hanley|first=Ken W.|title=“THE DEAD INSIDE” (DVD Review)|url=http://www.fangoria.com/new/the-dead-inside-dvd-review/|publisher=Fangoria|accessdate=5 May 2014}} ''[[Ain't It Cool News]]'' praised the film's acting and music, but stated that the movie had "a few pacing issues towards the end as it feels the angsty lyrical hysterics seem to overstay their welcome".{{cite web|title=AICN HORROR looks at SLEEP TIGHT, ATTACK OF THE HERBALS, THE SHRINE, THE DEAD INSIDE, THE SILENT HOUSE, EVERYONE MUST DIE, THE COLLECTIVE Vol4, plus Bug defends TEXAS CHAINSAW 3D!|url=http://www.aintitcool.com/node/60299#5|publisher=AICN|accessdate=5 May 2014}} ===Awards=== *''Best Score Award'' at the Los Angeles United Film Festival (2011, won){{cite web|title=The Dead Inside|url=http://orlandofilmfest.com/film/the-dead-inside-feature/|publisher=OFF|accessdate=5 May 2014}} *''Shriekfest Award for Best Supernatural Feature Film'' at [[Shriekfest]] (2011, won){{cite web|title=Winners, 2011|url=http://www.shriekfest.com/winners/#winners2011|publisher=Shriekfest|accessdate=5 May 2014}} *''Dan Harkins Breakthrough Filmmaker Award'' at the [[Phoenix Film Festival]] (2011, won){{cite web|title=Feature Film Awards|url=http://www.phoenixfilmfestival.com/feature-film-awards/|publisher=PFF|accessdate=5 May 2014}} ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * {{official website|http://www.thedeadinside.com/}} * {{IMDb title|1582270}} {{DEFAULTSORT:Dead Inside, The}} [[Category:2011 films]] [[Category:2011 horror films]] [[Category:2010s musical films]]
