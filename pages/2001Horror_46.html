{{Multiple issues| {{unreferenced|date=October 2015}} {{notability|Films|date=October 2015}} }} '''''Horror Vision''''' is a 2001 [[horror film]] directed by [[Danny Draven]] and produced by [[Chuck Williams (actor)|Chuck Williams]]. The story for the movie was written by JR Bookwalter and the [[screenplay]] was written by Scott Phillips. ==Plot== The [[website]] "horrorvision.com" has a mysterious secret... anyone who logs onto it winds up dead. After Dez, a web programmer, logs in his girlfriend and others are attacked.
