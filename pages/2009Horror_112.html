monterey media (U.S.) | released = {{Film date|2009|11|10}} | country = United States | language = English }} '''''Hurt''''' is a dramatic Gothic horror-thriller film released in 2009. The film was directed by Barbara Stepansky and stars actors [[Melora Walters]], [[William Mapother]], [[Sofia Vassilieva]], and [[Jackson Rathbone]]. ==Synopsis== The Coltrane family's life has been devastated by an untimely death. Widowed Helen Coltrane (Melora Walters – "Big Love"), along with her teenage son (Jackson Rathbone – "Twilight") and daughter, are given shelter by her reclusive and quirky gun-loving brother-in-law. As they grapple with the reality of their shattered, altered life and twist of fate, coincidence steps in with a seemingly lovely foster child ([[Sofia Vassilieva]]), who appears touting a story that Helen's husband had pledged to take her in.
