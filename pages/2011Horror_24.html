Despite its name, it is unrelated to ''[[The Legend of Boggy Creek]]'' or its two sequels. == Plot == {{more plot|date=May 2018}} Jennifer takes several of her friends to a remote cabin in Texas where her father died. There, locals warn them of hostile creatures that, according to legend, murder the men and abduct the women.
