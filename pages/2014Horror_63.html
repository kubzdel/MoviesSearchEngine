Canada | language = English }} '''''Devil's Mile''''' is a 2014 American-Canadian [[horror film]] and the feature film [[directorial debut]] of Joseph O'Brien, who also co-wrote the script and created the film's special effects.{{cite web|last1=Gingold|first1=Michael|title=Distrib news, new poster, exclusive comments: "DEVIL’S MILE"|url=http://www.fangoria.com/new/distrib-news-new-poster-exclusive-comments-devils-mile/|website=Fangoria|publisher=Fangoria|accessdate=21 July 2014}} The film premiered at the Fantasia Film Festival on July 26, 2014 and is scheduled for release on DVD and VOD on August 12, 2014. It stars [[David Hayter]] as a [[mob enforcer]] that finds himself embroiled in strange supernatural occurrences while transporting hostages through a detour.{{cite web|last1=Barton|first1=Steve|title=The Devil's Mile Wraps! First Still Appears!|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/47758/devils-mile-wraps-first-still-appears#axzz384QDaGuo|website=Dread Central|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=21 July 2014}}{{cite web|first1=Mr. Disgusting|title=David Hayter to Walk ‘The Devil’s Mile’|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/25805/david-hayter-to-walk-the-devils-mile/|website=Bloody Disgusting|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=21 July 2014}} Of the film's casting, O'Brien chose to bring on del Mar due to having worked with her in the past and chose Hayter due to being familiar with his work.{{cite web|last1=Wilson|first1=Staci Layne|title=Joseph O'Brien talks Walking the Devil's Mile|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/77127/joseph-obrien-talks-walking-devils-mile#axzz384QDaGuo|website=Dread Central|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=21 July 2014}} ==Synopsis== Toby ([[David Hayter]]), Cally ([[Maria del Mar (actress)|Maria del Mar]]), and Jackie (Casey Hudecki) are kidnappers trying to deliver their two captives to Mr. Arkadi (Frank Moore), their employer and a powerful mob boss. The trip is long and things aren't helped out by Toby being easy to rile up. They decide to take a detour in the hopes of making it to their destination that much quicker, but things take a turn for the worse when one of their captives is accidentally killed and the group finds that they have stumbled into something supernatural. ==Cast== * [[David Hayter]] as Toby McTeague * [[Maria del Mar (actress)|Maria del Mar]] as Cally * Casey Hudecki as Jacinta "Jackie" * Amanda Joy Lim as Kanako Kobayashi * Frank Moore as Mr. Arkadi * Adrienne Kress as Cally's Girlfriend * Samantha Wan as Suki Tsuburaya * Shara Kim as Demon * Craig Porritt as The Caretaker ==Reception== ''[[Shock Till You Drop]]'' panned the film overall, criticizing the film's script and direction, as they felt that O'Brien "goes overboard with the tricks and gimmicks."{{cite web|last1=Doro|first1=Paul|title=Review: Set Your GPS to Avoid Devil’s Mile|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/362313-review-devils-mile-horror-movie/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=11 August 2014}} ''[[Bloody Disgusting]]'' was also highly critical of the film and commented that they saw the film as being "weird for weirdness sake, without any real thematic or pulpy endgame in mind" and wrote that "It’s like filmmaker Joseph O’Brien is pounding you over the head with his alleged self-importance without having anything to back it up. It’s a frustrating experience."{{cite web|last1=Cooper|first1=Patrick|title=[Review] Travel the ‘Devil’s Mile’|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3298891/review-devils-mile-frustrating-mess/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=11 August 2014}} In contrast, ''[[Twitch Film]]'' gave an overly favorable review for ''Devil's Mile'', praising the film's visual elements, script, and post effects, stating that "O'Brien displays the right amount of patience to get the right amount of tension out of his audience."{{cite web|last1=Mack|first1=Andrew|title=Fantasia 2014 Review: Can You Escape From THE DEVIL'S MILE?|url=http://twitchfilm.com/2014/07/fantasia-2014-review-can-you-escape-from-the-devils-mile.html|publisher=Twitch Film|accessdate=11 August 2014}} == References == {{Reflist}} == External links == * {{IMDb title|2018136|Devil's Mile}} [[Category:2014 films]] [[Category:2014 horror films]] [[Category:2010s thriller films]]
