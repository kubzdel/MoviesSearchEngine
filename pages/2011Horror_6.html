After it was questioned to do [[Film theater|a theatrical release]] or rather releasing it [[direct-to-video]], the movie opened in German theaters on 4 April 2013. The film failed to open within the Top 5 of its opening week and grossed €309,172 at the end of its theatrical run. Before the official opening in Germany, ''205 - Room of Fear'' was part of the ''German films Go North'' festival and was released on 20 July 2012 in [[Turkey]]. === Home media === ''205 - Room of Fear'' will be released in Region 2 on [[DVD]] and [[Blu-ray]] on 10 October 2013 in Germany. It will be released with an [[Freiwillige Selbstkontrolle der Filmwirtschaft|FSK 16]] rating. == Reception == The response to the film has been mixed.
