It is a remake of a [[Satan's School for Girls (1973 film)|1973 ABC Movie of the Week]] of the same name. ==Plot== The TV movie is about a woman, Beth Hammersmith ([[Shannon Doherty]]), who attends Fallbridge College for Girls under the name Karen Oxford, to find out why her sister, who attended the college, is believed to have committed [[suicide]]. Once she is enrolled, she soon discovers a [[Satanic cult]] of [[witches]], who call themselves "The Five", who want Beth to join the cult.
