{{small|(Barricade Productions, Inc.)}} | distributor = WWE Studios | released = {{Film date|2012|09|25}} | runtime = 82 minutes | country = | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Barricade''''' is a 2012 [[Action film|action]] [[thriller (genre)|thriller]] directed by [[Andrew Currie (director)|Andrew Currie]]. The film stars [[Eric McCormack]]. The film was released on September 9, 2012. It is the first film entirely produced by [[WWE Studios]] to not feature a wrestler in any way.{{cite web|last1=Murray|first1=Darren|title=To Be or Not To Be DTV: The films of WWE Studios|url=http://screenanarchy.com/2017/09/to-be-or-not-to-be-dtv-the-films-of-wwe-studios-contrib.html|website=Screenanarchy|accessdate=25 September 2017|date=September 8, 2017}} ==Plot== Barricade tells the story of Terence Shade, a psychologist who doesn’t have enough time in the day to spend with his kids. His wife wants them all to go to her old family cabin way, way, way up in the mountains to give the kids a white Christmas. The story jumps to a year later, and Shade’s wife has died under mysterious circumstances, but he wants to honor her wishes and takes their two kids to the cabin.
