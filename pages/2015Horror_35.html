A sheriff and his daughter band together with several other survivors to defeat the lava-based zombies. == Cast == * Tom Downey as Sheriff Denton * Moniqua Plante as Mindy Roberts * Nicole Cummins as Nicole Roberts * Kevin Norman as Ryan Jacobs * [[Kyle T. Heffner]] as Dr. Stevens * Julia Lehman as Eve Jones * [[Tom Nagel]] as Deputy Tisdale * Adam Gregor as Ranger McGee * Jenny Lin as photographer * [[Robert F. Lyons (actor)|Robert F.
