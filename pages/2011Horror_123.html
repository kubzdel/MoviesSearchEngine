Full Fathom 5 | released = {{Film date|2011|11|01}} | runtime = 83 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Orphan Killer''''' is a 2011 independent horror film written and directed by [[Matt Farnsworth]]. It was produced by Farnsworth and Full Fathom 5.{{cite web|title=El enmascarado y su sangriento director|url=http://www.diariovasco.com/v/20111029/cultura/enmascarado-sangriento-director-20111029.html|publisher=Diario Vasco|accessdate=30 December 2012}} Farnsworth has stated that he has plans for sequels and a web series.{{cite web|title=Los seguidores del cine 'slasher' no han conocido un fenómeno así en 20 años|url=http://www.noticiasdegipuzkoa.com/2011/10/29/ocio-y-cultura/cultura/los-seguidores-del-cine-39slasher39-no-han-conocido-un-fenomeno-asi-en-20-anos|publisher=Noticias de Gipuzkoa|accessdate=30 December 2012}} ==Plot== The film follows the life of two children who become wards of the state in New Jersey after a home invasion results in the murder of their parents. Having witnessed the murder, Marcus is forever changed. The siblings are sent to a Catholic orphanage where Audrey is subsequently adopted and Marcus is left behind. He suffers abuse at the hands of the caretakers and as a punishment is masked and exiled. Never forgiving his sister for abandoning him, Marcus returns to his sister's life many years later in adulthood, still masked, wanting to teach her a lesson. ==Cast== *Diane Foster as Audrey *David Backhaus as Marcus Miller *Matt Farnsworth as Mike *[[James McCaffrey (actor)|James McCaffrey]] as Detective Jones *[[John Savage (actor)|John Savage]] as Detective Walker *[[Karen Young (actress)|Karen Young]] as Sister Mary *Charlotte Maier as Sister Constance *[[Spencer List]] as Young Marcus *Dana DeVestern as Young Audrey *Margot White as Jenny Miller *[[Mike Doyle (actor)|Mike Doyle]] as Marcus Miller Sr. *Ivan Martin as Jim *[[Karen Olivo]] as Angie *[[Matthew Arkin]] as Bob *Ezra Knight as Simon *Allison Salvetti as Ghost Orphan *Jon David Casey as Jerry ==Release== A limited test release of ''The Orphan Killer'' was distributed through Facebook.{{cite news|title=Writer/Director Matt Farnsworth Talks The Orphan Killer Past, Present and Future…And How You Can See it for Free!|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/39688/writer-director-matt-farnsworth-talks-the-orphan-killer-past-present-and-future/|accessdate=30 December 2012|work=Dread Central}} The film also premiered at the [[Sitges Film Festival]], where it was shown in the Brigadoon section of the festival.{{cite web|title=BRIGADOON 13 OCT: Asian Trash Cinema, The orphan killer & La masque de la Méduse|url=http://sitgesfilmfestival.com/eng/film/?id=10002270|publisher=Sitges Film Festival|accessdate=30 December 2012}} The film was also shown at the 30th Festival de Cine de Terror de Molins de Rei.
