Kübra is psychiatrist Ebru's childhood friend from Kıbledere's village. Ebru wants to observe Kübra for a visual phenomenon presentation and to shoot the actions taken by so-called jinn exorcists and their results. Ebru and jinn exorcist Faruk together reach the house where Kübra and her family live, and the struggle between religion and science becomes horrifying as the facts are revealed.
