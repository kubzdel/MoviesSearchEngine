[[Koch Vision]] | released = {{Film date|2000}} | runtime = 93 min. | language = English | budget = $55,000 (estimated) }} '''''Voodoo Academy''''' is a [[Horror film|Horror]]/[[Fantasy]] [[movie]], released in 2000, directed by [[David DeCoteau]]. ==Plot summary== Young Christopher has just enrolled at the prestigious Carmichael [[Bible College]], managed by the somewhat unusual Mrs. Bouvier. After some unexplained disappearances, Christopher discovers that Mrs. Bouvier and the Reverend Carmichael have some very bad intentions for the young men of their school.
