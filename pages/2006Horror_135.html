New Zealand | awards = | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Night of the Hell Hamsters''''' is a 2006 [[comedy horror]] [[short film]] in which demonic possessed hamsters terrorize a young babysitter and her boyfriend. It was rated 6.4/10 on IMDb. ==Plot== Karl pays his girlfriend Julie a surprise visit while she is babysitting her neighbour's children. He brings with him a [[Ouija board]] that Julie has been asking to play with. However, unhappy about messing around with the [[occult]], Karl has deliberately left the board at home. Determined to have her way, Julie fashions a makeshift Ouija board out of an innocent child's [[alphabet toy]], and, when Karl is bitten by one of the [[hamsters]], she uses a drop of his blood to consecrate it. The couple then attempt to summon up a spirit, unaware that they have come up with the name of a demon, which arrives in a bolt of lightning, electrocuting the family hamsters and then possessing their dead bodies, turning them into demonic possessed [[zombie]] [[hamsters]] . ==Cast== {| class="wikitable" border="1" |+ Cast !Actor !Character |- | Stephanie Ratcliff | Julie |- | Paul O'Neill | Karl |- |} Director Paul Campion and producer [[Elisabeth Pinto]] play two of the Giant Zombie Rabbits in the intro sequence. ==Festival Screenings== *2006 FantasyCon, Nottingham, England *2006 HorrorFest, England *2006 Nevermore Film Festival, North Carolina, USA *2007 A Night of Horror Film Festival, Sydney, Australia *2007 The Angry Film Festival *2007 Zompire Film Festival, USA *2007 It Came From Lake Michigan Film Festival, USA *2007 Another Hole in the Head, San Francisco, USA *2007 Dragon*Con *2007 Fantasia Film Festival, Montreal, Canada *2007 Rhode Island International Film Festival, USA *2007 Dark Carnival Film Festival, USA *2007 Vine Film Festival *2007 Encounters Short Film Festival, England *2007 Tabloid Witch Awards, USA *2007 Ravenna Nightmare Film Festival, Italy *2007 ScreamFest LA, USA *2007 Hollywood Horror Sc-Fi and Fantasy Film Festival *2007 Thriller!
