It is known more commonly by its video title, Miner's Massacre. ==Plot== A group of explorers go hiking in the forests of northern California. While exploring, they stumble upon an old mine which mined gold in the [[California Gold Rush]]. In the depths of the mine, they find a large amount of gold that they, of course, take. However, what they do not know is that the gold belonged to a miner named Jerimiah Stone, who died in the mine.
