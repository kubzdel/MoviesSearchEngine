{{Infobox film | name = Amateur Porn Star Killer | image = | caption = | director = Shane Ryan | producer = | writer = | starring = | music = | cinematography = | editing = | distributor = }} '''''Amateur Porn Star Killer''''' is an [[United States|American]] [[Horror film|horror]] [[film series]], currently consisting of three films. Created by Shane Ryan, the original film was theatrically released in 2007."Amateur Porn Star Killer Hits Theatres Today", ''News Blaze'', August 24, 2007. == First film == The first chapter of the series is a mockumentary about a [[snuff film]] shot, mainly consisting in a confrontation between the director of the snuff (played by the same Shane Ryan) and his 13 years old victim (Michiko Jimenez). According to Ryan, it was filmed in one weekend.{{cite news|last=Doug Brunell|title=Excess Hollywood: The Art Of Snuff — The Shane Ryan Interview Part One|url=http://www.filmthreat.com/features/1673/|accessdate=22 October 2013|newspaper=[[Film Threat]]|date=January 12, 2006}} The film received mixed reviews. According to the book ''From the Arthouse to the Grindhouse'', the film is "a postmodern take on the transgressive reality film, using the genre's format as a position from which to critique it".{{cite book|last=John Cline, Robert G. Weiner|title=From the Arthouse to the Grindhouse: Highbrow and Lowbrow Transgression in Cinema's First Century|publisher=Scarecrow Press, 2010|isbn=0810876558|pages=312–313}} [[DVD Verdict]]'s critic Gordon Sullivan panned the film, writing that it could be interesting as a 15-minute short, and referring to it as "boring" and lacking both "a competent audiovisual scheme and a compelling narrative".{{cite news|last=Gordon Sullivan |title=Case Number 13148 - Amateur Porn Star Killer |url=http://www.dvdverdict.com/reviews/amateurpornstarkiller.php |accessdate=22 October 2013 |newspaper=[[DVD Verdict]] |date=March 7, 2008 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131023061523/http://www.dvdverdict.com/reviews/amateurpornstarkiller.php |archivedate=23 October 2013 }} == Sequels == The other chapters of the film reprise the scheme of the first film, with just two actors (the snuff director and the victim) on scene. About the second film, according to the ''[[Film Threat]]'' review, "Ryan has, once again, created something bound to raise eyebrows and the ire of morality police everywhere. He makes no apologies for it, and he does it so well that if you go into the film with an open mind you can’t help but be impressed by how good a job he’s done."{{cite news|title=AMATEUR PORN STAR KILLER 2: THE SNUFF VERSION (DVD)|url=http://www.filmthreat.com/reviews/10859/|accessdate=22 October 2013|newspaper=[[Film Threat]]|date=May 13, 2008}} The final chapter of the film features the real pornstar Regan Reese as main actress. It was filmed in [[3D film|3D]].
