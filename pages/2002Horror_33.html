[[Fortissimo Films]] | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2002}} | runtime = | country = Hong Kong | language = Cantonese | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Era of Vampires''''' is a 2002 Hong Kong martial arts horror film directed by Wellson Chin and produced by [[Tsui Hark]]. An edited version of the film was released in North America under the title '''''Tsui Hark's Vampire Hunters'''''.
