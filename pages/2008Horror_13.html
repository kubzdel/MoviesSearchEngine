Joseph Conti | studio = | distributor = | released = {{Film date|df=yes|2008|01|15|USA}} | runtime = 90 mins | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''Army of the Dead''' is a 2008 American [[horror film]] directed by Joseph Contegiacomo aka Joseph Conti. The film centers on a university professor and his students are out a fun race in the desert and they become victim to an ancient curse. The film stars [[Ross Kelly (actor)|Ross Kelly]], Stephanie Marchese, Miguel Martinez, Mike Hatfield, Vic Browder, Jocelyn Tucker sand Audrey Anderson. ==Plot== The film begins when four century's ago, [[Francisco Vázquez de Coronado|Coronado]]'s sending a thousand me to find the legendary lost city of gold, [[El Dorado]]. The men never returned.Horrorpedia, 2 October 2017 - [https://horrorpedia.com/2017/10/02/army-of-the-dead-2008-horror-film-movie-cast-plot-reviews-trailer/ Army of the Dead – USA, 2008][[Rotten Tomatoes]] - [https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/army_of_the_dead Army of the dead (2008)] In the present, a group of friends and their university professor go to the [[Baja California Desert|Baja Desert]] to race against each other. They are assisted by their guide Fred Griffith (played by Mike Hatfield). An in-love married couple (played by Ross Kelly and Stefani Marchesi), John and Amy are among them. This race is Amy's birthday surprise to John. There are some tensions with an ex-lover in the midst. Professor Vasquez has other ideas and is using this outing to further his own agenda which is finding the lost treasure. He has also hired three commando type mercenaries to help him in his mission. The location of the treasure is located in the ''Cave of Souls'' which happens to be on a military artillery practice range which is used by the Mexican government.
