The film proved to be Richard Franklin's final project. ==Plot== The film deals with the feelings of a young woman sailing solo on a yacht around the world. The loneliness makes her start losing her sanity. The film in some ways is close to the theme of ''[[The Shining (film)|The Shining]]'' by [[Stanley Kubrick]].
