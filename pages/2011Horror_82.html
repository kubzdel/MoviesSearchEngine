The film was produced by Antoine Huet, Thomas Malmonte, Donato Rotunno, Joseph Rouschop. It stars [[Dianna Agron]], [[Steven Waddington]] and [[Tony Becker]]. It was released in limited theaters on December 27, 2011.{{cite web|title=The Hunters - News|url=http://www.thehuntersmovie.com/#/News|accessdate=October 29, 2014}} ==Plot== On the outskirts of town is a large abandoned fort turned wooded park. Detective Le Saint is an ex-soldier assigned to protect a police informant who he arranges to meet at an abandoned fort in the woods. Alice is a college student who sneaks into the fort with a friend to go exploring. Unfortunately, the fort is also used by a group of men as a base for hunting humans: vagrants, tourists, and anyone else who happens to stray too far into the woods or stay after the park closes.
