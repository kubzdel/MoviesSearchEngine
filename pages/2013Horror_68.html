{{Infobox film | name = The Demon's Rook | image = File:The Demon's Rook movie poster.jpg | alt = | caption = | film name = | director = James Sizemore | producer = | writer = James Sizemore, Akom Tidwell | screenplay = | story = | based on = | starring = Ashleigh Jo Sizemore, James Sizemore, John Chatham | narrator = | music = | cinematography = Tim Reis | editing = Tim Reis | studio = Black Rider Productions | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2013|7|27|Fantasia Film Festival)}} | runtime = 103 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Demon's Rook''''' is a 2013 independent fantasy-horror film and the [[directorial debut]] of James Sizemore (who is also known under the pseudonym of Loup'Rah Garomore).{{cite web|title=Official Trailer Now Online for Loup'Rah Garomore's The Demon's Rook|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/49776/official-trailer-now-online-louprah-garomores-demons-rook#axzz33druYxVh|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=4 June 2014}} The film had its world premiere on July 27, 2013 at the [[Fantasia Film Festival]] and stars Sizemore as Roscoe, a young man that must find a way to stop demons from overtaking the world. Funding for ''The Demon's Rook'' was partially received through crowdsourcing websites ''[[Kickstarter]]'' and ''[[Indiegogo]]''.{{cite web|title=The Demon's Rook|url=https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/the-demon-s-rook|publisher=Indiegogo|accessdate=4 June 2014}}{{cite web|title=The Demon's Rook by Loup'Rah Garomore|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/louprah/the-demons-rook|publisher=Kickstarter|accessdate=4 June 2014}} ==Synopsis== When he was a child Roscoe (James Sizemore and Emmett Eckert) was frequently visited by Dimwos (John Chatham), an elder demon, at night. One day Dimwos lures Roscoe into a portal that takes him to a strange world where Dimwos teaches Roscoe various things, including his ancient language. Dimwos withholds nothing from Roscoe, save one single secret that ends up becoming Roscoe's downfall, when he releases three deadly demons. Roscoe manages to flee by crossing back through the portal to the human world, but ends up taking the three demons with him. Once back in the human world, Roscoe recollects his previous human existence and the childhood friend he left behind, Eva (Ashleigh Jo Sizemore and Grace Kilgore), who has grown into a beautiful woman. This all causes conflict within Roscoe, as this clashes with what Dimwos had led him to believe.
