{{Infobox film | name = Pop Punk Zombies | image = Pop Punk Zombies.jpg | alt = | caption = | director = Steve Dayton | producer = {{plainlist| * Steve Dayton * Brian Leis }} | screenplay = Brian Leis | story = Steve Dayton | starring = {{plainlist| * Ian Kane * Nick Marinnuci * Laura Savage * Adam Hatfield }} | music = Tara Moulder | cinematography = Luke Gilbertson | editing = Steve Dayton | studio = | distributor = [[Brain Damage Films]] | released = {{Film date|2011|01|10}} | runtime = 84 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Pop Punk Zombies''''' is a 2011 American [[horror film]] directed by Steve Dayton and written by Brian Leis. Adam Hatfield stars as a greedy manager who brings back a punk band as zombies. == Plot == When the members of a punk band die, Dameon David, a greedy manager, brings them back to life again as zombies. David attempts to civilize the zombies as he manipulates them for his own benefit. == Cast == * Ian Kane as Eddy Whitset * Nick Marinnuci as Ciezmore Jones * Laura Savage as Lisa Corteen * Adam Hatfield as Dameon David == Production == ''Pop Punk Zombies'' was shot at The Warehouse, a local Wisconsin nightclub, in 2008 and 2009.
