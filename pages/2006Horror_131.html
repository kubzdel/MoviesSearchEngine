[[Lightyear Entertainment]] | released = {{Film date|2006|05|03}} | runtime = 87 minutes | country = United States | language = English }} '''''The Murder Game''''' is an American horror film starring Steve Polites, Katie Sirk, Samuel Klein and directed by Robert Harari. The film is distributed by [[Warner Bros.]] and [[Lightyear Entertainment]]. ==Plot== A group of teenagers create a game where one of them is secretly a killer while the others are victims. Using prop weapons, the killer must eliminate the other players before being discovered.
