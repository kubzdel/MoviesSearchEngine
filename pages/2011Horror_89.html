[[Universal Pictures]] was set release date by October 7, 2011 ==Reception== {{expand section|date=September 2014}} Critical reception for ''Intruders'' the film has been mostly negative. Film review aggregator [[Rotten Tomatoes]] reported an approval rating of 30%, based on {{nowrap|69 reviews}}, with an average rating of 4.8/10. The website's critical consensus reads, "Intruders has a fantastic first act but then settles into a bland plot and scare tactics that aren't all too scary".{{cite web|title=Intruders (2012)|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/intruders_2011/|website=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]|publisher=[[Fandango Media]]|accessdate=14 May 2018}} On [[Metacritic]], the film has a weighted average score of 45 out of 100, based on 21 critics, indicating "mixed or average reviews".{{cite web |url=http://www.metacritic.com/movie/intruders |title=Intruders Reviews |work=[[Metacritic]] |publisher=[[CBS Interactive]] |accessdate=14 May 2018}} [[Lou Lumenick]] from the [[New York Post]] liked the film's opening and atmosphere but also criticized the film as being "underwhelming".{{cite web|last1=Lumenick|first1=Lou|title=Not very forceful ‘Intruders’|url=https://nypost.com/2012/03/30/not-very-forceful-intruders/|website=New York Post.com|publisher=Lou Lumenick|accessdate=5 January 2016}} Sean Burns from [[Philadelphia Weekly]] gave the film a negative review, criticizing the film's climax, special effects, "disparate" storyline, and slow pacing.
