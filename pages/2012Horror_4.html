It was filmed in [[Los Angeles]], [[California]] and released on December 4, 2012.{{cite web|last=Allen|first=Michael|title=Nothing Happens in The Asylum's 12/12/12: A Movie Review|url=http://www.28dayslateranalysis.com/2012/12/nothing-happens-in-asylums-121212-movie.html|publisher=28dayslateranalysis.com|accessdate=17 December 2013}}{{cite web|title=Kritik: A Retro Kinski bespricht Evil Born (2012)|url=http://en.ofdb.de/review/232877,557884,Evil-Born|language=German|publisher=Online Film Datenbank|accessdate=May 18, 2015|date=June 19, 2013}} ==Plot== The film begins with a baby, Sebastian, being born. After Sebastian viciously murders the doctors who delivered him, his mother ([[Sara Malakul Lane]]) realizes that there is something seriously wrong with the child (it is later revealed that he is the son of the devil). Over the course of the movie, Sebastian brutally murders many people and tragedy strikes many others. Mahari ([[Jesus Guevara]]) attempts to steal the baby from Sebastian's birth parents many times and eventually does. He steals Sebastian in order to embrace his evil calling. Police officers attempt to kill Mahari but Mahari and his companions use Sebastian to murder them.
