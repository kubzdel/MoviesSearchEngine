The official trailer of the film was released on the October 14, 2011. ==Plot== The story follows Dr. Sonny Blake (McGowan), a radio talk show psychiatrist, when she moves back to her childhood home after her [[alcoholic]] father dies. Once back in her old neighborhood, she meets the local paperboy, a cunning, depraved [[antisocial personality disorder|sociopath]] who targeted her father and now targets her. When the boy starts calling her show and reciting eerie nursery rhymes, an unnerving game of cat-and-mouse begins.
