Eric Miller | distributor = [[Apprehensive Films]] | released = 2006 | runtime = 105 min. | country = United States | language = English | budget = }} '''''Mark of the Damned''''' is a 2006 American [[horror film]] directed by Eric Miller. Filmed in [[black-and-white]], it is an homage to the classic [[El Santo]] movies. The film was shown on ''[[Cinema Insomnia]]'''s 2009 Halloween special. ==Plot summary== Set in an amplified world, telling the story of Diane, a young woman pursued by a brutal force that threatens to consummate the inhuman prophecy she may have been born to fulfill. Alive with tension, this story offers a scenario where human kind must brave the passions of space horror to insure the tomorrow of a sane earth. Will the Zombie Epidemic puke into the lives of every man, woman, and child? Are the intentions of Professor Ramirez in Diane's best interest? Will the Vampire Queen's sarcophagous hunger crush the incalculable strength of the masked hero King Silver?
