The film stars [[Robert Belushi]], Cathy Shim, Evie Thompson, Olivia Ku, [[Lucille Soong]], [[Michael Badalucco]] and Carl Savering. __TOC__ ==Plot== In the town of Golderton, crooked businessman Billy Butler (Michael Badalucco), reopens an abandoned gold mine. It was closed after five miners were trapped in the mine sometime in the 1800s. Centuries later, Butler, a descendant of the mine's owner, reopens it, unaware of the curse that was cast upon the site. A creature, a monstrosity composed of the bodies of five deceased miners, awakens with the desire to kill anyone who takes its gold.
