The film takes place 40 years after the [[Ronald DeFeo Jr.|original murders]] in Amityville, New York. The movie opens in Amityville then after the credits picks up in small town Nebraska where a cursed antique toy monkey from the opening scene possesses a father when it is given to him as a birthday gift, one similar to what he had as a child. The movie pays homage to older Amityville films from the original series, in particular ''[[Amityville: It's About Time|It's About Time]]'', ''[[Amityville: A New Generation|A New Generation]]'', and ''[[Amityville Dollhouse]]''.
