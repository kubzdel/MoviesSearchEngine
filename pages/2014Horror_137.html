Marc Platt Productions | distributor = [[Universal Pictures]] | released = {{film date |2014|10|7}} | runtime = 81 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Mockingbird''''' is a 2014 American [[Found footage (pseudo-documentary)|found footage]] [[horror film]] that was written and directed by [[Bryan Bertino]].{{cite web|last1=Gilchrist|first1=Todd|title='The Strangers' Director Bryan Bertino to Answer 'Mockingbird' Call|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/strangers-bryan-bertino-mockingbird-285949|publisher=The Hollywood Reporter|accessdate=17 December 2014}}{{cite web|last1=Barone|first1=Matt|title=Permanent Midnight: Surprise! The Director of "The Strangers" Finally Has a New Movie Out|url=http://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2014/10/bryan-bertino-the-strangers-mockingbird-review|publisher=Complex|accessdate=17 December 2014}} The film was released to [[video on demand]] on October 7, 2014 and was given a DVD and Blu-ray release on October 21 of the same year. It stars [[Todd Stashwick]], [[Alexandra Lydon]], and Barak Hardley as three people that have been given video cameras with the instructions to film their daily activities for a strange contest.{{cite web|title=You Must Watch ‘Mockingbird’ This Halloween! (Exclusive Images)|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3318657/watch-mockingbird-halloween-exclusive-images/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=17 December 2014}} ==Plot== {{expand section|date=February 2015}} The film follows three groups of people, all of whom have found a video camera on their doorstep and begin filming under the impression that this is the key to winning money from a mysterious competition. Tom ([[Todd Stashwick]]) is an average guy filming the life of his family with his wife Emmy. Beth ([[Alexandra Lydon]]) is a bored and isolated college girl who sees the camera as something to fill her free time. Leonard (Barak Hardley) is a [[mother's boy]] who believes his clown makeup will steal the scene. Each group has been given a label - "The Family" (Tom & Emmy), "The Woman" (Beth), and "The Clown" (Leonard), but they are largely unaware of what is truly going on and are shocked when they receive instructions telling them to keep filming or die. ==Cast== *[[Audrey Marie Anderson]] as Emmy *[[Natalie Alyn Lind]] as Jacob's Friend #4 *[[Benjamin Stockham]] as Jacob's Friend #2 *[[Emily Alyn
