ANNE Terrorizes Digital Platform One More Time|last=Desk|first=TV News|access-date=2018-05-03|language=en}} ==Reception== ===Critical response=== ''Be My Cat: A Film for Anne'' has been widely acclaimed by critics and currently holds an 80% approval rating on [[Rotten Tomatoes]].{{cite web|url=https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/be_my_cat_a_film_for_anne/|title=Be My Cat: A Film for Anne|publisher=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]}} It has been included by [[Dread Central]] in Top 5 Best Horror Movies of 2016, by [[Blumhouse]] in Top 5 New Intelligent Found Footage Films You Need to See and by Audiences Everywhere in Top 50 Best Horror Movies of the 2000s.{{cite web|url=http://www.audienceseverywhere.net/100-horror-2000s-lists/3/ |title=The 100 Best Horror Movies of the 2000s (So Far) - Page 3 of 5 |publisher=Audienceseverywhere.net |date=2015-10-27 |accessdate=2016-04-13}} [[Austin Chronicle]] called it "terrifying"{{cite news|last=Whittaker |first=Richard |url=http://www.austinchronicle.com/daily/screens/2016-12-07/other-worlds-austin-review-be-my-cat-a-film-for-anne/ |title=Other Worlds Austin Review: Be My Cat: A Film for Anne: Bizarre Romanian horror brings insane spark to found footage - Screens |newspaper=[[The Austin Chronicle]] |date=2016-12-07 |accessdate=2017-05-07}} and wrote that it's "a jaw-dropping character study of a murderer, made all the more grueling and transfixing because of Țofei’s performance. Adrian (the character) is as unlikely a killer as [[Norman Bates]], but that’s what makes him so terrifying. This is emotional extreme horror, but you won’t be able to look away. Adrian Țofei’s pointed decision to avoid overt gore, instead concentrating on the interactions, makes the film something akin to a modern ''[[Peeping Tom (film)|Peeping Tom]]''." [[Dread Central]] gave the movie 4 stars and wrote that "Be My Cat takes meta-filmmaking to mind-bending levels and plays out like an arthouse snuff film. Simply calling it unique is an understatement. What we have here is potentially revolutionary and, like the most impactful examples of uncompromising art, potentially dangerous. The line between fact and fiction has never been so terrifyingly and brilliantly blurred."{{cite web|last=Millican |first=Josh |url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/117093/be-my-cat-a-film-for-anne-2015/ |title=Be My Cat: A Film for Anne (2015) |publisher=Dread Central |date=2015-08-07 |accessdate=2016-04-13}} 28DLA gave it 4 stars as well, called it a "disturbing found footage masterpiece"{{cite web|url=http://www.28dayslateranalysis.com/2015/09/be-my-cat-takes-swipe-at-hollywood-in.html |title=Be My Cat Takes a Swipe at Hollywood in this Disturbing Found Footage Masterpiece ~ 28DLA |publisher=28dayslateranalysis.com |date=2015-09-13 |accessdate=2016-04-13}} and added that "the authenticity comes from exceptionally strong performances by an excellent cast. Sonia Teodoriu, Florentina Hariton, Alexandra Stroe and Adrian Țofei are so convincing that, at times, it’s uncomfortable to watch." [[Dean Bertram]] from [[A Night of Horror International Film Festival|A Night of Horror]] described ''Be My Cat: A Film for Anne'' as "the most revolutionary addition to the found footage genre since ''[[The Blair Witch Project]]'' and infinitely more convincing and frightening"{{cite web|url=http://www.anightofhorror.com/2015program/#/be-my-cat-a-film-for-anne/ |title=Be My Cat: A Film for Anne at A Night of Horror |publisher=AnightOfHorror.com |date= |accessdate=2016-05-18}} and [[Lloyd Kaufman]] from [[TromaDance]] wrote that it's "a new masterpiece in horror".{{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/lloyd.kaufman/posts/10153760784181475 |title=See the east coast premiere of Adrian Țofei's "Be My Cat: A Film for Anne" Sat. July 30th at Tromadance in Brooklyn! A new masterpiece in horror!|publisher=Lloyd Kaufman on Facebook |date=2016-07-23 |accessdate=2016-07-25}} [[Little White Lies (magazine)|Little White Lies]] wrote that the movie "is a prime example of meta-cinema, endlessly blurring the line between behind-the-scenes reality and onscreen fantasy, between documentary and fiction, while also repeatedly commenting on the process of its own making. The results are an unnerving study in cinephilia and erotomania, falling somewhere between Rémy Belvaux's ''[[Man Bites Dog (film)|Man Bites Dog]]'' and Patrick Brice’s ''[[Creep (2014 film)|Creep]]''."{{cite web|url=http://lwlies.com/articles/be-my-cat-a-film-for-anne-adrian-tofei-dvd/ |title=Discover the stomach-turning horrors of this Anne Hathaway baiting thriller |website=Lwlies.com |date= |accessdate=2017-05-07}} Audiences Everywhere named it "dangerous and progressive"{{cite web|url=http://www.audienceseverywhere.net/cat-film-anne-dangerous-progressive/ |title=Be My Cat: A Film for Anne is Dangerous and Progressive |publisher=Audienceseverywhere.net |date=2015-10-23 |accessdate=2016-04-13}} and added that "the film is fascinating, challenging and essential for the evolution of found footage horror." Mondo Exploito wrote that it "totally transcends the trappings of the found footage subgenre to create a truly unique experience that will be talked about for years to come."{{cite web|url=http://mondoexploito.com/?p=13311 |title=Be My Cat: A Film for Anne (2015) |publisher=Mondo Exploito |date=2015-12-10 |accessdate=2017-05-07}} Addicted to Horror Movies said it's "the rare original horror film that fans scream for"{{cite web|author= |url=https://addictedtohorrormovies.com/2016/12/01/be-my-cat-a-film-for-anne-is-a-rare-original-horror-movie-review/ |title=‘Be My Cat: A Film for Anne’ is a Rare Original Horror Movie (Review) – Addicted to Horror Movies |website=Addictedtohorrormovies.com |date= |accessdate=2017-05-07}} and Cinehouse called it a "cinematic miracle". {{cite web|last=Harris |first=Sandra |url=https://cinehouseuk.blogspot.ro/2016/11/be-my-cat-film-for-anne-terrifying.html |title=Cinehouse: Be My Cat: A Film For Anne- A Terrifying Romanian Horror Film Reviewed By Sandra Harris |website=Cinehouseuk.blogspot.ro |date=2016-11-25 |accessdate=2017-05-07}} Found Footage Critic rated the movie 9.7 out of 10 and wrote that it's "a near perfect horror/thriller found footage film that sets itself apart in acting, cinematography, plot, tone, execution and production value. Found footage fan or not, this is a film you won’t want to miss! The acting of Adrian Țofei and the three supporting cast, Sonia Teodoriu, Florentina Hariton and Alexandra Stroe, is nothing short of exceptional, resulting in a genuineness that I have yet to encounter in any other found footage film to date.".{{cite web|url=http://foundfootagecritic.com/be-my-cat-a-film-for-anne-2015/review/ |title=Be My Cat: A Film for Anne (2015) Review |publisher=Found Footage Critic |date=2015-09-18 |accessdate=2016-04-13}} Adrian Țofei received praise for his acting performance as well, which has been described by critics as "incredible", "haunting", "complex", "captivating", "shockingly good" and "frighteningly natural".{{cite web|last=Macabre |first=Johnny |url=http://www.theblood-shed.com/revolutionary-dangerous-cat-film-anne-coming-vod-dvd/ |title=“Revolutionary and dangerous” Be My Cat: A Film for Anne coming to VOD and DVD |website=Theblood-shed.com |date=2016-12-10 |accessdate=2017-05-07}} Artsploitation said that he did "the most believable movie psychopath since [[Norman Bates]]." ===Awards and festivals=== {| class="wikitable" |- !
