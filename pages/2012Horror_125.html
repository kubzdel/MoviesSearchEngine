{{refimprove||date=October 2017}} {{italic title}} '''''Lowlife''''' is a 2012 Canadian [[psychological horror]] film directed by Seth Smith. It was first shown at the 2012 [[Fantasia International Film Festival]], then the [[Atlantic Film Festival]] where it won the audience award for best feature.{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/seth-smiths-lowlife-snags-top-372934|title=Seth Smith's 'Lowlife' Snags Top Audience Award|work=The Hollywood Reporter}} In November 2012, the filmmakers organized a DIY Canada-wide theatrical release in 25 cities reaching every province and territory, screening in independent theaters and alternative venues.{{cite web|url=http://www.ffwdweekly.com/screen/film-features/maritime-slime-10022/|title=Maritime slime|work=Fast Forward Weekly}} ==Cast== * Chik White as Asa * Kate Hartigan as Elle * Mitchell Weibe as Damon * John Urich as Dog * KC Spidle as Boss * Paul Hammond as Tommy Gods * William Simmons as The Transient ==Filming== The film was shot in the coastal forests surrounding [[Halifax, Nova Scotia]]. Shooting took place in the spring of 2011. Lowlife was Seth Smith's first feature film.{{cite web|url=http://www.getguerilla.ca/index.php/archive-menu-item-new/115-issue-36/472-guerilla-magazine-seth-smith|title=The curious Mr. Smith|work=Guerilla Magazine}} ==Reception== Vice's Noisey{{cite web|url=http://noisey.vice.com/blog/no-canada-lowlife-is-the-feel-bad-hit-of-2012|title="Lowlife" is the feel-bad hit of 2012|work=NOISEY}} named it "the feel bad hit of 2012" after the release of the trailer.
