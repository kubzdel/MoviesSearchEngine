English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Chain Mail''''' is a 2015 [[Philippines|Filipino]] [[mystery film|mystery]] [[horror film]] starring [[Meg Imperial]], [[Shy Carlos]] and [[Nadine Lustre]]. It was released on July 22, 2015 by [[VIVA Films|Viva Films]].{{cite news|url=http://www.philstar.com/psn-showbiz/2015/07/17/1477854/nadine-meg-shy-mag-iikot-sa-mga-sinehan|title=Nadine, Meg, at Shy mag-iikot sa mga sinehan|date=July 17, 2015|work=philstar.com|publisher=Pilipino Star Ngayon|accessdate=November 19, 2015|language=Filipino}} It was directed and written by Adolfo Alix Jr. , also written by Jerry Gracio and Agnes de Guzman. The film earned negative reviews from film critics. ==Synopsis== The movie follows a group of people who receive a [[chain letter]]. Some of them forward it along as it requested, but some just ignore it. Subsequent events suggest the letter is cursed, and results in people dying brutal and bloody deaths.
