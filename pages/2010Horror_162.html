New Image Entertainment | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2010|6|1}} | runtime = 75 minutes | country = Canada | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Star Vehicle''''' (re-titled in North America as '''''Bleading Lady''''') is a 2010 [[horror film]] directed by [[Ryan Nicholson]] and starring Dan Ellis, Sindy Faraguna, Nathan Durec and Nick Windebank.[https://www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=100618561434 ''Star Vehicle'' – The Movie: Facebook] ==Premise== {{expand section|date=April 2015}} The film tells the tale of frustrated Teamster driver Don Cardini, abused by spoiled stars and arrogant students, who becomes a ticking time-bomb of revenge.
