It debuted at 14 April 2006. ==Synopsis== The London-based Rai family consists of Suraj, his wife, Radha, and two children, a boy and a girl. Suraj's younger brother Raj comes to stay with them, and he slowly adapts the life in the city. Raj also has a secret, he can see ghosts and spirits. Soon enough, a localite finds out about this. A dying man calls Raj over to see whether he can spot his death having any connections with spirits. Whilst staying at his house, Raj falls in love with a youngster named Sapna. Soon, Raj realizes that not only in his imagination, but also in his own house, there is a ghost lurking to murder his family and seek vengeance. However, Raj has no idea why any ghost would want revenge from his family.
