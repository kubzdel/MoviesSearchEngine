Peter Schrijvers | editing = Bob Embregts | studio = Asura Pictures | distributor = Arts Home Entertainment | released = {{film date|2005|06|15|Amsterdam Fantastic Film Festival}} | runtime = 75 minutes | country = Netherlands | language = Dutch | budget = | gross = }} '''''Woensdag''''' is a 2005 Dutch [[horror film]] written and directed by Bob Embregts and Jean-Paul Arends. {{cite web|url = http://www.schokkendnieuws.nl/nieuws/3041-nederhorror-galore|title = Nedderhorror galore!|last = Voogd|first = Door Barend de|publisher = Schokkend Nieuws|date = 4 June 2013|website = schokkendnieuws.nl|accessdate = 10 November 2016}} == Plot == {{more plot|date=April 2017}} Eight people are chosen to compete on ''Camp Slasher'', a new horror-themed [[game show]] in which the contestants must complete challenges while trying to avoid a masked killer, one who is based on a local [[urban legend]]. Unbeknownst to the cast and crew of the show, an actual homicidal maniac resides in the woods where they have decided to shoot, and after massacring the program's behind-the-scenes personnel, he sets his sights on the competitors. == Cast == * Marisa Goedhart as Vera de Jong * Ronald van den Burg as Erik Faber * William van der Voort as Matthijs van Weert * Sjanett de Geus as Leanne Beekveld * Cêfas Hoofs as Tom Cranen * Reine Rek as Marieke Ouwerling * Nadia Huffmeijer as Jeniffer Moonen * Corne Heijboer as Kevin van Noordhof * Laura Molenaar as Reizend Meisje * Frans van der Meer as Presentatie * Debby de Brouwer as Presentatie * Marq van Broekhoven as Televisie Operator * [[Ruud Jolie]] as Internet Operator * Toine Schuurman as Assistant Techniek * Theunis van den Broek as Nepmoordenaar #1 * Jeroen van Gelderblom as Nepmoordenaar #2 * Fred Sibelink as Producent Camp Slasher * Patrice van der Linden as Moordenaar == Reception == [[Bloody Disgusting]] praised the gore, criticized the screenplay and acting, and gave the film a final score of 3/5, concluding, "''Woensdag'' may not be a great movie but it sure is entertaining and it being a relatively short movie (75 minutes) helps keep the pace and our attention up". {{cite web|url = http://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/109465/woensdag-wednesday-5/|title = ''Woensdag (Wednesday)''|publisher = [[Archive.is]]|date = 24 June 2005|website = bloody-disgusting.com|accessdate = 26 April 2017|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://archive.is/LNUId|archivedate = 19 October 2014}} The acting, dialogue, story, and production values were all panned by Neerlands Filmdoek, which gave ''Woensdag'' a grade of 1½ while also conceding that, "Nevertheless, there is still something to enjoy for lovers who have no objection to these shortcomings; the film is never boring and has enough amusing horror moments, fitted with the necessary gore". {{cite web|url = http://www.nlfilmdoek.nl/dvd-blu-ray/dvd/woensdag-dvd-recensie/|title = ''Woensdag'' - DVD Review|last = Peters|first = Kasper|publisher = Neerlands Filmdoek|date = 12 October 2007|website = nlfilmdoek.nl|accessdate = 19 October 2014}} Top of the Flops gave ''Woensdag'' a similar commendation, noting that despite the drawbacks inherent in it being a "semi amateurish production" the film was still enjoyable as something "designed by enthusiasts for enthusiasts". {{cite web|url = http://www.totf.nl/movies/woensdag/|title = ''Woensdag''|publisher = Top of the Flops|date = 23 July 2007|website = totf.nl|accessdate = 11 November 2016}} Horror Neerlands, which gave the film a final grade of 2/5, panned the personality-less characters, the seemingly nonexistent script, and the slow start, but went on to admit that ''Woensdag'' did succeed at being "quite entertaining" and "still pretty fun" in spite of its flaws. {{cite web|url = http://www.horror.nl/recensies/woensdag/|title = ''Woensdag''|publisher = Horror Neerlands|date = 28 April 2011|website = horror.nl|accessdate = 26 April 2017}} The same score was awarded by Slasherpool, which summed ''Woensdag'' up with, "Disappointing [[Slasher film|slasher flick]] from the [[Netherlands]] which won't likely impress anyone who lives outside [[Holland]]. It's a very dull slasher and the concept has been done to death in several, much better flicks. It doesn't help that it's almost impossible to see what was going on for most of the movie either". {{cite web|url = http://slasherp.nexcess.net/htm/reviews/woensdag.htm|title = ''Woensdag''|last = Fred|first = Anthro|publisher = [[Internet Archive]]|website = slasherpool.starbase.se|accessdate = 26 April 2017|deadurl = yes|archiveurl = https://web.archive.org/web/20080919090419/http://slasherp.nexcess.net:80/htm/reviews/woensdag.htm|archivedate = 19 September 2008}} == References == {{Reflist}} == External links == * {{IMDB title|0469228|Woensdag}} * [http://www.totf.nl/jean-paul-arends-over-woensdag/ Interview] with Jean-Paul Arends at Top of the Flops [[Category:2005 films]] [[Category:2005 horror films]] [[Category:2000s independent films]] [[Category:Directorial debut films]] [[Category:Dutch films]] [[Category:Dutch horror films]] [[Category:Dutch independent films]] [[Category:Dutch-language films]] [[Category:Dutch slasher films]] [[Category:Films about television]] [[Category:Films set in 2004]] [[Category:Films set in forests]] [[Category:Films set in the Netherlands]] [[Category:Films shot in Amsterdam]] [[Category:Films shot in the Netherlands]] [[Category:Mass murder in films]] [[Category:Reality television series parodies]]
