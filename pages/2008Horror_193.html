Shooting started in November 2007, and ended in February 2008. Post-production was completed in June 2008. The film stars [[Jonathan Torrens]], [[Sarah E. Dunsworth]], [[Lex Gigeroff]], [[Molly Dunsworth]], [[Glenn Matthews (actor)|Glenn Matthews]], and [[Kristin Slaney]]. The theme song to the film ''[[Cannibal Holocaust]]'' is played during the opening title. ==Synopsis== ''Treevenge'' depicts [[Christmas]] from the perspective of [[sentience|sentient]] [[Christmas tree]]s. After being hacked down and shipped to homes, they are subject to "humiliation" by humans, who decorated them and make them stand in their living rooms.
