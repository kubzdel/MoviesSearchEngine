It is directed by Justin Thomas Ostensen, and stars Edward Furlong, Michael Berryman and Kristin Booth. ==Plot== {{expand section|date=October 2014}} A screenwriter suffering from writer's block locks himself in a meat locker. There, he hopes to come up with the perfect story for a [[horror film]] about a man trapped in a meat locker. He imagines a story in which a man is menaced by an evil butcher. However, reality and fiction begin to merge, and his imaginary butcher turns into a real threat. ==Cast== *[[Edward Furlong]] as Jack/Frank *[[Michael Berryman]] as Gunner *[[Kristin Booth]] as Penny *Dee Hannah as Mrs. Hatcher *[[Michael Eisner]] as Monty *Sadie Madu ==Production== Writer Signe Olynyk developed writer's block whilst writing the film. In order to overcome it, she locked herself in a meat locker for five days to develop the story.{{cite web|url=http://belowzeromovie.com/read-the-screenplay|title=Read the Screenplay|work=belowzeromovie.com|accessdate=2013-04-19}} In February 2010, it was revealed that director Justin Thomas Ostensen would start filming in March 2010 in Canada. Then in March, ''[[The Hills Have Eyes (1977 film)|The Hills Have Eyes]]'' actor [[Michael Berryman]] was announced to appear in the film as Gunnar.{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/19328/michael-berryman-deep-frozen-in-below-zero-3d/|title=Michael Berryman Deep Frozen in ‘Below Zero 3D’!|work=Bloody Digusting|last=Miska|first=Brad|date=2010-03-03|accessdate=2013-04-19}} [[Edward Furlong]] was also announced in April 2012.{{cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/37228/go-below-zero-3d-with-edward-furlong|title=Go Below Zero in 3D with Edward Furlong|work=[[Dread Central]]|last=Barton|first=Steve|date=2012-04-28|accessdate=2013-04-19}} In the same month [[Kristin Booth]], [[Michael Eisner]], Sadie Madu and Dee Hannah were also added.{{cite web|url=http://www.darkhorizons.com/news/17025|title=Edward Furlong Slips 'Below Zero'|work=Dark Horizons|last=Franklin|first=Garth|date=2010-04-29|accessdate=2013-04-19}} Filming took place in [[Alberta, Canada]].
