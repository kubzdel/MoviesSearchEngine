{{multiple issues| {{COI|date=March 2013}} {{notability|Films|date=March 2013}}}} '''''Gallery of Fear''''' is a 2013 four-part horror anthology, directed by [[Alan Rowe Kelly]] and Anthony G. Summer. Kelly also wrote the screenplay for the film based on a story by Doug Smith. The film stars [[Debbie Rochon]], [[Raine Brown]], and Susan Adriensen.{{cite web|url=http://anythinghorror.com/2012/02/11/gallery-of-fear-2010/ |title=Gallery of Fear (2010) | AnythingHorror Central |publisher=Anythinghorror.com |date= |accessdate=2013-10-08}} The four segments of the anthology are titled "Critics Choice," "By Her Hand, She Draws You Down," "Down the Drain," and "A Far Cry from Home." {{cite web|url=http://anythinghorror.com/2013/06/05/gallery-of-fear-finally-lands-distribution-a-release-date/ |title=Gallery of Fear Finally Lands Distribution & a Release Date | AnythingHorror Central |publisher=Anythinghorror.com |date=2013-06-12 |accessdate=2013-10-08}} The film was distributed by Southpaw Pictures and Tiny Core Pictures.{{cite web |url=http://www.killingboxx.com/reviews/movies/10817-gallery-of-fear |title=Gallery Of Fear |publisher=KillingBoxx |date=2012-06-14 |accessdate=2013-10-08 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130606091816/http://www.killingboxx.com/reviews/movies/10817-gallery-of-fear |archivedate=2013-06-06 |df= }} ==References== {{Reflist}} ==External links== * {{IMDb title|1772871|Gallery of Fear}} [[Category:2013 horror films]] [[Category:2013 films]] {{2010s-horror-film-stub}}
