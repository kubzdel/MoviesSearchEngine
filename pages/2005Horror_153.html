Vanessa Horneff | writer = [[Ti West]] | producer = [[Larry Fessenden]] | music = Jeff Grace | cinematography = Eric Robbins | editing = Ti West | released = {{Film date|2005}} | runtime = 80 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = }} '''''The Roost''''' is a 2005 American [[horror film]] written and directed by [[Ti West]]. == Synopsis == Following a near-death car accident, four friends on their way to a Halloween wedding venture to a secluded farm for help. There they disturb an ancient evil with ghastly plans in store for them. == Production == The Roost was photographed in Super 16mm on location in Wilmington, Delaware, and Kennett Square, Pennsylvania, in October 2003. The film features the original song "I Raise Hell" by [[Mr. Move]]. == Releases and critical reception == The Roost premiered at the South by Southwest Film Festival in March, 2005. It was screened at the Woodstock Film Festival, Sitges Film Festival, Fright Fest, The Fantasia Film Festival, Los Angeles Film Festival. The Roost was released to DVD in the United Kingdom on August 21, 2006. The Roost was released to DVD in the United States on October 3, 2006. It was nominated for Best Limited Release Film at the Fangoria Chainsaw Awards.
