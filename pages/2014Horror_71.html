Breakthrough Entertainment | distributor = Breakthrough Entertainment | released = {{film date |2014|8|2|Fantasia Film Festival}} | runtime = 88 minutes | country = Canada | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Drownsman''''' is a 2014 Canadian horror/fantasy thriller that was directed by [[Chad Archibald]], who co-wrote the script with Cody Calahan.{{cite web|last1=Turek|first1=Ryan|title=A New Trailer for The Drownsman Surfaces|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/359901-the-drownsman-brand-new-trailer-fantasia/|publisher=Shock Till You Drop|accessdate=18 October 2014}} The film had its world premiere on 2 August 2014 at the [[Fantasia Film Festival]] and stars Michelle Mylett as a [[hydrophobic]] young woman that finds that the cure might be worse than the fear itself.{{cite web|last1=Kay|first1=Jeremy|title=Cannes: Breakthrough adds Drownsman|url=http://www.screendaily.com/festivals/cannes/cannes-breakthrough-adds-drownsman/5071803.article|publisher=Screen Daily|accessdate=18 October 2014}} Filming took place in [[Guelph, Ontario]] during winter of 2013.{{cite web|last1=Barton|first1=Steve|title=The Drownsman Begins Filming in Canada|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/49817/the-drownsman-begins-filming-in-canada/|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=18 October 2014}} ==Synopsis== A near-death experience in a lake leaves Madison (Michelle Mylett) with extreme hydrophobia. It's grown so bad that she's unable to attend her best friend Hannah's (Caroline Korycki) wedding because of the rain, as Madison believes that a sinister figure known as the Drownsman (Ry Barrett) will pull her into his world and kill her. In their attempt to help her, Madison's friends try to hold a seance to make contact with this being and find that Madison is being haunted by Sebastian Donner (also played by Barrett), a serial killer that loved to listen to his victims' hearts beat as they slowly died.
