Billed as Singapore's first Hollywood supernatural feature, the film tells the story of an American journalist who travels to Singapore to investigate the mysterious suicide of her sister, and delves into one of Tong's favourite horror subgenres: exorcism.{{cite web|first1=Zara|last1=Zhuang|title=Kelvin Tong lights joss sticks, explains horror movie to spirits|url=http://www.todayonline.com/entertainment/movies/kelvin-tong-lights-joss-sticks-explains-horror-movie-spirits|website=[[Today (Singapore newspaper)|Today]]|accessdate=2016-05-14|date=10 May 2016}}{{cite web|first1=Marcus|last1=Goh|title=Review: ''The Faith of Anna Waters'' has corny demons|url=https://sg.movies.yahoo.com/review-the-faith-of-anna-waters-has-corny-061626948.html|website=[[Yahoo]]|accessdate=2016-05-14|date=10 May 2016}}{{cite web|first1=Genevieve Sarah|last1=Loh|title=Filmmaker Kelvin Tong in talks for second American horror co-production|url=http://www.channelnewsasia.com/news/singapore/filmmaker-kelvin-tong-in/2783150.html|website=[[Channel NewsAsia]]|accessdate=2016-05-14|date=13 May 2016}} The film was released on 6 May 2016, in the USA under the title '''''The Offering'''''. ==Plot== {{more plot|date=March 2017}} American journalist Jamie Waters ([[Elizabeth Rice]]) travels to Singapore to investigate the mysterious suicide of her sister, Anna.
