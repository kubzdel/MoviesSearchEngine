[[Akiko Kobayashi (voice actor)|Akiko Kobayashi]] | distributor = [[YamatoWorks]] | released = {{Film date|2005|03|df=y}} | runtime = 25 minutes | language = Japanese | budget = }} {{nihongo|'''''Kakurenbo: Hide & Seek'''''|カクレンボ|Kakurenbo|lit. "Hide and Seek"}} is a [[Japan]]ese [[Cel-shaded animation|cel-shaded]] [[anime]] [[short film]] written and directed by [[Shuhei Morita]]. The film entails a game of {{nihongo|"Otokoyo"|オトコヨ||lit. Man Hunt}}, a version of [[hide and seek]] played by children, wearing fox masks, near the ruins of an abandoned old [[Kowloon Walled City|Kowloon-inspired]] city. The children who play this disappear, believed to be [[Kamikakushi|spirited away]] by demons. ''Kakurenbo'' follows Hikora, a boy who joins the game with hopes of finding his missing sister, Sorincha. The storyline is built on the idea that Tokyo is losing its ''natural'' aesthetic, which includes child's games such as hide and seek in order for industrial progress to ensue i.e. lighting the city of Tokyo costing innocence of childhood games. == Characters == ;{{nihongo|Hikora|ヒコラ}} :{{anime voices|[[Junko Takeuchi]]|[[Michael Sinterniklaas]]}} :The young boy who plays the game to find his missing sister. ;{{nihongo|Sorincha|ソリンチャ}} :{{anime voices|[[Masami Suzuki]]|[[Veronica Taylor]]}} :Hikora's sister who went missing when she played the game with the other children. ;{{nihongo|Yaimao|ヤイマオ}} :{{anime voices|[[Makoto Ueki]]|[[Dan Green (voice actor)|Dan Green]]}} :Hikora's best friend who plays the game to lend a hand in finding Sorincha. ;{{nihongo|Noshiga|ノシガ}} :{{anime voices|[[Ryo Naito]]|[[Sean Schemmel]]}} :The leader of his little gang who claims is not afraid of demons. ;{{nihongo|Tachiji|タチジ}} :{{anime voices|[[Mika Ishibashi]]|[[Tom Wayland]]}} :One of the members in Noshiga's gang.
