The film had a predominantly negative reception. ==Synopsis== An eager and nameless new priest ([[Stuart Brennan]]) is sent to a small village to serve as their resident priest, but he is unaware that he is the basis of a bet between the [[God|Almighty]] ([[Giovanni Lombardo Radice]]) and the [[Satan|Withstander]] ([[Rutger Hauer]] ), with his soul as the prize. This bet is put into action when he is turned into a vampire. He then has to find a way to not only battle his craving for blood, but also rid the area of crime and save the life of the prostitute Tracy ([[Emily Booth]]). ==Cast== *[[Stuart Brennan]] as The Reverend *[[Tamer Hassan]] as Harold Hicks *[[Rutger Hauer]] as Withstander *[[Doug Bradley]] as Reverend Andrews *[[Giovanni Lombardo Radice]] as Almighty *[[Emily Booth]] as Tracy *[[Simon Phillips (actor)|Simon Phillips]] as Detective *Marcia Do Vales as Girl *[[Shane Richie]] as Prince *Mads Koudal as Viking *Edmund Kingsley as Thug *[[Helen Griffin]] as Mrs. Jenkins *[[Dominic Burns]] as Big Bazza *Billy Rumbol as Ryan *Richie Woodhall as Inspector Rodin ==Reception== Critical reception for ''The Reverend'' has been predominantly negative.{{cite web|title=The Reverend|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/the_reverend_2011/|publisher=Rotten Tomatoes|accessdate=13 February 2014}} Much of the criticism centered around the film's plot, as most reviewers felt that the movie did not live up to its full potential.{{cite web|last=Getz|first=Rob|title=Film Review: The Reverend (2011)|url=http://horrornews.net/81262/film-review-reverend-2011/|publisher=Horrornews.net|accessdate=13 February 2014}}{{cite web|last=Lee|first=Matthew|title=Grimm Up North 2011: The Reverend review|url=http://twitchfilm.com/2011/10/grimm-up-north-2011-the-reverend-review.html|publisher=Twitch Film|accessdate=13 February 2014}} ''[[Twitch Film]]'' heavily panned the film and commented that "''The Reverend'' is that rare beast, in other words, a terrible film that thinks it's onto a winner; if you want to see how terrible, then go right ahead, but everyone else should stay well away."
