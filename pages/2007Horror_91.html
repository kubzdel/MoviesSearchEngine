[[Italian language|Italian]] }} '''''The Etruscan Mask''''' is a [[supernatural]] [[horror movie]] written and directed by [[Ted Nicolaou]]. Shot in [[Italy]] in 2006, the [[film]] was released in 2007. ==Plot== {{expand section|date=September 2014}} In the unique city of [[Siena]], [[Italy]], five foreign university students stumble across an ancient Etruscan mask. After a series of unexplainable events, it becomes clear that the mask must be destroyed. However, one of the students has already succumbed to its powers and in doing so has released an ancient [[demon]] which has no intention of being destroyed. ==Cast== {{expand section|date=September 2014}} ==Production== {{expand section|date=September 2014}} The film was shot in [[Siena]] and [[Turin]]. ==Reception== {{multiple issues|section=yes| {{unreferenced section|date=September 2014}} {{expand section|date=September 2014}} }} The movie won the Best Film Prize at the 2007 edition of the International Horror And Fantasy Film Festival in [[Estepona]]. ==External links== * [http://www.bankside-films.com/etruscan%20mask.html Official Website] * [http://www.fangoria.com/news_article.php?id=4208/ Fangoria article] * [http://www.dreadcentral.com/index.php?name=News&file=article&sid=2071/ Dread Central article] * [http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1031240/ The Etruscan Mask] at the [[Internet Movie Database]] {{DEFAULTSORT:Etruscan Mask, The}} [[Category:2007 horror films]] [[Category:Supernatural horror films]] [[Category:2007 films]] [[Category:Films directed by Ted Nicolaou]] {{2000s-horror-film-stub}}
