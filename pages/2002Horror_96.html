(executive producer) | music = [[Guy Harrington]] | editing = | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2002|07|13|U.S.}} | runtime = 87 min. | country = United States | language = English | budget = }} '''''Vampire Clan''''' is a 2002 American [[Drama film|drama]]/[[Horror film|horror]] [[film]], directed by [[John Webb (film director)|John Webb]]. It received its premiere screening at the 2002 Dances With Films Festival.{{cite web|work=[[The New York Times]]|title=Vampire Clan|url=https://www.nytimes.com/movies/movie/272350/Vampire-Clan/overview}} ==Plot== Based on the horrific true story of the 1996 Vampire Killings in Florida,{{cite web|work=[[Crime Library]]|title=The Vampire Killers|url=http://www.crimelibrary.com/serial_killers/weird/vampires/8.html}} the film follows the police investigation of five Goth teenagers who claimed to be real-life vampires. They drank each other's blood and embraced the occult. But they were also ordinary, middle-class kids looking for an outlet for their angst and morbid curiosity. Somewhere along their road trip to New Orleans, their fantasy life became all too real. Now the police have two savagely beaten corpses on their hands—parents of the teenaged vampires. What really happened?
