On a road trip, a group of actors confronts Elvis impersonators, religious extremists, and zombies. == Plot == After they each fail their audition for Jesus in a play, a Jew, a militant African American, a feminist, and a bisexual hippie go on a road trip. On the way, they confront hostile Elvis impersonators and end up in a fight. Fleeing the law, they end up in an intolerant religious community that holds the secret of the apocalypse; a botched magical spell reveals it to be a zombie apocalypse. Zombies kill everyone but Jessie, who lives among them and decides to study their culture. == Cast == * Tim Gouran as Sky * Angie Louise as Jessie * Jeff Gilbert as Rahim * Todd Licea as Joshua * Keith Winsted as Preacher John * Jason Collins as Ralph Peed * Joseph Franklin as Mo Jack == Release == ''Gory Gory Hallelujah'' had a limited release in January 2005 and made $12,604.
