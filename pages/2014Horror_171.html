It stars Sarah Booth and Kimberly-Sue Murray as two women who seek revenge against their former sorority. == Premise == Six sorority sisters enter a haunted house attraction, only to discover that there are two former members who seek revenge on them for past misdeeds. == Cast == * Sarah Booth as Corey Peters * Kimberly-Sue Murray as Elaina Forrester * [[Katherine Barrell]] as Jaqueline Gill * Jennifer Miller as Lisa Jenner * Teagan Vincze as Shelby Hickman * Emily Alatalo as Katrina Larson * Dani Barker as Emily Lewis * Ivana Stojanovic as Caitlin Bowden == Production == Pre-production was announced in July 2013. Filming took place in [[Windsor, Ontario]], in August 2013.{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3252181/exclusive-first-look-at-the-horrors-within-the-scarehouse/|title=Exclusive First Look At the Horrors Within 'The Scarehouse'!|last=Miska|first=Brad|work=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|date=2013-09-05|accessdate=2015-03-28}}{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3246034/canadian-scarehouse-begins-pre-production/|title=Canadian 'Scarehouse' Begins Pre-Production|last=Miska|first=Brad|work=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|date=2013-07-29|accessdate=2015-03-28}} Shooting ended after 22 days, and it entered post-production in September 2013.{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3254972/the-scarehouse-described-as-saw-meets-mean-girls-enters-post/|title='The Scarehouse' – Described As 'Saw' Meets 'Mean Girls' – Enters Post|last=Miska|first=Brad|work=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|date=2013-09-19|accessdate=2015-03-28}} == Release == ''The Scarehouse'' premiered in Windsor on 5 October 2014.
