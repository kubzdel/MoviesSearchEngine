Polish | budget = | gross = }} '''''Haunted Poland''''' ({{lang-pl|Nawiedzona Polska}}) is a 2011 [[horror film]] in the [[Found footage (pseudo-documentary)|found footage]] genre, pieced together from amateur footage. The film was produced by ''Maso & Co Productions''. Told cinéma vérité-style, ''Haunted Poland'' depicts the contents of recorded tape filmed by a couple ''Ewelyn'' played by Ewelina Lukaszewska, and ''Pau'' played by [[Pau Masó]] who visited Poland to meet and visit family. However, our duo soon find themselves disturbed by all manner of strange phenomena upon visiting the girl's hometown where she once played with a Ouija board.{{cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/46041/ready-visit-haunted-poland |title=Ready to Visit Haunted Poland? |date=July 28, 2011 |publisher=DreadCentral}} The film premiered at the AIFF on November 20, 2011. A sequel, [[Haunted Poland: The Origins]] ({{lang-pl|Nawiedzona Polska 2}}), has been announced.{{cite web|url=http://horrornews.net/45597/haunted-poland-scares-up-a-release-and-follow-up/|title=‘Haunted Poland’ scares up a release and follow-up|date=December 22, 2011 |publisher=Horrornews.net}} ==Plot== In 2011, a couple living in America, Ewelina and Pau, travel to Poland to meet the girl's family. As the film goes by, strange phenomena occurs, but her reluctant "non-believing" boyfriend, just pokes fun at her instead of supporting her, while she is actually having a hard time. ==Cast== * [[Ewelina Lukaszewska]] * [[Pau Masó]] as Pau * Irene González as Irene * Dominik Lukszewski as Ewelyn's brother ==Production== Filming began in July 2011 and lasted seven days.{{cite web|url=http://www.pomorska.pl/apps/pbcs.dll/article?AID=/20110721/REGION/268187293 |title=Horror na Kujawach!
