The film is directed by [[Tod Williams (filmmaker)|Tod Williams]], produced by [[John Cusack]], with a screenplay by King and Adam Alleca. The film stars Cusack, [[Samuel L. Jackson]], and [[Isabelle Fuhrman]]. The film was released on June 10, 2016 to [[video on demand]], prior to a [[limited release]] scheduled for July 8, 2016.{{cite web|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/movie/3388853/cell-trailer-rings-zombie-esque/|title=The ‘CELL’ Trailer Rings in a Zombie-esque Apocalypse!|work=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|first=Brad|last=Miska|date=April 26, 2016|accessdate=April 26, 2016}} ''Cell'' is the second film adaptation of a King story to co-star Cusack and Jackson, after ''[[1408 (film)|1408]]'' (2007). The film was panned by critics upon its release; most criticized the film's acting and plot. == Synopsis == When an evil [[electronics|electronic]] signal is broadcast across mobile networks worldwide, cell phone users are instantly and dangerously re-programmed into rabid killers.
