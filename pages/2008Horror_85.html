{{refimprove|date=June 2010}} '''H3 – Halloween Horror Hostel''' is a [[German language|German]] [[Horror film|horror]]-[[Parody film|parod]]y, produced by [[ProSieben Funny Movie]]. The film was directed by [[Michael Karenist]] and written by [[Stefan Barth]]. == Plot == During a cooking show, Tim Mälzer got repeated phone calls from a mysterious person, who asks for popcorn. After several calls Mälzer responds to the person, that threatened him to kill him, like he did it with the camera man. Mälzer looked for the camera man, but he had disappeared. Then the microwave rings and Mälzer opened it and find the camera man's head inside. After that a [[Jigsaw (Saw franchise)|Jigsaw puppet]] appeared driving a small red car and asked again enraged for the popcorn. In the same time the four friends Nico, Janine, Acki and Martin driving through a forest. == Parodies == The movie contains many references and parodies of many known horror-thriller movies:[[:de:H3 – Halloween Horror Hostel#Parodien]] *''[[Halloween (franchise)|Halloween]]'' - The main theme can be heard, when the four friends walking through the forest. *[[Friday the 13th (franchise)|Friday the 13th]] *[[Hostel]] *[[Dead End (2003 film)|Dead End]] *''[[Saw (franchise)|Saw]]'' - The [[Jigsaw (Saw franchise)|Jigsaw]] puppet. *[[The Ring (2002 film)|The Ring]] *[[Ju-on (film)|Ju-on]] *[[I Know What You Did Last Summer]] *[[Scream (1996 film)|Scream]] *[[28 Weeks Later]] *''[[House of Wax (2005 film)|House of Wax]]'' - Letter animation at the beginning. *''[[The Evil Dead]]'' - Camera drives. *[[Dawn of the Dead (1978 film)|Dawn of the Dead]] *[[The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (2003 film)|The Texas Chainsaw Massacre]] *[[Freddy vs. Jason]] *[[From Dusk till Dawn]] *''[[Donnie Darko]]'' - The smoking lookalike Donnie Darko rabbit scene *''[[E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial]]'' - Jigsaw puppet flies with his red car in front of shining moon.https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X80Gd80X40s&feature=relatedhttps://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F76U4oHFcC0 *[[Harry Potter (film series)|Harry Potter]] *''[[Michael Jackson's Thriller (music video)|Michael Jackson's Thriller]]'' - The dance routine of the zombies, with the music. == Cast == *Alfonso Losa as Nico *[[Jennifer Ulrich]] as Janin *[[Axel Stein]] as Acki *Hendrik von Bültzingslöwen as Martin *[[Christian Tramitz]] as Harry *[[Mike Krüger]] as Michael Meier *Ivonne Schönherr as Ficky *Anni Wendler as Paris *[[Mirjam Weichselbraun]] as Sidney *Holger C.
