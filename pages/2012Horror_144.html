'''''Project: S.E.R.A.''''' is a 2012 [[Zombie (fictional)|zombie]] horror [[short film]] written and directed by Ben Howdeshell and starring [[Julia Voth]]. Voth previously portrayed [[Jill Valentine]] in the 2002 video game ''[[Resident Evil (2002 video game)|Resident Evil]]'',[http://www.comingsoon.net/news/movienews.php?id=87016 Exclusive: First Look at Project: S.E.R.A.], ComingSoon.net, February 13, 2012Kofi Outlaw, [http://screenrant.com/project-sera-short-film-julia-voth-kofi-155348/ Short Film ‘Project S.E.R.A.’: ’28 Days Later’ Meets ‘Resident Evil’], Screen Rant, 2012 and Project: S.E.R.A. was said to be inspired by the ''Resident Evil'' series.{{cite web|url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2016/01/05/wrap-your-head-around-this-jill-valentine-cosplay-from-jill-valentine-actress |archiveurl=https://archive.is/20170227115905/http://au.ign.com/articles/2016/01/05/wrap-your-head-around-this-jill-valentine-cosplay-from-jill-valentine-actress |archivedate=27 February 2017 |title=Wrap Your Head Around This Jill Valentine Cosplay From Jill Valentine Actress |publisher=IGN |last=Macy |first=Seth G. |date=January 5, 2016 |accessdate=September 11, 2016 |deadurl=yes |df= }} The film was later turned by [[IGN]] into a [[web series]] directed by Howdeshell and written by Bill Kirchen and Nathan Miller, starring Voth and [[Derek Theler]], produced by Shawn Wallace, and executive produced by Bernard Ho and Grant Thompson, and Travis Milloy.
