Black Hat Productions | distributor = [[Echo Bridge Home Entertainment]] | released = {{Film date|2006}} | runtime = 93 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = $70,000 (estimated) | gross = }} '''Bloody Mary''' is a 2006 [[thriller film|thriller]]-[[horror film]] written and directed by Richard Valentine. ==Plot== {{expand section|date=September 2014}} The film begins with a group of nurses at a [[psychiatric hospital]] daring a fellow nurse, Nicole (Jessica Von), to go into the hospital's basement for a game of [[Bloody Mary (folklore)|Bloody Mary]]. Playing what the others call "The Mirror Game", she releases the vengeful spirit and is snatched away. When Nicole is reported missing, her writer/reporter sister Natalie (Kim Tyler) decides to investigate on her own. As the film progresses, more people are killed by the spirit of Bloody Mary (Richard Valentine) in gruesome ways while Natalie uncovers clues about the truth behind her sister's disappearance and Mary herself. Near the end, almost all of the main characters are dead except for Natalie, who discovers that Bloody Mary is actually her mother. ==Cast== * Kim Tyler ... Natalie * [[Matthew Borlenghi]] ...
