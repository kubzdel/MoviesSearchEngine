{{Other uses|Found footage (disambiguation){{!}}Found footage}} {{Infobox film | name = Found Footage 3D | image = Found Footage 3D.jpg | alt = | caption = Film poster | director = [[Steven DeGennaro]] | producer = {{unbulleted list|Steven DeGennaro|Charles Mulford|[[Kim Henkel]]}} | writer = Steven DeGennaro | starring = {{unbulleted list|Carter Roy|Alena von Stroheim|Chris O'Brien|Tom Saporito|Scott Allen Perry|Jessica Perrin|Scott Weinberg}} | music = | cinematography = Drew Daniels | editing = Pete Barnstrom | studio = FF3D | distributor = | released = | runtime =108 min | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Found Footage 3D''''' is a 2016 American [[found footage (pseudo-documentary)|found footage]] [[horror film]]. It is the debut feature film of writer/director Steven DeGennaro, and is being [[Film producer|produced]] by ''[[The Texas Chain Saw Massacre|Texas Chainsaw Massacre]]'' co-creator [[Kim Henkel]].{{cite web|last=Dreher|first=Dan|title=FOUND FOOTAGE 3D Moves Into Production|url=http://horrornews.net/81052/found-footage-3d-moves-production/|publisher=HorrorNews.net|accessdate=18 May 2014}} It is the first found footage horror movie shot natively in [[3D film|3D]]. Filming began on May 26, 2014 in [[Gonzales, Texas]], starring Carter Roy, Alena von Stroheim, Chris O'Brien, Tom Saporito, Scott Allen Perry, Jessica Perrin, and Scott Weinberg,{{cite web|title=Found Footage 3D Starts Filming; Cast Fills Out|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/76275/found-footage-3d-starts-filming-cast-fills-out#axzz34mvF2CCN|publisher=[[Dread Central]]|accessdate=29 May 2014}} and wrapped mid-June 2014. The film's trailer debuted on Entertainment Weekly August 10, 2016.{{Cite web|url=http://www.ew.com/article/2016/08/10/found-footage-3d-trailer|title=Horror movie 'Found Footage 3D' has a killer NSFW trailer -- exclusive|access-date=2016-08-11}} The film premiered at Bruce Campbell's Horror Film Fest on August 20, 2016,{{Cite web|url=http://wizardworld.com/foundfootage3d.html?all=true|title=Found Footage 3D|website=wizardworld.com|access-date=2016-08-11}} where it won the festival's inaugural jury award.{{Cite web|url=http://www.fthismovie.net/2016/08/dispatches-from-2016-bruce-campbell.html|title=F This Movie!: Dispatches from the 2016 Bruce Campbell Horror Film Festival|website=www.fthismovie.net|access-date=2016-08-23}} Its international premiere took place at [[London FrightFest Film Festival|FrightFest]] on August 29, 2016.{{Cite web|url=http://www.frightfest.co.uk/2016films/29th-august---discovery-3.html#monday3|title=29TH AUGUST - DISCOVERY 3 - HORROR CHANNEL FRIGHTFEST|website=www.frightfest.co.uk|access-date=2016-08-11}} It screened at 20 different festivals between August and November 2016, winning 15 awards. == Plot == A group of filmmakers travel to a cabin in a remote part of [[Central Texas]] to film the first ever 3D found footage movie. However, they find themselves becoming part of their film and battling for their lives as the evil entity from their film begins to show up in their behind-the-scenes footage.{{cite news|last1=Neato|first1=Vince|title=First Date + Interview With Director Steven Degennaro!|url=http://cinapse.co/2013/10/28/first-date-interview-with-director-steven-degennaro/|accessdate=August 1, 2014|publisher=[[Cinapse]]|date=October 28, 2013}} == Cast == {{div col|colwidth=30em}} * Carter Roy as Derek * Alena von Stroheim as Amy * Chris O'Brien as Mark * Tom Saporito as Andrew * Scott Allen Perry as Carl * Jessica Perrin as Lily * Scott Weinberg as himself {{div col end}} == Production == In January 2014, Kim Henkel announced that production on ''Found Footage 3D'' was underway and that director Steven DeGennaro had written the film's script.{{cite web|last=Barton|first=Steve|title=Texas Chain Saw Massacre's Kim Henkel to Deliver Found Footage 3D|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/73157/texas-chain-saw-massacres-kim-henkel-deliver-found-footage-3d#axzz2xZWBBbQX|publisher=[[Dread Central]]|accessdate=18 May 2014}} DeGennaro, who had been developing the film's concept for two years, stated that he intended to incorporate 3D filmmaking into the movie's plot and that it "isn’t just a marketing ploy". He was inspired by films such as ''[[The Blair Witch Project]]'' and ''[[Scream (1996 film)|Scream]]'' while creating ''Found Footage 3D'' and wanted to utilize the film concept in ''Scream'' in that the film's characters are familiar with the horror genre and the rules and conventions therein. Actress Jessica Perrin was cast in April 2014,{{cite web|title=Found Footage 3D Makes First Casting Announcement|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3272062/found-footage-3d-to-begin-shooting-this-may/|publisher=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|accessdate=18 May 2014}}{{cite web|last=Squires|first=John|title=Found Footage 3D Makes First Casting Announcement|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/75649/found-footage-3d-makes-first-casting-announcement#axzz3229rmcGr|publisher=[[Dread Central]]|accessdate=18 May 2014}} with the rest of the cast announced in late May. Filming began in [[Austin]], Gonzales, and Dale, TX on May 26, 2014 and wrapped in mid-June of the same year.{{cite web|last1=Whittaker|first1=Richard|title='Found Footage 3D' Wraps|url=http://www.austinchronicle.com/screens/2014-07-18/found-footage-3d-wraps/|publisher=[[Austin Chronicle]]|accessdate=1 August 2014}}{{cite web|last=Whittaker|first=Richard|title=Film Flam: El Rey Shines on Austin|url=http://www.austinchronicle.com/daily/screens/2014-06-10/flim-flam-el-rey-shines-on-austin/|publisher=[[Austin Chronicle]]|accessdate=17 June 2014}}{{cite web|last=Miska|first=Brad|title=Found Footage 3D’ Begins Filming In Austin, TX|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/indie/3296583/found-footage-3d-begins-filming-austin-tx/|publisher=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|accessdate=17 June 2014}} The first draft of the film's found-footage script was written in 2012 and the idea for including the 3D concept was developed by "the 3rd or 4th draft", and over the course of two years and multiple drafts, the aspect became "woven more and more deeply into the fabric of the story and the visual planning for the film."{{cite web|last1=Squire|first1=John|title=Exclusive Interview: ‘Found Footage 3D’ Director Steven DeGennaro|url=http://halloweenlove.com/exclusive-interview-found-footage-3d-director-steven-degennaro/|publisher=Halloween Love|accessdate=August 1, 2014|date=June 17, 2014}} == Festivals & Awards == ''Found Footage 3D'' premiered at Bruce Campbell's Horror Film Festival on August 20, 2016.{{Cite web|url=http://dailydead.com/bruce-campbells-horror-film-festival-2016-to-screen-dont-breathe-night-of-the-creeps-abattoir/|title=Bruce Campbell’s Horror Film Festival 2016 to Screen DON’T BREATHE, NIGHT OF THE CREEPS, ABATTOIR -
