Upon release, the film became a commercial blockbuster at the box office.{{cite web|title=మంచి ఐడియాతో తీస్తే... ‘ఐస్‌క్రీమ్’లా ఆర్థిక లాభాలు!|url=http://www.sakshi.com/news/movies/ramgopal-varma-directed-by-ice-cream-film-financial-benefits-in-film-industry-149014|publisher=Sakshi|accessdate=22 August 2014}} ==Plot== Renu ([[Tejaswi Madivada]]) is a rich girl, who stays back alone at her huge bungalow that she recently moved into instead of going to her cousins wedding. On the very first day itself, Renu starts getting some weird dreams about her house being haunted with evil people. A scared Renu calls over her boyfriend Vishal ([[Navdeep]]) to her house for help. Vishal and Renu enjoy themselves at the bungalow. Vishal also pretends to scare Renu a couple of times, but later he too realizes the house is haunted. Rest of the story is as to what happens to both of them, and will they survive this evil haunted house during their course of stay. ==Cast== *[[Navdeep]] as Vishal *[[Tejaswi Madivada]] as Renu ==Trivia== Actress [[Tejaswi Madivada]] was signed as the movie's lead actress and stated that "Working with RGV was a dream come true because he lets you be yourself, gives you space to act without the camera restrictions".{{cite web|title=Working with RGV matters more than a hit film: Tejaswi|url=http://www.business-standard.com/article/news-ians/working-with-rgv-matters-more-than-a-hit-film-tejaswi-114070200295_1.html|publisher=Business Standard|accessdate=22 August 2014}} ==Sequel== In July 2014, shortly after the release of the film, Ram Gopal Varma announced that he was going to make a sequel entitled ''[[Ice Cream 2]]''.{{cite web|title=Ram Gopal Varma to Soon Start Working on Ice Cream 2|url=http://movies.ndtv.com/bollywood/ram-gopal-varma-to-soon-start-working-on-ice-cream-2-560066|publisher=NDTV|accessdate=22 August 2014}}{{cite web|title=20 First looks of 'Ice Cream 2' on 21st|url=http://www.indiaglitz.com/20-first-looks-of-ice-cream-2-on-21st-telugu-news-112337|publisher=IndiaGlitz|accessdate=22 August 2014}} [[Mrudhula Basker]] was confirmed to be the film's lead actress.
