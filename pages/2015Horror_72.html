Solo Media (UK) | released = {{film date|2015|1|22|Peru}} | runtime = 80 minutes | country = Peru | language = Spanish | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Entity''''', also known as ''La Entidad'', is a 2015 Peruvian [[supernatural horror film]] that was directed by Eduardo Schuldt. The film was released in [[3D film|3D]] in Peru on 22 January 2015 and had its film festival debut on 28 August 2015 at [[Film4 FrightFest]].{{cite web|title=Eduardo Schuldt: “Hacer cine siempre es un riesgo”|url=http://larepublica.pe/13-12-2014/eduardo-schuldt-hacer-cine-siempre-es-un-riesgo|publisher=La Republica|accessdate=11 November 2015}}{{cite web|last1=Younker|first1=Nick|title=‘The Entity’ Releases Terrifying New Trailer, Resembles 'Paranormal Activity,' 'The Blair Witch Project' [Watch]|url=http://www.latinpost.com/articles/83410/20150930/horror-film-the-entity-releases-terrifying-new-trailer-watch.htm|publisher=Latin Post|accessdate=11 November 2015}} ''The Entity'' has been billed as Peru's first 3D horror film and to have been loosely based on true stories.{{cite web|title=La Entidad, primera película peruana de terror en 3D (The Entity, the first Peruvian 3D horror film)|url=http://rpp.pe/lima/actualidad/la-entidad-primera-pelicula-peruana-de-terror-en-3d-noticia-745565|publisher=[[Radio Programas del Perú]]|accessdate=11 November 2015}}{{cite web|title=La Entidad se estrenará en Latinoamérica en simultáneo|url=http://rpp.pe/lima/actualidad/la-entidad-se-estrenara-en-latinoamerica-en-simultaneo-noticia-757993|publisher=Radio Programas del Perú|accessdate=11 November 2015}} ==Synopsis== Needing a good final project for college, a group of media students have chosen to film a documentary researching [[Reaction video|reaction videos]]. They're surprised when they find a video featuring someone they know, especially when they discover that anyone who has watched the original video (not the reaction video) has died after they finish watching. Intrigued, the students focus their research, which requires that they visit the [[Deep web (search)|Deep Web]], and they find that the original video is tied to an ancient and deadly curse. ==Cast== *Rodrigo Falla as Joshua *Daniella Mendoza as Carla *Carlos Casella as Lucas *Mario Gaviria as Benjamín *Analú Polanco as Isabel ==Reception== Prior to its release ''The Entity'' received comparisons to similarly themed films such as ''[[Paranormal Activity]]'', ''[[The Blair Witch Project]]'', and ''[[Ring (film)|Ring]]''. Review websites Flickering Myth and Nerdly commented upon these comparisons, as both felt that ''The Entity'' suffered from being too overly familiar to pre-existing works.{{cite web|last1=Wheat|first1=Phil|title=Frightfest 2015: ‘The Entity’ Review|url=http://www.nerdly.co.uk/2015/08/28/frightfest-2015-the-entity-review/|publisher=Nerdly|accessdate=11 November 2015}}[[Twitch Film]] was more positive, writing "Seasoned horror fans will find nothing new here, being able to guess most of the plot beats; but then again, Peruvian directors are just starting to try their hand at a genre which has been around for a long while. As more of them attempt to scare audiences, they'll hopefully improve on what came before.
