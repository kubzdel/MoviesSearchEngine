It all starts with a Poker game; losing the ultimate bet to the worst person. Jaska's a soul hunter; a demon. He's a fugitive from the justice of man and the divine and has plagued mankind for centuries. He and Forneus, his ally, are responsible for a swathe of bloody murders in Barcelona. Jaska ([[Darwin Reina]]) and Forneus are macabre, multilingual and deadly - with a thirst for the taste of the damned. They disguise all of their perversions and their hunger, and don sheep's clothing, then they seek to lure their victims in.
