[[Alexian Kernel|Percy Meza]] | studio = Audiovisual Films | distributor = | released = {{Film date|df=y|2012|10|30|ref1={{cite web|title=Filme ‘Cementerio General’ promete ponerte los pelos de punta|url=http://peru.com/cine/filme-cementerio-general-promete-ponerte-pelos-punta-noticia-37935|accessdate=4 February 2012|work=Peru.com|date=15 January 2012|language=Spanish}}}} | runtime = | country = Peru | language = Spanish | budget = | gross = }} '''''General Cemetery''''' (''Cementerio General'' in Spanish) is a Peruvian [[supernatural horror film]] directed by Dorian Fernandez Moris. Written by Javier Velasquez, the plot is based on [[urban legend]]s in the city's main [[cemetery]]. The film stars Airam Galliani, Nikko Ponce, [[Leslie Shaw]], [[Marisol Aguirre]], among others. It is produced by [[Iquitos]]-based Audiovisual Films company. The filming began in January 2012 in Iquitos. Moreover, it is an indirect [[sponsored film]] due to its ample, solicited [[budget support]] by state-owned and privately held entities.{{cite web |title=Cementerio General |url=http://proycontra.com.pe/2012/01/27/cementerio-general/ |work=Pro y Contra |accessdate=5 February 2012 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120201200221/http://proycontra.com.pe/2012/01/27/cementerio-general/ |archivedate=1 February 2012 |df= |language=Spanish }} ''General Cemetery'' is the first full-length horror film in Peru. The film will include extensive [[Found footage (pseudo-documentary)|found footage]] scenes, a new film style in the country. == Premise == Set in [[Iquitos]], the story follows Andrea (Airam Galliani), a 15-year-old teenage girl, who suffers after the death of her father. Her friends from school convince her to try and contact her father using a [[ouija]] board. However, this triggers a series of terrifying events.{{cite web|title=Filme peruano se basa en 'Actividad Paranormal'|url=http://www.larepublica.pe/14-01-2012/filme-peruano-se-basa-en-actividad-paranormal|work=La República|accessdate=5 February 2012|language=Spanish}} == Production == ''General Cemetery'' was conceived in early 2011. The screenplay was co-written by Javier Velasquez and Dorian Fernández.{{cite web|title="Cementerio general": filman película en Iquitos, con Marisol Aguirre, Nikko Ponce y Leslie Shaw|url=http://www.cinencuentro.com/2012/01/13/cementerio-general-filme-pelicula-peru-iquitos-marisol-aguirre-nikko-ponce-leslie-shaw/ |work=Cinencuentro |accessdate=5 February 2012|language=Spanish}} The story is based in urban legends about the city's main cemetery, which were condensed in the script, and compacted to the investigation of facts. Airam Galliani was selected to her role, when Fernandez-Moris found her in the Ensamble acting workshop. Attempting to follow the [[Found footage (pseudo-documentary)|found footage]] style, Fernández-Moris was influenced by ''[[Paranormal Activity]]'', ''[[Cloverfield]]'', ''[[REC (film)|REC]]'' and ''[[The Blair Witch Project]]''. == References == {{reflist}} ==External links == *{{IMDb title|2271245|General Cemetery}} [[Category:2012 horror films]] [[Category:Peruvian films]] [[Category:2012 films]] [[Category:Films set in 2012]] [[Category:Found footage films]] [[Category:Supernatural horror films]]
