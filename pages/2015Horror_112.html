The film premiered on August 19, 2015 and is inspired by the works of [[H. P. Lovecraft]], particularly ''[[The Shadow over Innsmouth]]''.{{Cite web|title = Travel to Innsmouth for a Teaser and Some Stills|url = http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/126029/travel-to-innsmouth-for-a-teaser-and-some-stills/|website = Dread Central|access-date = 2016-02-05|language = en-US}}{{Cite web|title = INNSMOUTH (Short Film)|url = http://www.starburstmagazine.com/reviews/latest-reviews-of-movies/12980-innsmouth-review|website = Starburst Magazine|access-date = 2016-02-05}} The short received attention for its predominantly female cast, as many of Lovecraft's works tended to feature predominantly or solely male characters. Of this, Lee stated that "''Innsmouth'' was created to make [Lovecraft] roll over in his grave a little by having the cast 98% female and switching the gender roles.”{{Cite web|url=http://diaboliquemagazine.com/innsmouth-izzy-lee-review/|title=Izzy Lee puts a New Spin on Lovecraft with Short INNSMOUTH|date=2016-03-08|website=Diabolique Magazine|language=en-US|access-date=2016-05-26}} == Synopsis == Detective Olmstead has arrived on a peculiar crime scene, with a body that has both a bite wound and an egg sac on her back. Her only clue is a photograph of the victim with a strange woman. Olmstead manages to determine that the photograph was taken in [[Innsmouth]] and travels there in search of answers, which causes her to cross paths with Alice Marsh. == Cast == * [[Diana Porter]] as Detective Olmstead * [[Tristan Risk]] as Alice Marsh * Izzy Lee as Izzy * [[Sophia Cacciola]] as Body * Vera Schränkung as Henchwoman #1 * Porcelain Dalya as Henchwoman #2 * J. Zocalo as Cop #1 * Phil Healy as Cop #2 == Reception == Critical reception for the short has been positive and many outlets such as ''[[Rue Morgue (magazine)|Rue Morgue]]'' praised Risk's acting and character.{{Cite web|title = Film review: Nihil Noctem's Lovecraftian short, INNSMOUTH|url = http://www.rue-morgue.com/#!Film-review-Nihil-Noctems-Lovecraftian-short-INNSMOUTH/cjds/55f04e960cf24af6ea37927b|website = Rue Morgue|access-date = 2016-02-05|last = McKenzie|first = Ron}}{{Cite web|title = NecronomiCon Providence 2015 Review: INNSMOUTH Is Short But Shocking|url = http://twitchfilm.com/2015/08/necronomicon-providence-2015-review-innsmouth-short-but-shocking.html|website = TwitchFilm|access-date = 2016-02-05|language = en-US|first = Ard|last = Vijn}} Dread Central complimented the film for "stick[ing] to the Lovecraftian legend and even has tons of the details of the town correct, right down to the year it was founded and the last name of Marsh, which was a critical moniker in the Innsmouth of the Lovecraftian universe."{{Cite web|title = Innsmouth (Short, 2015)|url = http://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/124574/innsmouth-short-2015/|website = Dread Central|access-date = 2016-02-05|language = en-US}} ''[[Starburst (magazine)|Starburst]]'' also praised Risk's role in the movie and commented that they wished that it had been longer, "as the mythology of the original novella could have been explored even further." ''Diabolique'' magazine commented that the film was "Lee’s most striking work. While she has collaborated with cinematographer Bryan McKay before, ''Innsmouth'' is by far and away their best joint effort." Icons of Fright's review was also positive, stating that ''Innsmouth'' "is exactly what short films should be: a glimpse of the talent behind the filmmaker and their stars, a story that leaves you wanting more, in the best of ways."{{Cite web|url=http://iconsoffright.com/2015/08/26/review-innsmouth/|title=Review: INNSMOUTH|date=2015-08-27|website=Icons of Fright|access-date=2016-05-26}} === Awards === * Best Short Screenplay at Monster Fest (2015, won){{Cite web|title=MONSTER FEST ANNOUNCES 2015 AWARD WINNERS |url=https://monsterfest.com.au/2015/12/01/default-post-example/ |website=Monster Fest (archived) |access-date=2016-02-05 }} * Best Supporting Actress at Vancouver Badass Short FF (2016, won - Tristan Risk){{Cite web|url=http://www.vbaff.com/nominations.html|title=Vancouver Badass Film Festival|website=Vancouver Badass Film Festival|language=en-CA|access-date=2016-05-26}} ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * {{official website|http://www.nihilnoctem.com/#!innsmouth/fvys0}} * {{IMDb title|5022756}} {{Media based on H.
