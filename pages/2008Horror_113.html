[[Grindstone Entertainment Group]] | released = {{Film date|2008|09|16}} | runtime = 93 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = $1,000,000 | gross = }} '''''The Legend of Bloody Mary''''' is a 2008 American [[horror film|horror]]-[[thriller film]] written by John Stecenko and Dominick R. Domingo, directed by Stecenko and starring [[Paul Preiss]], Nicole Aiken and [[Caitlin Wachs]]. The film has an [[Motion Picture Association of America film rating system|R rating]] for its violence, language and sexuality/nudity. ==Plot== {{expand section|date=September 2014}} Ryan (played by [[Paul Preiss]]) has been plagued by [[nightmares]] since his sister Amy (Rachel Taylor) [[Unexplained disappearances|disappeared]] after playing the game "[[Bloody Mary (folklore)|Bloody Mary]]" eight years earlier. Ryan, now a senior at college, suffers from stress and guilt from his sister's disappearance. His girlfriend Rachel (Irina Costa) calls for the help of Father O'Neal (Robert J. Locke), a former professor of Ryan. O'Neal, a priest and an archaeologist, tries to help Ryan by attempting to uncover the circumstances on Amy's vanishing. ==Cast== *[[Paul Preiss]] as Ryan *Nicole Aiken as Nicole *[[Caitlin Wachs]] as Mary Worth *Robert J.
