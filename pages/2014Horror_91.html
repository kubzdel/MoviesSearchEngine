Hogtown Productions | distributor = [[Gravitas Ventures]] | released = {{Film date|2014|3|25}} | runtime = 75 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Happy Camp''''' is a 2014 American [[Found footage (pseudo-documentary)|found footage]] [[horror film]] written and directed by Josh Anthony in his [[directorial debut]].{{cite web|last=Gallagher|first=Brian|title='Happy Camp' Interview with Director Josh Anthony|url=http://www.movieweb.com/news/happy-camp-interview-with-director-josh-anthony-exclusive|publisher=MovieWeb|accessdate=29 March 2014}} It was released on [[video on demand]] on March 25, 2014 through [[Gravitas Ventures]] and was produced through [[Drew Barrymore|Drew Barrymore's]] production company [[Flower Films]]. The movie stars Michael Barbuto as a man trying to discover the truth behind his brother's disappearance years ago.{{cite web|last=Yamato|first=Jen|title=Gravitas Snags Found Footage Thriller ‘Happy Camp’ From Exec Producer Drew Barrymore|url=http://www.deadline.com/2014/02/gravitas-drew-barrymore-flower-films-happy-camp-found-footage-horror-trailer/|publisher=Deadline|accessdate=29 March 2014}} ==Plot== {{more plot|date=June 2018}} In the 1980s Mike (Michael Barbuto) was adopted by Walt and Sandy and was taken to live with them in [[Happy Camp, California|Happy Camp]], a small logging town, with their son Dean. Mike got along instantly with his new brother and was overjoyed that he would finally have a family. This happiness was cut short on October 22, 1989 when Dean is abducted by persons unknown. Mike, the only witness to the crime, can't remember anything about his brother's disappearance, the latest in a string of over 600 disappearances over the last 27 years. Twenty years later Mike's girlfriend Anne (Anne Taylor) has persuaded him to revisit Happy Camp to try to recover his memory of what happened that day. She also persuades him to allow a film crew to videotape the entire process. However rather than have an experience that will help him reconcile with his past, Mike finds that he, Anne, and the film crew are in serious danger. ==Cast== *Josh Anthony as Josh *Michael Barbuto as
