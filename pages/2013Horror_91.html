An original production by [[CineTel Films]] for [[Syfy]], it was directed by [[Steven R. Monroe]] and written by Ryan W. Smith and Sheldon Wilson. The film is set in [[Japan]], but it was filmed in [[Canada]]. ==Plot== {{expand section|date=August 2015}} On October 31, Maiko, a Japanese-born American student at a Japanese University, travels into the [[Aokigahara Forest]] to find her birth mother, who has recently committed suicide.
