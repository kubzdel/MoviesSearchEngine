It stars [[Sprague Grayden]] as a teenager who discovers that a local research company has been creating [[zombie]]s. == Plot == A research company founded by ex-Nazi scientists moves to a small town. Bored, several teens break in and find that the company has been reanimating the dead. Stuck there with the zombies, they attempt to escape with the help of a few cops and employees who are also present. == Cast == * [[Sprague Grayden]] as Laura Forman * David Garver as Steve * [[Al Thompson (actor)|Al Thompson]] as Mike Walker * Jon Avner Sgt.
