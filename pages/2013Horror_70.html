{{Infobox film | name = Devil in My Ride | image = File:Devil in My Ride film poster.jpg | alt = | caption = | film name = | director = Gary Michael Schultz | producer = | writer = Mike Dozier, Gary Michael Schultz | screenplay = | story = | based on = | starring = Frank Zieger, Joey Bicicchi, Erin Breen | narrator = | music = Tim Montijo | cinematography = Armando Ballesteros | editing = Michael Heffler | studio = Devil In My Ride, GMS Films | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2013|10|6|Shriekfest}} | runtime = 95 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Devil in My Ride''''' is a 2013 American horror comedy movie and the feature film directorial debut of Gary Michael Schultz, who also co-wrote the movie's script. It had its world premiere on October 6, 2013 at [[Shriekfest]] and was released to [[video on demand]] the following year. It stars Joey Bicicchi and Erin Breen as a couple whose dream wedding is ruined when the bride gets possessed by the Devil.{{cite web|title=[Shriekfest '13] Trailer and Poster Premiere For ‘Devil in My Ride’|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3254560/shriekfest-13-trailer-and-poster-premiere-for-devil-in-my-ride/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=4 June 2014}} Filming took place in [[Chicago]] and [[Las Vegas]].{{cite web|last1=Moran|first1=Dan|title=‘Devil in My Ride’ film includes Lake County area|url=http://newssun.suntimes.com/entertainment/23906592-421/devil-in-my-ride-film-includes-lake-county-area.html#.U46_ynJdWTJ|publisher=News-Sun|accessdate=4 June 2014}} ==Synopsis== Hank (Joey Bicicchi) and Doreen (Erin Breen) are madly in love and can't wait to get married. They've planned out their special day in painstaking detail, from what to wear to who to invite. Everything seems to be going well until Doreen's brother Travis (Frank Zieger) shows up with a present for his sister, a beautiful but cursed locket that causes Doreen to get possessed by the Devil. Now Travis and Hank have to drive to [[Las Vegas]] in order to find Johnny Priest (Llou Johnson) a homeless exorcist and the last known person to have successfully performed an [[exorcism]]- something made more difficult because they only have 72 hours to find Johnny and perform the exorcism before Doreen's soul is forever lost. ==Cast== *[[Frank Zieger]] as Travis *[[Joey Bicicchi]] as Hank *[[Erin Breen]] as Doreen *[[Sid Haig]] as Iggy *[[Llou Johnson]] as Johnny Priest *[[Zarinah Ali]] as Veronica the Sales Clerk *[[Hud Cantu]] as Groomsman Hud *[[Tom Carlson (actor)|Tom Carlson]] as Gustor *[[Harold Dennis (actor)|Harold Dennis]] as Mechanic *[[Craig J.
