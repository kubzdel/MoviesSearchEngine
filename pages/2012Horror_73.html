Chinese | budget = | gross = }} '''''Empress Vampire''''' (Chinese: ''Huang Hou Xi Xue Gui'') is an American [[horror film]] produced in 2009 and released in 2012. The film is directed by [[Phil Condit (director)|Phil Condit]] and stars [[Ange Maya]], [[Tom Cochran (actor)|Tom Cochran]], [[Beau Nelson]], [[Laura Cotenescu]], and [[Garrett Brawith]].{{cite web|url=http://www.empressvampire.com |title=Movie Empress Vampire Official WebSite |publisher=EmpressVampire.com |date= |accessdate=2010-02-20}} ==Main cast== * Ange Maya as Empress Vampire, Aleta, Empress Yang, Grand Empress Dowager Yang * Tom Cochran as Arthur Hayes * Beau Nelson as Dan Higgins * Laura Cotenescu as Ariana * Garrett Brawith as Ivor Helsing ==Production== The independent film was produced by Ange Maya Pictures and Sick Puppy Pictures. Ange Maya, who played Hathor's Handmaiden in [[Blood Scarab]], began pre-production in August 2008 with director, co-producer, and co-writer Phil Condit. The production ended in August 2009 after a total of 30 filming days.
