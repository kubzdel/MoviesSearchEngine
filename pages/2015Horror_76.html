United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Female Werewolf''''' is a 2015 independent horror film that was written and directed by [[Chris Alexander (editor)|Chris Alexander]].{{Cite web|title = A Female Werewolf Is on the Loose! - Dread Central|url = http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/80116/female-werewolf-loose/|website = Dread Central|publisher = |accessdate = 2015-12-03|language = en-US}} The film stars Carrie Gemmel as a woman known only as "She", who believes that she is turning into a werewolf.{{Cite web|title = Teaser, Images: Chris Alexander's Female Werewolf|url = http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/373207-teaser-images-chris-alexanders-female-werewolf/#/slide/1|website = Shock Till You Drop|accessdate = 2015-12-03|language = en-US}} ''Female Werewolf'' marks Gemmel's third film with Alexander, as she had previously appeared in his prior two movies ''[[Blood for Irina]]'' and ''[[Queen of Blood (2014 film)|Queen of Blood]]''. == Synopsis == The film follows "She" (Carrie Gemmell), an office woman that initially appears to lead a dull life working at an uninspiring office job. At night, however, she has several surreal fantasies involving sex, blood, and a young woman (Cheryl Singleton) she works with at the office.
