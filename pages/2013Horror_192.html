''Strange Factories'' was released on 26 October 2013. == Premise == A writer, possessed by a terrifying story, hunts for its secret heart in a mysterious landscape. He journeys into unknown, dreamlike places, haunted by the infamous Hum emitted from a strange factory.{{cite web|url=http://awakeningartsnetwork.wordpress.com/2011/06/07/storyteller-on-the-writing-of-strange-factories-by-john-harrigan/|author=awakeningartsnetwork|title=Storyteller: On the writing of ‘Strange Factories’ John Harrigan|date=7 June 2011}}{{cite web|url=http://mizmjm.tumblr.com/post/53238946947/strange-factories-my-thoughts|author=Amanda - Illusioned Whispers|title=Strange Factories, A Review|date=18 June 2013|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131029195249/http://mizmjm.tumblr.com/post/53238946947/strange-factories-my-thoughts|archivedate=29 October 2013|df=dmy-all}} == Cast == *John Harrigan as Victor *Annalisa Astarita as Hettie *Rachael Blyth as Emma *Tereza Kamenicka as Lady Thayn *David Monard as Sam *Claire Louise Oliver as Jessica *Lucy Harrigan as Rose *Claire Tregellas as Jess *Mark Postgate as Arlec *Xanadu Xero as Marina == Production == Shot in [[black and white]], and influenced by the [[phantasmagoria]] of theatrical performances which preceded early [[horror film]]s, the production was filmed during September–November 2011 in the Czech Republic. Internal theatre scenes were shot at the [[Kings Theatre, Southsea|King's Theatre in Southsea]], Portsmouth. [[Post-production]] was completed January 2013. The [[Indiegogo]] crowdfunding campaign exceeded its goal, with total funding reaching $12,100 on 24 July 2011, after becoming Indiegogo's Project Of The Day on 29 June 2011. Music for the film was created by composer [[Stephen Baysted]]. == Innovations == Backers of the crowdfunding campaign were given access to 'subscription of experiences' {{cite web|url=https://www.wired.co.uk/news/archive/2011-06/15/gallery-strange-factories|author=Alice Vincent|title=Wired UK - Interview with John Harrigan "Strange Factories offers up 'subscription of experiences' to fan-funders"|date=15 June 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305002404/http://www.wired.co.uk/news/archive/2011-06/15/gallery-strange-factories|archivedate=5 March 2016|df=dmy-all}}{{cite web|url=http://trickstertopia.tumblr.com/post/6903187243/les-fabriques-de-letrange-performance-transmedia-et|author=Nadia Elmrabet|title=Les Fabriques de l’Etrange: performance, transmedia et crowdsourcing - French article|date=2 June 2011|deadurl=yes|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131029192235/http://trickstertopia.tumblr.com/post/6903187243/les-fabriques-de-letrange-performance-transmedia-et|archivedate=29 October 2013|df=dmy-all}} which included [[transmedia]] content delivered by email and web page.
