Tony-Seven Films | distributor = Inception Media Group | released = {{Film date|2013|5|28}} | runtime = 96 minutes | country = [[United States]] | language = [[English language|English]] | budget = $2.5 million | gross = }} '''''A Haunting At Silver Falls''''' is a 2013 American horror film directed by Brett Donowho.{{cite web|title=Spooky Things Happening At Silver Falls in Time For Cannes|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/55111/spooky-things-happening-silver-falls-time-cannes#axzz2w0SlWa2F|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=15 March 2014}} The film was released on May 28, 2013 and stars Alix Elizabeth Gitter as a young woman that must uncover the true murderer of a heinous crime before she becomes a victim herself.{{cite web|last=Turek|first=Ryan|title=First Look: The Ghost Story At Silver Falls|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/167133-first-look-the-ghost-story-at-silver-falls/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=15 March 2014}} It is very loosely based upon a true story Donowho read about two murdered twins.{{cite web|title=Director Brett Donowho On '5 Souls' And His Three Other Current Films!|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3142339/interview-director-brett-donowho-on-5-souls-and-his-three-other-current-films/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=15 March 2014}} ==Plot== The movie starts with a young girl running through a dark wood from a shadowy figure. The scene becomes bleak as a severed hand is seen next to the girls dead body and the body is dragged away. On the hand is a silver ring. Jordan (Alix Elizabeth Gitter) is a teenager that has been orphaned after her father dies of Leukemia. Her mother died from drowning when Jordan was five years old. She's sent to live with an aunt (Tara Westwood), who is the identical twin of her mother, and uncle ([[Steve Bacic]]). They live in the town of Silver Falls, where she learns of ghost stories being told after seeing a burning mannequin at a park where teens go to party. When police come to raid the party Jordan wanders the surrounding forest and discovers the ring, which she places on her forefinger without even thinking. When she returns home she tries to remove the ring, but it won't come off. The ring, it turns out, attracts the ghost of the girl who had mysteriously been murdered twenty years before. No one, however, knows of the murder. The ghost does mischievous acts around the house, such as moving pictures, opening doors, and stealing small items from Jordan's aunt and uncle.
