Last accessed: October 2009. is the DVD re-titled release of 2016 American horror film ''Charlie, Charlie'' directed by Derek Presley. It is an unofficial sequel to ''[[Ouija (2014 film)|Ouija]]'' and ''[[Ouija: Origin of Evil]]'' made by [[Blumhouse Productions]]. This film is completely unrelated and doesn't even feature a Ouija board in the plot but instead the [[Charlie Charlie challenge]] paper and pencil game.
