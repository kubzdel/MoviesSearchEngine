P. Lovecraft]]'s [[Cthulhu Mythos]]. Her new breasts become bloodthirsty and seek world domination. ==Cast== * [[Jessica Zwolak]] as Betty Downer * [[Debbie Rochon]] as Dr. Cate Thulu * [[Paul McGinnis (actor)|Paul McGinnis]] as Tim * [[Michael Thurber]] as Mr. Raquel * [[Lloyd Kaufman]] as Dr. Foin * [[Brooke Lewis]] as the Killer Rack (voice) * [[Sam Qualiana]] as Dutch [[Roy Frumkes]] makes a [[cameo appearance]].{{cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/70934/wrap-killer-rack-new-stills/|title=It’s a Wrap for Killer Rack; New Stills and More!|author=Debi Moore|date=29 September 2014|website=[[Dread Central]]|access-date=26 February 2017}} ==Production== The film was shot in [[Buffalo, New York]]. Make-up effects were created by Arick Szymecki and Stacey Book, while Brett Piper provided [[Stop motion|stop-motion animation]], and [[Chroma key|chroma key compositing]] was used. Director Greg Lamberson stated that the film "is a movie for fans of [[practical effect]]s, although we'll use [[Computer-generated imagery|CGI]] to enhance what we shot on set. Our [[Cinematographer|DP]], Chris Rados, had to do a lot of shooting behind [[Poly(methyl methacrylate)|plexiglass]]".{{cite web|url=http://www.horrorsociety.com/2014/10/01/greg-lamberson-s-killer-rack-wraps-production-buffalo-new-york/|title=Greg Lamberson ‘S Killer Rack Wraps Production In Buffalo, New York|author=Mitchell Wells|date=1 October 2014|website=Horror Society|access-date=26 February 2017}} ==Release and reception== The film premiered at the Scare-A-Con film festival on September 11, 2015, where it won best film,{{cite web|url=http://horrornews.net/101880/killer-rack-announces-world-premiere-and-fest-dates-early-rack-stills/|title='Killer Rack' Announces World Premiere and Fest Dates – Early Rack Stills|date=11 September 2015|website=HorrorNews.net|access-date=26 February 2017}} and played dozens of festivals thereafter, including many international, during which it took several awards including a Best Writer Award for Paul McGinnis. The film was released on [[DVD]] by Camp Motion Pictures on December 13, 2016.{{cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/185619/camp-motion-pictures-releasing-killer-rack-dvd-slime-cityslime-city-massacre-blu-ray-december/|title=Camp Motion Pictures Releasing Killer Rack DVD and Slime City/Slime City Massacre Blu-ray in December|author=Debi Moore|date=30 August 2016|website=[[Dread Central]]|access-date=26 February 2017}} Amy Seidman of [[CraveOnline|ComingSoon.net]] called the film "a fun, no-brainer kind of flick".{{cite web|url=http://www.comingsoon.net/horror/news/748024-review-monstrous-mammarys-attack-greg-lambersons-killer-rack|title=Review: Monstrous Mammary’s Attack in Greg Lamberson’s KILLER RACK|author=Amy Seidman|date=16 December 2015|website=[[CraveOnline|ComingSoon.net]]|access-date=26 February 2017}} Scott Hallam of the website [[Dread Central]] wrote that "you have to overlook a lot of the low-budget pitfalls that often haunt a film like this, but it’s indeed worth looking past the blemishes of the movie to enjoy [its] humor and great spirit".{{cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/126545/killer-rack-2015/|title=Killer Rack (2015)|author=Scott Hallam|date=15 September 2015|website=[[Dread Central]]|access-date=26 February 2017}} ==References== {{Reflist}} ==External links== *{{IMDb title|3369624}} {{comedy-horror-film-stub}} [[Category:2015 films]] [[Category:American parody films]] [[Category:American comedy horror films]] [[Category:American musical films]] [[Category:American black comedy films]] [[Category:2015 horror films]]
