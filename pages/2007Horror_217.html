[[Samoan language|Samoan]] | budget = }} '''''The Tattooist''''' is a 2007 New Zealand [[horror film]] directed by Peter Burger and starring [[Jason Behr]], [[Nathaniel Lees]], [[Michael Hurst]] and [[Robbie Magasiva]] among others. The film is the first in a series of official [[Co-production (filmmaking)|co-productions]] between [[New Zealand]] and [[Singapore]].{{cite news |url=http://www.nzherald.co.nz/movies/news/article.cfm?c_id=200&objectid=10387202 |title=Spirit of partnership as NZ and Singapore sign thriller deal |author=Thompson, Wayne |date=19 June 2006 |work=[[The New Zealand Herald]] |accessdate=21 November 2011}} ==Plot== Jake Sawyer (Jason Behr) is a global wanderer and tattooist who explores ethnic themes in his designs. While visiting Singapore to sell his craft at a local trade show, he swipes an ancient Samoan tattoo tool. After flying to New Zealand to resume his art, he meet up with a lovely Samoan woman named Sina (Mia Blake) and discovers the local Samoan culture. But Jake slowly learns that his stolen tool ends up unleashing an evil avenging spirit whom targets all of the customers that Jake has given tattoos to since his theft of the tool.
