He had previously sequestered himself away with his girlfriend Beth ([[Jessica Messenger]]), however she left him one night in order to see if her family was still alive. Scott's only other human interaction has come via radio communications with a couple hidden away elsewhere, but they're eventually overtaken by zombies, leaving him truly alone. Things seem to be looking up when Beth returns, however when they meet two more uninfected people their problems become even more complex. == Cast == * Shameer Seepersand as Scott * [[Jessica Messenger]] as Beth * Mark Drake as Max * Rachel Benson as Lolli * Gavin Harrison as George * Carl Bryan as Dave == Production == Shooting took place in [[Derby]], England.
