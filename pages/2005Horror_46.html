Believing the man's story, the quartet bring him along when they break into the school one night to look for evidence of the principal's guilt. As the students search the building, they are picked off one by one by a hooded and masked figure who is determined to stop the truth from getting out. == Cast == * Sigrid Josefsson as Elin * Tomas
