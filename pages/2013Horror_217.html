[[Uwe Boll]] served as producer and acted within the film in the role of President of the United States. The film stars [[Christian Boeving]], Mike Mitchell, and Tara Cardinal,{{cite web|last=Vejvoda|first=Jim|title=Uwe Boll's Zombie Massacre|url=http://www.ign.com/articles/2011/09/06/uwe-bolls-zombie-massacre|publisher=IGN|accessdate=17 July 2013}} and is an adaptation of the [[Wii]] video game of the same name, developed by 1988 Games.{{cite web|url=http://www.1988games.com/projects/zombie-massacre/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=July 21, 2013 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131001094022/http://www.1988games.com/projects/zombie-massacre/ |archivedate=October 1, 2013 }} 1988 Games: Zombie Massacrehttp://www.joystiq.com/2011/09/04/zombie-massacre-is-another-gaming-film-from-uwe-boll/ The film had its [[United Kingdom|UK]] DVD release on July 1, 2013. ==Synopsis== As the U.S. government begins experimenting with a [[bacteriological weapon]] in a small town in [[Eastern Europe]], a disaster occurs in which the weapon spreads its effects on the citizens of the area, turning them into mutated zombies.
