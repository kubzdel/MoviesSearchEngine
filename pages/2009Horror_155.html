The film centers on survivors of a feral flesh-eating clan who abduct locals. It is based on the novel of the same name by Jack Ketchum, which is itself the sequel to ''[[Off Season (novel)|Off Season]]''. Pollyanna McIntosh reprises her role in the sequel, ''[[The Woman (2011 film)|The Woman]]''.
