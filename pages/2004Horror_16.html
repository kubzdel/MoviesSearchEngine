The lead characters, one black and one white and both racists, are Donnie played by Andre Ware and Doug played by [[Steve Hedden]]. They co-inherit a trailer park near [[Joshua Tree National Monument]]. Despite their antagonism, they join forces to battle a monster created by retired cultists living in the park and stalking its other denizens. Eventually they discover that they are fraternal twins whose Caucasian archeologist mother [[Shirley Jones]] was married to an African-American serviceman.
