It stars Victor Neri, Anthony Falcon, RK Bagatsing, Timothy Mabalot, Andy Bais, and [[Joel Lamangan]]. It premiered at the [[Cinema One Originals]] film festival, where it won Best Editing, Best Picture, Best Sound, and Best Supporting Actor for Bais. At the [[Gawad Urian Awards]], it won Best Sound and was nominated for Best Cinematography, Best Editing, and Best Supporting Actor for Lamangan. == Plot == At the height of a super-typhoon that could well be the first wave of an impending Apocalypse, five men take refuge in a flooded-in police station and spend the rest of the night with a prisoner who may or may not be the Devil. == Cast == * Victor Neri as Gilberto Pring * Anthony Falcon as Lukas Manabat * RK Bagatsing as Gabriel Ragas * Timothy Mabalot as Nathan Winston Payumo * Andy Bais as Vic * [[Joel Lamangan]] as Benito Alano == Production == Writer-director Dayao is a film critic, and ''Violator'' is his directorial debut. He says the step was natural to him, though he accepts others find it less so. When casting the film, Dayao consciously cast the actors against type.
