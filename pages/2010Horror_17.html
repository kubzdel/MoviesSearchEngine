East plays an office worker who must defend himself against zombies, unhelpful co-workers, and annoying party entertainers. == Plot == On his first day at a new job at a local metal company, Andy attends the boring retirement party of a manager. The party includes clowns, mimes, and magicians, all of whom are eccentric and annoy the office workers.
