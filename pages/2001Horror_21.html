[[Odyssey Video]] | released = {{Film date|2001}} | runtime = 90 minutes | country = United States | language = [[English language|English]] | budget = | gross = }} '''''A Crack in the Floor''''' (2001) is a [[horror film|horror]] film directed by Sean Stanek and Corbin Timbrook.{{cite web|work=[[The New York Times]]|title=A Crack in the Floor|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160307224659/https://www.nytimes.com/movies/movie/238090/A-Crack-in-the-Floor/overview|archivedate=March 7, 2016|url=https://www.nytimes.com/movies/movie/238090/A-Crack-in-the-Floor/overview}} ==Plot== For the past 33 years Jeremiah Hill ([[Roger Hewlett]]) has lived alone, with no contact with the outside world, and he intends to keep it that way. Jeremiah's mother ([[Tracy Scoggins]]) had always warned him not to associate with the outside world, and her brutal rape and killing right before his eyes taught him a lesson he intends never to forget; and those who enter his world will pay a deadly price. As Lehman ([[Mario Lopez]]) and his five friends from [[Los Angeles]] start their weekend camping trip they run into Tyler Trout ([[Gary Busey]]) and Floyd Fryed ([[Rance Howard]]) who represent the gateway into this distorted world that they are about to enter. These things just don't happen in the small mountain town of Sheriff Talmidge ([[Bo Hopkins]]) and Deputy Kevin Gordon ([[Stephen Saux]]). By stumbling upon this lonely cabin in the woods, the men have shattered 33 years of solitude in Jeremiah's world.
