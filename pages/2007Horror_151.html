Porta Produkcija | distributor = | released = 20 July 2007 | runtime = 87 minutes | country = [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]] | language = Croatian | budget = | gross = | preceded by = | followed by = }} '''''The Living and the Dead''''' ({{lang-hr|Živi i mrtvi}}) is a 2007 [[Croatian language|Croatian]] military [[horror film]] directed by [[Kristijan Milić]]. It tells the story of [[Bosnian Croat]] ([[Croatian Defence Council|HVO]]) fighters who, while fighting against [[Serbs] the], face something not entirely of this world. The film was released in 2007. It was filmed in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]], at a small town known as [[Prozor-Rama]].
