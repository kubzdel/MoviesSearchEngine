United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Inside''''' is a 2016 Spanish-American [[horror film]] directed by Miguel Ángel Vivas, written by [[Jaume Balagueró]], and starring [[Rachel Nichols (actress)|Rachel Nichols]], [[Laura Harring]], and [[Stany Coppet]]. It is a remake of the 2007 French film, ''[[Inside (2007 film)|Inside]]''. ==Plot== Sarah Clarke suffers a car accident during the third trimester of her [[pregnancy]], which leaves her husband dead, and her partially [[deafness|deaf]]. On [[Christmas Eve]], she prepares for her child's birth which is due Christmas Day.
