[[Momentum Pictures]] | released = September 9, 2016 | runtime = 90 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = $500,000 | gross = }} '''''Happy Birthday''''' is a 2016 American [[horror film|horror]]-[[thriller film]] written and directed by [[Casey Tebo]]. The film stars [[Matt Bush (actor)|Matt Bush]] and Riley Litman as two friends that travel to Mexico and end up getting kidnapped. ''Happy Birthday'' also stars [[Steven Tyler]] of [[Aerosmith]] as a shaman.{{Cite news|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/videos/steven-tyler-stars-in-weird-horror-movie-trailer-w429342|title=Watch Steven Tyler Throw Cockfights in Horror Movie Trailer|last=Reed|first=Ryan|date=July 15, 2016|work=|newspaper=Rolling Stone|access-date=2016-12-08|via=}} == Plot == Brady and Tommy have decided to travel to Mexico in search of drinks and debauchery to celebrate Brady's birthday and to help him get over his girlfriend's infidelity. Their vacation takes a dark turn after they pick up two American women, Katie and Lucia, who trick the two men and kidnap them as part of a scheme by the local drug lords to gain ransom money from their parents. == Cast == * [[Matt Bush (actor)|Matt Bush]] as Brady Baxter * [[Riley Litman]] as Tommy Quinn * [[Vanessa Lengies]] as Katie Elizondo * [[Britne Oldford]] as Lucia Frankie Allen *
