Written and directed by Luke Ricci, the film stars [[Dameon Clarke]], [[Matthew Gray Gubler]], [[Laura Regan]], and [[George Wyner]]. ==Plot== Mike Wilson ([[Dameon Clarke]]) is a charismatic, educated, and articulate young man who has found his life's purpose in exterminating people. Determined to spread his message about the joy of serial murder, Mike recruits a lost soul named Bart ([[Matthew Gray Gubler]]) to be his pupil and leads his charge through the "ethics" of murder as well as teaching him various lessons in disposing of corpses, balancing work and play, methods of killing, and many more.
