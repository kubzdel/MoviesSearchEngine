{{Infobox film | name = Deadly Revisions | image = | alt = | caption = | film name = | director = [[Gregory Blair]] | producer = | writer = Gregory Blair | screenplay = | story = | based on = | narrator = | starring = [[Bill Oberst Jr.]], Mikhail Blokh, Cindy Merrill | music = Andrew Poole Todd | cinematography = Kate Sobol | editing = Barry Bond | studio = Good Kids Productions, PIX/SEE Productions | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2013|01||Los Angeles}} | runtime = 95 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Deadly Revisions''''' is a 2013 horror thriller that was written and directed by [[Gregory Blair]] and is his [[directorial debut]].{{cite web|title=Murder Your Babies in DEADLY REVISIONS!|url=http://www.aintitcool.com/node/65505|publisher=AICN|accessdate=1 April 2014}} The film stars [[Bill Oberst Jr.]] as a horror writer and filmmaker trying to figure out what caused a gap in his memory and why.{{cite web|last=Squires|first=John|title=New Teaser Trailer Offers Up Deadly Revisions|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/75254/new-teaser-trailer-offers-deadly-revisions#axzz31OnwnIB0|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=11 May 2014}} ==Synopsis== When horror writer and filmmaker Grafton Torn ([[Bill Oberst Jr.]]) wakes from a coma, he's horrified to find that he can't remember the events that led to his coma and leg injury. His physician has recommended that Grafton try to recover his memories by using [[hypnotherapy]] and medication while resting at a cabin in a peaceful location. Grafton agrees to this, but he finds that he's plagued by nightmares that involves not only the characters of his own creation but also potentially real memories. Things are made worse when these nightmares begin to spill over into his waking life, making Grafton question his very sanity. ==Cast== *[[Bill Oberst Jr.]] as Grafton Torn *[[Mikhail Blokh]] as Deter McMannus *[[Cindy Merrill]] as Ally Morris *[[Lise Hart]] as Kat Torn *[[Gregory Blair]] as Crawford Davis *[[Ronny Coleman]] as Doctor Myers *[[Shaun Gerardo]] as Ash *[[Dawna Lee Heising]] as Nurse Voorhees *Josh Patterson as Noose Man / Hatchet Man ==Production== Plans to film ''Deadly Revisions'' were first announced in spring 2012.{{cite web|last=Hallam|first=Scott|title=Take Your First Looks at Feature Film Scare Tactics Read|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/55963/take-your-first-looks-feature-film-scare-tactics#axzz2xcEJ6Tb6|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=1 April 2014}} Initially titled ''Scare Tactics'', the movie was intended to enter pre-production in 2012 and release the following year.
