{{Use dmy dates|date=July 2014}} {{Use British English|date=July 2014}} '''''Dark Night''''' is a 2006 award winning [[B movie]] by writer/director Daniel Grant. The film stars Vaughn Stein, Isabella Maidment, Stephen Mcleod and Anthony Noble. It was filmed in 2005 and finally completed [[post-production]] in the closing months of 2006. It toured the festival circuit in 2007.
