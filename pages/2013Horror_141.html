Uncork'd Entertainment (USA, Canada) | released = {{film date |2013|12|13|Courmayeur Film Festival}} | runtime = 86 minutes | country = Italy | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Neverlake''''' is a 2013 Italian [[slasher film|slasher]] [[horror film]] and the feature film [[directorial debut]] of Riccardo Paoletti. The film had its world premiere on 13 December 2013 at the Courmayeur Film Festival and stars Daisy Keeping, David Brandon, and Joy Tanner.{{cite web|last1=Barton|first1=Steve|title=The Dead Swim in Neverlake|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/53473/the-dead-swim-in-neverlake/|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=17 February 2015}} == Plot == Jenny (Daisy Keeping) is a young English woman who travels to Italy to visit her father Dr. Brooks (David Brandon), a former doctor turned amateur [[archaeologist]]. Her father is currently participating in a dig at a lake that was previously worshipped by the [[Etruscans]] and is surrounded by mystery. Jenny is ultimately unaware that her visit will bring to light several horrible secrets about her father and her past, secrets that could put her very well-being at risk. == Cast== *Daisy Keeping as Jenny Brook *[[David Brandon (actor)|David Brandon]] as Dr. Brook *[[Joy Tanner]] as Olga *Martin Kashirokov as Peter ==Reception== Critical reception has been mixed.{{cite web|title=AICN HORROR: Advance review of SMOTHERED!
