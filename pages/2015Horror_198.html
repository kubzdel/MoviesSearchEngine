Starks | distributor = LeglessCorpse Films | released = March 13, 2015 | runtime = 96 min | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Volumes of Blood''''' is a 2015 [[horror film|horror anthology film]] directed by P.J. Starks (segment "Ghastly"), Jakob Bilinski (segment "13 After Midnight"), Nathan Thomas Milliner (segment "The Encyclopedia Satanica"), John Kenneth Muir (segment "A Little Pick Me Up"), and Lee Vervoort (segment "That’s A Wrap!").{{cite web|last=Peck|first=Stuart|title=Volumes of Blood |url=http://owensboroliving.com/features/volumes-blood/|publisher=Owensboro Living Magazine|accessdate=December 20, 2016}} ==Plot== A sociology student and three friends meet in a library and craft four new urban legends. ==Production== Director P.J. Starks and Jim Blanton of the Daviess County Public Library in [[Owensboro, Kentucky]] developed the "Unscripted Film School" program so people from Owensboro could experience filmmaking for themselves.{{cite web|title=Interview with the Filmmakers of Volumes of Blood|url=http://theindiefilmgroup.com/?p=731|publisher=The Indie Film Group|date=March 30, 2015|accessdate=December 20, 2016}} Their first project was an eight minute horror short and their second would be ''Volumes of Blood''. Using [[Kickstarter]], the crew raised over $4,000 for the film.{{cite web|title=Volumes of Blood|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1024621484/volumes-of-blood|publisher=[[Kickstarter]]|accessdate=December 20, 2016}} ==Reception== ''[[JoBlo.com]]'' awarded the film 7/10.{{cite web|last=Hamman|first=Cody|title=Volumes of Blood (Movie Review)|url=http://www.joblo.com/horror-movies/news/volumes-of-blood-movie-review-265|publisher=[[JoBlo.com]]|date=May 2, 2015|accessdate=December 20, 2016}} ''[[Bloody Disgusting]]'' called the film "the Best Damn Anthology This Year."{{cite web|last=Hicks|first=Jess|title=[Review] ‘Volumes of Blood’ Is the Best Damn Anthology This Year|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/reviews/3347775/review-volumes-blood-best-damn-anthology-year/|publisher=[[Bloody Disgusting]]|date=June 4, 2015|accessdate=December 20, 2016}}''[[Dread Central]]'' awarded the film 3.5 stars and praised it to have "all the right bits in place to entertain the horror masses"{{cite web|last=Boiselle|first=Matt|title=Volumes of Blood (2015)|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/95821/volumes-of-blood-2015/|publisher=[[Dread Central]]|date=April 7, 2015|accessdate=April 7, 2015}} ==Sequels== In a 2015 interview, P.J.
