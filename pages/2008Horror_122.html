Four friends, Camilla, Roger, Mia and Jørgen go for a vacation in a [[forest]]. They stop at an inn and meet people and a girl who joins their group. Their newfound friend takes them on a journey to the deep end of the forest, where they become systematically hunted and killed for sport by a party of locals.
