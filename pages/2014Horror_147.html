She pulls some strings to get her lover, Kosuke Hasegawa ([[Ebizō Ichikawa XI]]) cast in the play, even though he's a relatively unknown actor. Other performers, Rio Asahina ([[Miho Nakanishi]]) and Jun Suzuki ([[Hideaki Ito]]), lust after Miyuki. Offstage, the cast's possessive love and obsessions exist as reality. Trapped between the play and reality, the cast's feelings for each other are amplified.
