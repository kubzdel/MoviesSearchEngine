It stars Randy Baruh, Noah Bean, Kelly Hutchinson, Charissa Camorro, Nicholas DeCegli, and Kate Gersten as a group of friends who encounter "lunar radiation" that causes people to go insane. == Plot == A theater troupe from New York City visit a secluded area known as Moon Lake. Unknown to them, the cabin in which they stay is located on a Native American burial ground among other sites. In addition, it is the location of a lunar meteorite that has bathed the area in radiation that causes people to go violently insane. == Cast == * Randy Baruh as Lenny * [[Noah Bean]] as Chuck * Kelly Hutchinson as Cindy * Charissa Camorro as Ally * Nicholas DeCegli as the giant * Kate Gersten as Sara * [[Lennon Parham]] as deaf mute sister * [[Sarah Saltzberg]] as Jo * Ariel Shafir as Steve * [[Thomas Michael Sullivan]] as Christopher [[Gilbert Gottfried]] and writer-director Dan Fogler appear in cameos. == Release == ''Hysterical Psycho'' premiered at the [[Tribeca Film Festival]].
