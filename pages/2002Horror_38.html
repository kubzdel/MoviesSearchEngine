[[DEJ Productions]] | released = {{Film date|2002}} | runtime = 85 min. | language = English | budget = | music = | awards = }} '''''The Frightening''''' is a 2002 [[horror film]] directed by [[David DeCoteau]]. ==Plot summary== It's Corey Peterson's first day at Hallow End [[High school (North America)|High School]] (nicknamed ''[[Halloween]] High''). He had moved to this small town with his mother due to a traumatic incident in his past. During his first day, he is approached by a fellow new arrival named Mason. His new friend warns Corey that a friend of Mason's, who had attended the school, has died. Mason warns Corey to stay away from the [[wrestler]] Perry and the people who follow him. After two students vanish, Corey attempts to consult with the principal, but gets nowhere.
