[[File:Sleeper_DVD_Cover.jpg|thumb|right|20px]] ==Reception== HorrorNews.net gave ''Sleeper'' a favorable review, writing that "Even though ''Sleeper'' sounds like films most of us have seen hundreds of times it still finds a way to knock its viewer on their head. The film simply flows and there isn’t really any slow moments, Resnick gets free and proceeds to tear through the town."{{Cite web|title = Film Review: Sleeper (2012)|url = http://horrornews.net/53632/film-review-sleeper-2012/|website = Horrornews.net|accessdate = 2016-01-04|language = en-US}} ''28 Days Later Analysis'' wrote "the story is a violent one, but one of better film elements within Sleeper are the many diverse settings", and also noted the "ending is a little vague, but the many action scenes make up for some of the deficiencies."
