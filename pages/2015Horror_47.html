The movie was directed by [[Josh Forbes]], based on a script written by Craig Walendziak, and [[Eric England (director)|Eric England]], who wrote and directed the first film. ''Phase II'' follows the character of Riley ([[Matt Mercer (actor)|Matt Mercer]]) as he tries to find a cure for the zombie virus.{{cite web|last1=Collis|first1=Clark|title=Contracted: Phase II director talks about his horror sequel -- and 'surreal' VMAs experience|url=http://www.ew.com/article/2015/09/02/contracted-2-justin-bieber|publisher=Entertainment Weekly|accessdate=8 October 2015}} The film had its world premiere on July 5, 2015 at the [[Neuchâtel International Fantastic Film Festival]]. ==Synopsis== After the events in the previous film Riley ([[Matt Mercer (actor)|Matt Mercer]]) has begun showing symptoms of the same illness that caused Samantha (Najarra Townsend) to turn into a zombie. Panicking, Riley decides that he needs to track down the person who first infected Samantha, BJ (Morgan Peter Brown), in the hopes of gaining a cure.
