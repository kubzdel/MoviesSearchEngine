It was produced by [[Moonscoop]] and released by [[Classic Media]] and premiered on October 20, 2006. It has a TV series [[Casper's Scare School (TV series)|of the same name]] in 2009. It also has a video game [[Casper's Scare School (video game)|of the same name]]. ==Plot== As a result of Casper being too friendly when playing with a boy named Jimmy Bradley, Kibosh: The King of the Underworld has Casper enrolled into a Scare School headed by the two-headed headmaster Alder and Dash. He befriends Ra, a mummy with unraveling issues and Mantha, a zombie girl who keeps falling apart.
