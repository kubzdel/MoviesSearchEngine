Márquez | editing = José Pepe Tito | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2003|07|01}} | runtime = 20 minutes | country = Portugal | language = Portuguese | budget = }} '''''I'll See You in My Dreams''''' is a 2003 Portuguese short horror film directed by Miguel Ángel Vivas. == Plot == The film is set in a small rural town that is haunted by the [[undead]]. Only one man seems to be able to stop them, and that is Lúcio, a worker whose wife recently turned into a zombie, forcing him to keep her locked in the basement. In a local tavern he finds a second shot at true love, but this blooming romance is threatened by the situation plaguing the town. ==Production== It was produced by a crew of musicians, filmmakers and actors who call themselves O Pato Profissional Produções.
