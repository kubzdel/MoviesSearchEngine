Directed by Andrew Schuth, it was released on [[DVD]] on August 22, 2006. The film is rated PG-13 by the [[MPAA]]. ==Plot== Four friends take a vacation on a boat, with its skipper and three crew. By a campfire one night, the seven discuss the kind of death each fears most.
