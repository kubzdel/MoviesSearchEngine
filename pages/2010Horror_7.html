Tarnation Films | released = {{Film date|2010|5||Cannes}} | runtime = 13 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} "All Flowers in Time" is a 2010 short surrealist [[horror film]] directed by [[Jonathan Caouette]]. The plot centers on a television signal that possesses young children, making them believe they can transform into other people or monsters. The film stars [[Chloë Sevigny]] and has been screened at several [[film festivals]] as of Fall 2010.{{cite web|url=http://www.allflowersintime.com/#festivals|title=All Flowers In Time: Festivals|accessdate=2011-02-17| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110207130302/http://allflowersintime.com/| archivedate= 7 February 2011 | deadurl= no}} Although it shares a similar name, the film's title is not directly connected to the [[Jeff Buckley]] song, "All Flowers in Time Bend Towards the Sun".{{cite web|url=http://dirtbagjournalism.wordpress.com/2010/11/08/interview-jonathan-caouette/|work=Dirtbag Journalism|title=Interview: Jonathan Caouette|date=2010-11-08|author=Kim, Melissa}} ==Release== The film premiered at Cannes Film Festival in Cannes, France in May 2010 and has since been screened at several festivals, including: New York Film Fest, [[BFI London Film Festival]], ZeroFilm Fest, [[Sundance Film Festival|Sundance]], and the [[Portland International Film Festival]].{{cite web|url=http://festivals.nwfilm.org/piff34/schedule/a/|title=34th Portland International Film Festival: Films By Title (A)|work=NW Film Institute|accessdate=2011-02-17}} The film was also shown before [[Darren Aronofsky]]'s ''[[Black Swan (film)|Black Swan]]'' (2010) at Cinema du Parc in December 2010. ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * {{Official website|http://www.allflowersintime.com/}} * {{IMDb title|1753423}} [[Category:English-language films]] [[Category:2010 films]] [[Category:2010 horror films]] [[Category:2010s short films]] [[Category:Films directed by Jonathan Caouette]] [[Category:2010 short films]] {{short-film-stub}} {{2010s-horror-film-stub}}
