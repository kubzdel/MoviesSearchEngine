The film had its world premiere on 14 April 2012 at the Lewiston Auburn Film Festival and was later released to DVD on 9 December 2014 through Big Screen Entertainment Group.{{cite web|last1=Turek|first1=Ryan|title=You Can’t Kill Stephen King Makes Its Way to DVD|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/366423-cant-kill-stephen-king-makes-way-dvd/|publisher=Shock Till You Drop|accessdate=13 December 2014}}{{cite web|last1=Perkins|first1=Dennis|title=Indie Film: Stephen King inspires creators of horror spoof made in Maine|url=http://www.pressherald.com/2012/04/12/stephen-king-inspires-creators-of-horror-spoof-made-in-maine_2012-04-12/|publisher=Press Herald|accessdate=13 December 2014}} The film follows a group of friends that decide to visit the area horror author [[Stephen King]] lives, but find themselves threatened with their own potential deaths. ==Summary== Siblings Monroe (Monroe Mann) and Hilary (Crystal Arnette) have discovered that they have inherited a lake house and to make things even better, the famous horror author Stephen King is rumored to live somewhere nearby. They decide to take their friends down to the lake house to check things out, only to find immediate resistance from all of the locals, who insist that King doesn't live in the area. Despite being completely unwelcome, they decide to stay and soon find that people are being killed one by one in a manner similar to several deaths in various Stephen King stories. ==Cast== *Monroe Mann as Monroe Bachman *Ronnie Khalil as Ronnie *Crystal Arnette as Hilary Bachman *Kayle Blogna as Nicole *Kate Costello as Lori *Justin Brown as Lamont *Polly Humphreys as Deedee *Arthur S. Brown as Dale *Michael Bernstein as Verrill ==Reception== ''[[DVD Talk]]'' gave a mostly favorable review for the film, writing that while it wasn't "outstanding" the film was overall enjoyable and "a well-made and good looking film" as long as viewers did not have overly high expectations.{{cite web|last1=Biltz|first1=Jeremy|title=You Can't Kill Stephen King (review)|url=http://www.dvdtalk.com/reviews/66901/you-cant-kill-stephen-king/|publisher=DVD Talk|accessdate=13 December 2014}} ''HorrorNews.net'' was more negative in their review, criticized the film for being neither innovative or artistic while also praising it for embracing the "obvious clichés".{{cite web|last1=Duncan|first1=Lizzie|title=Film Review: You Can’t Kill Stephen King (2012)|url=http://horrornews.net/77208/film-review-you-cant-kill-stephen-king-2012/|publisher=HorrorNews.net|accessdate=13 December 2014}} ===Awards=== *''People’s Choice – Best Feature film Award'' at the Lewiston-Auburn Film Festival (2012, won){{cite web|title="You Can’t Kill Stephen King" Wins People’s Choice Award at Lewiston-Auburn Film Festival|url=http://dispatchmag.com/you-cant-kill-stephen-king-wins-peoples-choice-award-at-lewiston-auburn-film-festival/|publisher=Dispatch Magazine|accessdate=13 December 2014}} ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * {{IMDb title|1691452}} [[Category:2012 horror films]] [[Category:2010s comedy horror films]] [[Category:American comedy horror films]] [[Category:American films]] [[Category:Directorial debut films]] [[Category:2010s independent films]] [[Category:American independent films]] [[Category:2012 films]]
