Despite being poorly advertised, "Villa Estrella" debuted at no. 3 on its 1st week run earning P 21M in Metro Manila alone, occupying 2nd spot was "Ice Age 3: Dawn of the Dinosaurs" earning P 36.6M in its 1st week run and the top spot was "Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen" which was on its 2nd week run.[http://www.boxofficemojo.com/intl/philippines/?yr=2009&wk=27&p=.htm/ BoxOfficeMojo.com] Philippines Box Office July 1–5, 2009 Villa Estrella on its 2nd week run stayed in no. 3, earned P36,343,703.9.[http://www.boxofficemojo.com/intl/philippines/?yr=2009&wk=28&p=.htm/ BoxOfficeMojo.com] Philippines Box Office July 8–12, 2009 Villa Estrella total gross for its 4-week run is P46,394,496.00.[http://www.boxofficemojo.com/intl/philippines/?yr=2009&wk=30&p=.htm/ BoxOfficeMojo.com]
