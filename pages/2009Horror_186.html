It was produced in the United Kingdom in 2009, and directed by Lee Andrew Matthews.http://www.fleshfuryimageworks.co.uk/shrove%20tuesday.htmlhttp://www.thisisgloucestershire.co.uk/gloucestershireheadlines/Budding-director-wows-Cleeve-horror-flick/article-1491378-detail/article.html It tells the story of Pancake Marion, an 18th-century Witch, who was burned alive for her '[[Blasphemy|blasphemous]]' ways. Her evil spirit returned whenever a protective flower (planted to keep her at bay) had wilted, or was picked by local children. A young girl finds the flower, and thus begins a 22-minute hell ride into your darkest fears. ''Shrove Tuesday'' won Best British Film at Horror UK's 28 Hours Later film festival,http://www.horroruk.com/portsmouthscream/schedule.htmhttp://www.horroruk.com/28/winners.php Best Experimental Short at South Africa's Horror Fest V,http://www.shadowrealminc.com/horrorfest/2009horrorfest.htm and Best Mythic Film at Vampire Film Festival,https://www.facebook.com/vampirefest?ref=mf New Orleans.
