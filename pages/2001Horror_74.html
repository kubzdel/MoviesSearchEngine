Starring [[Kim Myung-min]] and [[Jang Jin-young]], it tells the story of a young taxi driver who moves into a dilapidated old apartment building, the site of a brutal tragedy thirty years earlier. The film has been nominated for and won several awards.{{cite web|last1=Kim|first1=Kyu-hyun|title=''Sorum'' – Korean thriller captures true spirit of classic film noir|url=http://english.ohmynews.com/articleview/article_view.asp?at_code=319395|website=[[OhmyNews International]]|accessdate=2015-05-04|date=27 March 2006}}{{cite web|title=Sorum|url=http://www.koreanfilm.org/kfilm01.html#sorum|website=Koreanfilm.org|accessdate=2015-05-04}}{{cite web|last1=Paik|first1=Peter Y.|title=Sorum|url=http://www.thefilmjournal.com/issue2/sorum.html|website=The Film Journal|accessdate=2015-05-04}} ==Plot== Looking for a new start, a young taxi driver moves into Apt. 504 of an old tenement named Migum Apartments. He soon finds that the previous tenant died mysteriously, and everyone on the floor is connected to the man. After befriending the neighbor living a few doors down, he falls into an affair that sends them both down a dark path. But there's something else odd about the atmosphere in the run-down building.
