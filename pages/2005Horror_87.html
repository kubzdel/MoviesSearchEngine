After appearances at several international festivals in 2005, the film debuted theatrically in Japan on March 4, 2006.{{cite web|url=http://www.allcinema.net/prog/show_c.php?num_c=324152|script-title=ja:ＨＡＺＥ ヘイズ|publisher=[http://www.allcinema.net/ AllCinema]|accessdate=2013-10-16|language=Japanese}} Two versions of the film exist: the original release, a short 25 minute version; and what Director Tsukamoto entitled the "Long Version", which runs 49 minutes.[http://twitchfilm.com/reviews/2005/11/haze-by-shinya-tsukamoto-original-long-version-update.php ''Twitch Film''] ==Plot synopsis== {{more plot|date=June 2018}} A man wakes up in a small concrete space bleeding from the abdomen. He can barely move and has no recollection of why or how he came to be there.
