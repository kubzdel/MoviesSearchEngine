[[Syfy]] (TV) | released = May 3, 2016 (DVD) | language =English | country = United States }} '''''Sharkansas Women's Prison Massacre''''' is a 2015 American action and [[science fiction horror film]] directed by [[Jim Wynorski]] and starring [[Dominique Swain]] and [[Traci Lords]]. It was released direct-to-DVD in the U.S. on May 3, 2016. ==Cast== *[[Dominique Swain]] as Honey *[[Traci Lords]] as Detective Kendra Patterson *[[Christine Nguyen]] as Michelle Alika *[[Cindy Lucas]] as Anita Conners *Amy Holt as Shannon Hastings *Tabitha Marie as Samantha Pines *[[John Callahan (actor)|John Callahan]] as Carl *Corey Landis as Detective Adam ==Production== Wynorski said the idea for the film came to him when he was in the shower.
