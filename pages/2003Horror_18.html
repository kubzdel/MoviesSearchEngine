It stars Jose Prendes as an amnesiac spy who must recover his memories and learn the origin of a zombie apocalypse before the end of the world. == Plot == During a zombie apocalypse, Malcolm Grant, a CIA agent, allows himself to be experimented on by a government lab run by General Morton. Morton believes the only way to stop the apocalypse is by wiping Grant's memories and injecting Grant with the experiences of Quint Barrow, the [[index case]] of the zombie plague. Through Grant's flashbacks as Barrow, they learn that the apocalypse was begun through a deal with Satan.
