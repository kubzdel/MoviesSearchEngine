It is retelling of [[Edgar Allan Poe]]'s classic ''[[The Pit and the Pendulum]]'' with a new twist. ==Plot summary== Seven students answer an advertisement to participate in an experiment to explore how the sensation of pain can be eliminated. Arriving at a secluded institute, they are welcomed by mysterious and eccentric scientist JB Divay (Lorielle New). But when students begin to disappear one by one, they begin to question JB's true intentions.
