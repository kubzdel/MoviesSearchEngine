It stars [[Jim Caviezel]], [[Rhona Mitra]], [[Frankie Faison]], and [[Colm Feore]]. The score was composed by [[Mark Isham]]. ==Plot== A man known only as Rennie ([[Jim Caviezel]]) is motivated by revenge to track down and kill the man who ran over his wife, a serial killer ([[Colm Feore]]) immobilized by the man himself. The killer uses a wheelchair. He drives a [[Cadillac Eldorado|1972 Cadillac Eldorado]] to stalk and kill his victims in car accidents.
