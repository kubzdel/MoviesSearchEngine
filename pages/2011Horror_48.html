The film tells the story of a group of six friends, who after going to a party on an island, mysteriously disappeared, with frightening images of them registered by the cameras. Found by the authorities, these images are the only way to find out what happened to them. With an independent production, the film was shot for fifteen days in [[Ilhabela, São Paulo]]. Filmed as a [[pseudo-documentary]], similar to the American film [[The Blair Witch Project]] (1999).
