Italian | budget = | gross = }} '''''Dante's Inferno: Abandon All Hope''''' is a 2010 black and white film produced and directed by Boris Acosta. The story is based on the first part of [[Dante Alighieri]]'s ''[[Divine Comedy]]'' - ''[[Inferno (Dante)|Inferno]]''. ==Plot== This film is a narrative journey from Dante's own hand, through the worst of the afterlife, Inferno. It is a chronological descent to the deepest of Hell, circle by circle to the exit into Purgatory.
