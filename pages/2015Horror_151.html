Ascher chose his subject because it had happened to him in the past. The film's crew initially began approaching participants via "message groups, [[YouTube]] videos, and a half dozen books that had been written", but found that participants began approaching them after the documentary's premise was announced.{{cite web|last1=Ebiri|first1=Bilge|title=Sundance: Room 237 Director Rodney Ascher on His New Documentary The Nightmare|url=http://www.vulture.com/2015/01/sundance-room-237-director-on-his-new-doc.html|publisher=Vulture|accessdate=March 22, 2015}} ==Synopsis== The documentary focuses on eight people suffering from [[sleep paralysis]], a phenomenon where people find themselves temporarily unable to move, speak, or react to anything while they are falling asleep or awakening. Occasionally this paralysis will be accompanied by physical experiences or hallucinations that have the potential to terrify the individual. In the film, Ascher interviews each participant and then tries to re-create their experiences on film with professional actors.{{cite web|title=THE NIGHTMARE|url=http://www.sundance.org/projects/the-nightmare|publisher=Sundance|accessdate=March 22, 2015}} ==Cast== * Siegfried Peters as Chris * Steven Yvette as Shadow Man * Yatoya Toy as Connie's roommate * Nicole Bosworth as Forrest's girlfriend * Elise Robson as Chris's girlfriend * Age Wilson as Homeless man #2 * [[Heather Langenkamp]] as [[Nancy Thompson (A Nightmare on Elm Street)|Nancy Thompson]] (clip shown from ''[[A Nightmare on Elm Street]]'') * [[Johnny Depp]] as [[List of A Nightmare on Elm Street characters #Glen Lantz|Glen Lantz]] (clip shown from ''[[A Nightmare on Elm Street]]'') ==Release== ''The Nightmare'' premiered at the [[2015 Sundance Film Festival]] on January 26, 2015 before making its way to [[South by Southwest]] on March 13. The film entered a [[Limited release|limited theatrical release]] on June 5, 2015 while also attaining an online release. ===Critical reception=== The film received generally positive reviews from critics.
