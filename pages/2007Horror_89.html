The [[Flashback (narrative)|flashback narrative]] plot follows her trapped in a subway. A Christian [[doomsday cult]], which has been consuming and distributing [[hallucinogen]]-laced muffins that make people see visions of flashlight-eyed demons. On a texted signal, they take over services and begins massacring non-believers throughout the city, believing it is their mission to "save" the souls of humanity for God, which can only be accomplished by killing people with swords and daggers. As a result of the attack, Mike spends a lot of time whining.
