[[Corey Feldman]] | music = Andrew Phillips | cinematography = Ismael Issa Lopez | editing = Andrew McKee | studio = Northern Girl Productions | distributor = | released = | runtime = 85 minutes | country = United Kingdom | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''The Zombie King''''' is a British [[Horror-comedy|horror comedy film]] starring [[Edward Furlong]]{{cite news|last=Scullion|title=Zombie King News #2|url=http://www.gorepress.com/2011/11/07/zombie-king-news-2/|accessdate=7 November 2011|newspaper=Gore Press|date=7 November 2011}} and [[Corey Feldman]].{{cite news|last=Benson|first=Daniel|title=Hail to the Zombie King, Baby|url=http://www.horrortalk.com/news/1767-hail-to-the-zombie-king-baby.html|accessdate=5 November 2011|newspaper=Horror Talk|date=20 October 2011}} The film is directed by Aidan Belizaire and is based on a story by Jennifer Chippindale and Rebecca-Clare Evans.{{cite news|title=Ed Furlong and Corey Feldman to star in UK Horror The Zombie King|url=http://www.liveforfilms.com/2011/11/07/ed-furlong-and-corey-feldman-to-star-in-uk-horror-the-zombie-king/|accessdate=7 November 2011|website=Liveforfilms.com|date=7 November 2011}} ==Plot== Samuel Peters ([[Edward Furlong]]), once an ordinary man, dabbles in the laws of [[Haitian Vodou|voodoo]] to bring his wife back from the grave. He soon encounters the god of malevolence, [[Kalfu]] ([[Corey Feldman]]), and makes a pact with him to destroy the underworld and bring chaos to earth. In return, he will become the Zombie King and walk the earth for eternity with his late wife. But as the growing horde of zombies begins to wipe out a countryside town, the government creates a perimeter around the town and employs a shoot-on-sight policy. Trapped within the town, the locals, an unlikely bunch of misfits, must fight for their lives and unite in order to survive. ==Cast== * [[Edward Furlong]] as Samuel Peters/The Zombie King * [[Corey Feldman]] as [[Kalfu]] * [[George McCluskey]] as Ed Wallace * [[David McClelland]] as Munch * [[Michael Gamarano]] as Boris * [[Seb Castang]] as Simo * [[Rebecca-Clare Evans]] as Danny * [[Jennifer Chippindale]] as Tara * [[Jon Campling]] as Father Lawrence * [[Timothy Owen]] as Neville * [[Anabel Barston]] as Tabitha * [[Jane Foufas]] as Vera * [[Leo Horsfield]] as Scott * [[Sebastian Street]] as Doctor Carter * [[Tanya Kararina]] as Samuel Peters' Wife * [[Forbes KB]] as Gravedigger ==Production== The film went into production in the U.K. on 20 November 2011{{cite news|last=Cooper|first=Sarah|title=Corey Feldman to star in UK indie feature The Zombie King|url=http://www.screendaily.com/news/production/corey-feldman-to-star-in-uk-indie-feature-the-zombie-king/5033538.article|accessdate=5 November 2011|newspaper=Screen Daily|date=19 October 2011}} and was shot over 21 days. ==Release== McCluskey originally planned a release for the film in December 2012.{{cite web|url=http://horrorbug.com/interviews/exclusive-interview-with-british-actor-and-author-george-mccluskey/ |title=EXCLUSIVE: Interview with British Actor and Author George McCluskey |publisher=HorrorBug |date=26 October 2012 |accessdate=20 April 2014}} In 2013, the film was released on DVD in Germany in April, and Japan in May.{{cite web|url=http://www.hemlockbooks.co.uk/News/Blog/news/56 |title=2013: Taste the Blood of British Horror Films | authorlink = M.
