Seth Zuckerman | released = {{Film date|2009|10|31}} | runtime = 80 minutes | country = United States | language = English }} '''''Reality Horror Night''''' is a 2009 horror comedy film created by Sean Pomper and directed by Douglas Elford Argent who wrote it with [[Nicholas Mark Harding]].{{cite web|url=http://www.realityexploits.com/2008/12/30/billy-garcia-tells-us-more-about-reality-horror-night |title=Billy Garcia Tells Us More About "Reality Horror Night"}} The film features mostly reality stars who portray themselves on a twisted new reality show. It was filmed in [[Glen Cove, New York|Glen Cove]], [[New York (state)|New York]] at the Glen Cove Mansion. The film was released on October 31, 2009. ==Plot== The cast enters a mansion to compete on a new reality show for $1 million but with an unexpected twist. Mysteriously, a cast member disappears but without a formal elimination ceremony.
