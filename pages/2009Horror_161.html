English | budget = | gross = P24,491,067 }} {{Use dmy dates|date=May 2012}} '''''Patient X''''' is a 2009 Filipino [[horror film]] directed by Filipino director [[Yam Laranas]] and stars [[Richard Gutierrez]] and [[Cristine Reyes]].{{cite web|url=http://www.filmbiz.asia/reviews/patient-x|title=Patient X|author=Elley, Derek|accessdate=15 September 2010|work=Film Business Asia|date=30 July 2010}} It is co-produced by [[GMA Films]], [[VIVA Films|Viva Films]], and RGUTZ Productions. This is the first movie of Richard Gutierrez under Viva Films Productions.{{cite web|url=http://www.filmbiz.asia/news/eleven-arts-to-care-for-patient-x|title=Eleven Arts to Care for Patient X|author=Frater, Patrick|accessdate=15 September 2010|work=Film Business Asia|date=15 February 2010}} == Synopsis == After twenty years, local police capture the murderer of the older brother of a young boy. Now a doctor, he must go back to his old town and face the murderers himself. He then discovers that the murderers are Aswangs (folkloric Filipino vampire-like creatures) and they pose a deadly threat not only to him but also for the entire town. ==Cast== *[[Richard Gutierrez]] as Dr. Lukas Esguerra *[[Cristine Reyes]] as Guada *[[TJ Trinidad]] as Alfred *[[Miriam Quiambao]] as Nurse Betty *[[Nanding Josef]] as Dr. Jack *[[Elvis Guiterrez]] as Marcus *[[Dion Ignacio]] as Samuel *[[Barbie Forteza]] as Sonia *[[Paulo Avelino]] as Robert *[[Crispin Pineda]] as Mateo *[[Junyka Santarin]] as Mia *[[Jake Vargas]] as James *[[Che Ramos]] as Melinda ==Release== [[Eleven Arts]] has bought the rights for the international sales duties for ''Patient X'' from both [[GMA Films]] and [[Viva Films]]. The film was released in the Philippines on 28 October 2009. ===Reception=== Derek Elley of Film Business Asia gave the film a rating of three out of 10 stating that "The film desperately needed a professional action director, and a proper horror score, to give it some pace."
