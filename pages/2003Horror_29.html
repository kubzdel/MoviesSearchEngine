Brandi-Ann Milbradt | music = | cinematography = | editing = | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2003}} | runtime = | country = [[Canada]] | language = | budget = }} '''''Samhain''''', or '''''Evil Breed: The Legend of Samhain''''', is a 2003 [[Canada|Canadian]] horror film, starring [[Bobbie Phillips]], [[Richard Grieco]], and [[pornographic actor]] legends [[Ginger Lynn Allen]], [[Chasey Lain]], [[Taylor Hayes (actress)|Taylor Hayes]], and [[Jenna Jameson]]. The plot concerns a group of American students camping in [[Ireland]], and studying the legends of the [[Druids]], who are attacked by deformed mutant cannibals from the backwoods. The film was originally produced in 2002–2003, in a longer version with more blood humor and nudity, titled simply ''Samhain'', but languished until 2005–2006, when it was shortened, retitled ''Evil Breed: The Legend of Samhain'', and released on DVD by [[Lions Gate Entertainment]].
