Based on the [[Richard Matheson]] short story "Blood Son", it was written for the screen and directed by Keith Dinielli. ==Plot== In this short film a young man, Jules Walter, gets kicked out of school because he has an unhealthy obsession with [[vampire]]s. While spending time at a local zoo, Jules manages to steal a bat and sneak it away to an old shack by the railyards.
