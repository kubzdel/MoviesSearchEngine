[[Lionsgate]] (DVD) |runtime = 84 minutes. |country = United States |language = English }} '''''The Morgue''''' is a 2008 [[Direct-to-video|direct-to-DVD]] horror film directed by Halder Gomes and Gerson Sanginitto, and written by Najla Ann Al-Doori and Andrew Pletcher. ==Plot synopsis== {{expand section|[[WP:Manual of Style (writing about fiction)#Real-world perspective|plot]] information—must not be [[Plagiarism|copied]] from elsewhere|date=May 2009}} The protagonist Margo Dey is paying her way through college by working part-time in a morgue. Her only [[Wiktionary:corporeal|corporeal]] companion in the long nights at the morgue is George, the night watchman; a man stricken with grief from the loss of his daughter.
