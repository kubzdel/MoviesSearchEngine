{{Infobox film | name = Skinwalker Ranch | image = SkinwalkerRanchFilmPoster.jpg | alt = | caption = | director = Devin McGinn | producer = | writer = Adam Ohler | starring = Taylor Bateman, Steve Berg, Michael Black | music = | cinematography = Michael Black | editing = | studio = DeepStudios | distributor = | released = {{Film date|2013|10|30}} | runtime = 86 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Skinwalker Ranch''''' (marketed in the [[United Kingdom|UK]] as '''''Skinwalkers''''') is a 2013 [[Found footage (film technique)|found footage]] [[Science fiction film|science fiction]] [[horror film]] directed by Devin McGinn. The movie had a [[video on demand]] and limited theatrical release on October 30, 2013 and stars Taylor Bateman, Steve Berg, and Michael Black.{{cite web|title=Found Footage Flick Skinwalker Ranch Gets October Release|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/175585-found-footage-flick-skinwalker-ranch-gets-october-release/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=16 December 2013}} It is loosely based upon folklore surrounding the titular [[Utah|Utah-based]] [[Skinwalker Ranch]], which is rumored to be the site of several [[UFO sightings]].{{cite web|title=First Details for Skinwalker Ranch, ‘Based on a True Story’|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/173553-first-details-for-skinwalker-ranch-based-on-a-true-story/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=16 December 2013}} ==Synopsis== Strange occurrences have been happening on the Skinwalker Ranch, which have culminated in the disappearance of the ranch owner's son. This prompts the dispatch of an investigative team to document the activity and look into the boy's disappearance by way of cameras set up throughout the ranch. The cameras record a series of increasingly eerie events and a warning by a [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|Native American]] man, who tells the crew that their lives are in danger. ==Cast==
