Inspired by actual events from the director's life, FINALE is reminiscent of ''Suspiria'', ''Hellraiser'' and ''Rosemary's Baby'', shot in super 16mm to capture an authentic 1970s feel, with all practical in-camera effects, borrowed from the lost arts of early film magicians.
