[[Azri Iskandar]] | distributor = Golden Satellite | released = {{Film date|df=yes|2004|05|20}} | runtime = | language = Malaysian | country = Malaysia | budget = }} '''''Pontianak Harum Sundal Malam''''', also known as ''Pontianak Scent of the Tuber Rose'' or ''Fragrant Night Vampire'', is a 2004 Malaysian horror film directed and written by [[Shuhaimi Baba]]. Starring [[Maya Karin]], the film is about a restless spirit (''[[Pontianak (folklore)|pontianak]]'') Meriam who seeks revenge upon those who killed her. The film was released on 20 May 2004 and was a major box office success in [[Malaysia]]. A sequel, ''[[Pontianak Harum Sundal Malam 2]]'', was released in 2005.
