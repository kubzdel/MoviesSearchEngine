| special effects= Cassandra Baker | distributor = Petri Entertainment | released = October 29, 2016{{cite web|title=Volumes of Blood:Horror Stories Premiere|url=http://visitowensboro.com/event/volumes-of-bloodhorror-stories-premiere/?instance_id=106751|publisher= Owensboro-Daviess County Convention & Visitors Bureau|accessdate=December 21, 2016}} | runtime = 118 min | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Volumes of Blood: Horror Stories''''' is a 2016 [[horror film|horror anthology film]] directed by Sean Blevins, John William Holt, Jon Maynard, Nathan Thomas Milliner, Justin Seaman and James Treakle. The screenplay was a collaboration by Sean Blevins, Nathan Thomas Milliner, P.J. Starks, and Jason Turner. This installment of the ''[[Volumes of Blood]]'' franchise was produced by Eric Huskisson, David Justice, P.J. Starks, and Devin Taylor. Special effects were designed and created by Special Effects Supervisor, Cassandra Baker. The film is a sequel to the 2015 horror anthology ''[[Volumes of Blood]]'', both of which were filmed in Owensboro, Kentucky. ==Reception== ''[[Fangoria]]'' called the film "hands down, the most
