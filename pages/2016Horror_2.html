Sector 5 Films | released = {{Film date|2016|6|3}} | runtime = 84 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''2 Jennifer''''' is a 2016 indie [[horror film]] written and directed by Hunter Johnson, who also stars in the film.{{Cite web|url=http://horrornews.net/109848/film-review-2-jennifer-2016/|title=Film Review: 2 Jennifer (2016)|last=|first=|date=|website=HorrorNews.net|language=en-US|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-04-18}} The film is a sequel to [[James Cullen Bressack|James Cullen Bressack's]] ''[[To Jennifer]]'' and features Johnson as a young man intent on creating a sequel to the 2013 film.{{Cite news|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/101088/james-cullen-bressack-hunter-johnson-prepping-2-jennifer/|title=James Cullen Bressack and Hunter Johnson Prepping 2 Jennifer|last=Rigney|first=Todd|date=2015-05-11|work=Dread Central|access-date=2017-04-18|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|language=en-US}} ==Synopsis== Spencer wants to create a sequel to one of his favorite horror films, ''To Jennifer,'' and is hoping that doing so will launch his career as a filmmaker. As such, he is playing one of the movie's main characters and wants to find the perfect woman to serve as the film's titular character, even going so far as to insist that they only consider actresses that are named Jennifer.
