Grimm Up North | released = {{film date |2014|9|23|FrightFest}} | runtime = 79 minutes | country = United Kingdom | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''White Settlers''''' (also released as '''''The Blood Lands'''''){{cite news | last = Williams | first = Owen | title =Q&A: Creators Talk the Controversial British Horror Film "THE BLOOD LANDS" | work =[[Fangoria]] | url = http://www.fangoria.com/new/qa-creators-talk-the-controversial-british-horror-film-the-blood-lands/ |date=September 3, 2015| accessdate = 2017-01-03}} is a 2014 British [[horror film]] that was directed by Simeon Halligan.{{cite web|last1=Whittington|first1=James|title=FrightFest: Interview With White Settlers Director Simeon Halligan|url=http://www.horrorchannel.co.uk/articles.php?feature=10244|publisher=Horror Channel|accessdate=5 October 2014}} The film had its world premiere on 23 September 2014 at [[Film4 FrightFest]] and stars [[Pollyanna McIntosh]] and [[Lee Williams (actor)|Lee Williams]] as a couple that find that their new home is not as hospitable as they would have hoped. ==Plot== {{more plot|date=March 2017}} Married couple Ed ([[Lee Williams (actor)|Lee Williams]]) and Sarah ([[Pollyanna McIntosh]]) have decided that they want to get away from their busy, stressful lives in London and move somewhere more peaceful. They believe that they've found the perfect place in a [[bucolic]] farmhouse in Scotland; however, their real estate agent Flo (Joanne Mitchell) informs them that the land is the site of a gruesome battle between the English and the Scottish. Despite this knowledge and Flo's chilly demeanor, Ed and Sarah choose to purchase the farmhouse and restore the property. Initially all seems well, but on their first night they hear strange sounds in the nearby area and discover that they are not at all welcome in the area. ==Cast== *[[Pollyanna McIntosh]] as Sarah *[[Lee Williams (actor)|Lee Williams]] as Ed *Joanne Mitchell as Flo *James McCreadie as Local *Dominic Kay as Local ==Reception== Critical reception for ''White Settlers'' has been mixed,{{cite web|title=WHITE SETTLERS (2014)|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/white_settlers/|website=Rotten Tomatoes|accessdate=5 October 2014}}{{cite web|last1=Newman|first1=Kim|title=White Settlers (review)|url=http://www.screendaily.com/reviews/the-latest/white-settlers/5076578.article|website=Screen Daily|accessdate=5 October 2014}}{{cite web|last1=Cummings|first1=Chris|title=Frightfest 2014: ‘White Settlers’ Review|url=http://www.nerdly.co.uk/2014/08/23/frightfest-2014-white-settlers-review/|website=Nerdly|accessdate=5 October 2014}} and the film currently holds a rating of 57% on ''[[Rotten Tomatoes]]'', based on 7 reviews.{{cite web|title=White Settlers|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/white_settlers/|website=Rotten Tomatoes|accessdate=6 November 2014}} ''[[Bloody Disgusting]]'' and ''[[Starburst (magazine)|Starburst]]'' both praised the movie,{{cite web|last1=Harley|first1=Joel|title=WHITE SETTLERS [FRIGHTFEST 2014]|url=http://www.starburstmagazine.com/reviews/latest-reviews-of-movies/9518-white-settlers-frightfest-2014|work=Starburst|accessdate=5 October 2014}} and ''Bloody Disgusting'' noted "''White Settlers'' couldn’t have been planned more timely.
