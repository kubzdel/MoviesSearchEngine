The film had its world premiere on September 22, 2016 at [[Fantastic Fest]].{{Cite news|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/189686/fantastic-fest-2016-sweet-sweet-lonely-girl-looks-like-return-classic-gothic-horror/|title=Fantastic Fest 2016: Sweet, Sweet Lonely Girl Looks Like a Return to Classic Gothic Horror|last=Gelmini|first=David|date=2016-09-19|work=|newspaper=Dread Central|language=en-US|access-date=2016-12-06|via=}} Filming for ''Sweet, Sweet Lonely Girl'' took place in [[Vernon, Connecticut]] during late 2015.{{Cite news|url=http://www.courant.com/entertainment/movies/hc-sweet-sweet-lonely-girl-shot-in-rockville-to-debut-in-austin-20160915-story.html|title=‘Sweet Sweet Lonely Girl,' Shot In Rockville, To Debut At Fantastic Fest In Austin|last=Dunne|first=Susan|date=September 15, 2016|work=|newspaper=Hartford Courant|access-date=2016-12-06|via=}} ==Plot== {{more plot|date=March 2017}} Adele is a lonely young teenage woman that has been sent to care for her aunt Dora, an agoraphobic who refuses to have any contact with Adele other than a series of messages and rules slid under her door. She seems to have an escape from this bleak existence after meeting Beth, a vibrant woman her own age and Adele's opposite in looks and personality.
