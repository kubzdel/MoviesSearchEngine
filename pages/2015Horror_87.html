{{Distinguish|Ghost Shark (film)}} {{Use dmy dates|date=November 2012}} {{Use New Zealand English|date=November 2012}} {{Infobox film | name = Ghost Shark 2: Urban Jaws | image = Ghost Shark 2.jpg | released = {{Film date|2015|06|27}} | runtime = 76 minutes | country = New Zealand | language = English }} '''''Ghost Shark 2: Urban Jaws''''' is a 2015 [[New Zealand]] [[Supernatural film|supernatural]] [[horror film]] directed by Andrew Todd and Johnny Hall. It was shot on a [[Canon EOS 550D|Canon 550D]] in [[Auckland]], [[New Zealand]], with additional shooting taking place in [[Christchurch]] and [[Los Angeles]]. ==Plot== The plot concerns the mayor of [[Auckland]] and an "expert ghost shark hunter" as they fight to save the city from a supernatural beast known as Ghost Shark. Despite being labelled "''Ghost Shark 2''", it is not related to [[SyFy]]'s ''[[Ghost Shark (film)|Ghost Shark]]'', and the decision to make it a sequel was made for narrative reasons.
