Good news for Ramsay fans. Shyam Ramsay, one of the famous five Ramsay brothers who patented mass-appeal Bollywood horror between the seventies and the nineties, is returning with a new film. The film is called Neighbours and is typically about a family of vampires that comes to live in a peaceful neighbourhood and it stars an unknown cast plus Shakti Kapoor. " The cast includes [[Arbaaz Ali Khan]], Roushika Reikhi, [[Shakti Kapoor]], [[Gavie Chahal]] and Rufy Khan. The film was released on 14 March 2014.
