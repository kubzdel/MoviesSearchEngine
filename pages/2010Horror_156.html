It is a sequel to Greg Lamberson's earlier ''[[Slime City]]''. ==Plot== A dirty bomb has decimated [[New York City]]'s financial district and reduced midtown to a post-apocalyptic nightmare. The neighborhood known as Slime City has been evacuated, except for the homeless, and in the ruins of a soup kitchen four squatters discover a supply of food that transforms them into hideous slimy creatures (like Zachary), driven to murder.
