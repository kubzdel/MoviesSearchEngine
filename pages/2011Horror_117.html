It stars Khristian Fulmer as a cemetery watchman who must contend with undead residents and paranormal occurrences. == Plot == A night watchman at the local cemetery deals with zombies and other residents who refuse to be still. Along with his skeleton sidekick Herbie and boss Claire, he must confront a hostile takeover from a rival cemetery, a werewolf, and Adremalech, a powerful demon. == Cast == * Khristian Fulmer as Rue Morgan * Erin Lilley as Claire Rennfield * Soren Odom as Herbie * Jordan Woodall as Curly * Jonathan Pruitt as Capt.
