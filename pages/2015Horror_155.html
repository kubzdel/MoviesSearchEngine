Sekhar | music = {{plainlist| *Hari Nikesh *Song lyrics by Naveen Krishna (Kannada) }} | country = India | language = Kannada }} '''''Ouija''''' (also known as ''Ouija: Game Never Ends'') is a 2015 [[Kannada]] supernatural horror film produced by Vikram Raju under the Vega Entertainment banner. The movie is directed by Raaj Kumar Reddy.{{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/telugu/movies/did-you-know-/Ouijas-team-haunted-by-ghosts/articleshow/45842795.cms|title=Ouija's team haunted by ghosts|accessdate=11 January 2015|publisher=[[Times of India]]}} The film stars Bharath, [[Shraddha Das]],{{cite web|url=http://www.deccanchronicle.com/141118/entertainment-tollywood/article/shraddha-das-shoots-ouija-langkawi|title=Shraddha Das shoots for 'Ouija' in Langkawi|accessdate=18 November 2014|publisher=[[Deccan Chronicle]]}} [[Gayathri Iyer]],{{cite web|url=http://www.bangaloremirror.com/entertainment/south-masala/Risk-lurks-beyond-glitz/articleshow/46218506.cms|title=Risk lurks beyond glitz|accessdate=12 February 2015|publisher=Banglore Mirror}} Madhuri Itagi, [[Dr. Kadambari Jethwani|Kadambari Jethwani]], [[Sayaji Shinde]], Avinash, Raja Ravindra and Raghu Kunche. The films' music has been composed by Hari Nikesh. The stunt sequences in the film were directed by action director William Ong. All five songs for the film have been choreographed by Raghu Master. The major portion of this film was shot in [[Malaysia]] and Bangalore with a few scenes being shot in Hyderabad. [[Aata (2015 film)|Aata]].
