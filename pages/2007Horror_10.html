'''''The Angel''''' is a [[2007 in film|2007]] short [[sci-fi]] [[martial arts]] [[horror film]] directed by [[Paul Hough]] starring [[Eddie McGee]], Celine Tien and Thomy Kessler. It was inspired by [[Eddie McGee]], a disabled actor who was trained in [[wire-work]].
