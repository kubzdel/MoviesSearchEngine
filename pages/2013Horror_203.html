Brian Weber | production companies = Small Town Girl Productions | distributor = | released = {{film date |2013|9|13|Arizona Underground Film Festival}} | runtime = 84 minutes | country = United States | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Truth or Dare''''' is a 2013 American horror film and the [[directorial debut]] of [[Jessica Cameron]], who also starred in, wrote, and produced the film.{{cite web|last1=Barton|first1=Steve|title=Two New Festival Posters Arrive for Jessica Cameron’s Truth or Dare|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/49571/two-new-festival-posters-arrive-for-jessica-cameron-s-truth-or-dare/#axzz3EImb1M5H|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=30 September 2014}} The movie had its world premiere on 13 September 2013 at the Arizona Underground Film Festival, where it also won ''Best Horror Feature''.{{cite web|title=2013 AWARD WINNERS|url=http://azuff.org/archives/4896|publisher=AZUFF|accessdate=30 September 2014}} ''Truth or Dare'' centers upon a group of college students that publish [[YouTube]] videos that gain them not only world-wide infamy but also an obsessed fan turned deadly stalker.{{cite web|title=A New Trailer For Jessica Cameron’s ‘Truth or Dare’|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/3237244/a-new-trailer-for-jessica-camerons-truth-or-dare/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=30 September 2014}} ==Synopsis== Jennifer ([[Jessica Cameron]]), Ray (Shelby Stehlin), Courtney (Devanny Pinn), Michelle ([[Heather Dorff]]), Tony (Brandon Van Vliet), and John (Jesse Wilson) are all members of a group named the "Truth or Dare-Devils". They're known for their outrageous [[YouTube]] videos where they take the party game [[Truth or Dare?]] to extreme levels, which eventually gains them significant media attention and a decent fan following. It's at one of their guest appearances that they have a confrontation with Derik (Ryan Kiser), an obsessed fan that is angry that they haven't responded to all of his e-mails. He's also upset that there are elements of the group's videos have been faked for dramatic effect and that scenes of gore and violence aren't real. The group ultimately dismisses Derik as just an over-the-top obsessed fan and head to John's home to rest, only to become horrified when they realize that Derik has followed them and is intent on making new Truth or Dare-Devils videos that are all too real. ==Cast== *[[Jessica Cameron]] as Jennifer Collins *Ryan Kiser as Derik B.
