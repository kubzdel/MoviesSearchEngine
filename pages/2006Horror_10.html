A vampire gunslinger from the Old West terrorizes a modern-day town when his bride is revived. == Plot == {{More plot|date=December 2015}} After a female corpse in perfect condition is exhumed, two funeral home employees remove a wooden stake from it. The corpse instantly revives and reveals itself as a vampire. Sensing the return of his bride, the vampire who created her, an Old West gunslinger, comes to the town and begins to turn the people into zombies that serve his will. == Cast == * Susana Gibb as Shannon * Reece Rios as Mikey * Natalie Jones as Molly * Michael W.
