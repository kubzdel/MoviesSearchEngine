After a terrorist attack in [[Leicester]], survivors take cover from zombies. == Plot == After a terrorist attack, Sarah takes her injured father to a busy and overworked hospital, only to lose consciousness during the stressful attempt to save his life. When she comes to, the hospital seems deserted. Sarah quickly discovers that zombies have taken over the building, and, along with other survivors, flees the hospital and attempts to survive against the undead hordes. == Cast == * Ruth King as Sarah * Kris Tearse as Jay * Barry Thomas as Steve * Christopher J.
