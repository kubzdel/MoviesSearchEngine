It is claimed to be Pakistan's first Psycho-[[Thriller (genre)|Thriller]] Feature Film.{{cite web|url=http://www.pakistantv.tv/2014/01/16/hotal-forthcoming-pakistani-moviecastcrewrelease-date-and-official-trailer/|title="HOTAL" Forthcoming Pakistan's First Psycho-Thriller Feature Film / Teaser / First Look - Pakistan TV|author=Web Editor|date=16 January 2014|work=Pakistan TV}} Whilst its also dubbed as the first Hindi feature film outside Bollywood.{{cite web|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1155503|title=Meera’s film 'Hotal' set for nationwide release in March 2015|author=Entertainment Desk|publisher=}} The film's cast has [[Meera (actress)|Meera]], alongside Humayun Gillani, Neha Anil, Tariq Jamal, Baila Naz, Anees Raja, Nisa Jabie, Nasreen Jaan, and [[Wiam Dahmani]].{{cite web|url=http://tribune.com.pk/story/1066155/hotal-checks-in-early/|title=‘Hotal’ checks in early|date=15 March 2016|work=The Express Tribune}} The film was released on 13 May 2016. Upon release, it received largely negative critic's reviews and was a box office failure.{{Cite news|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/1104310/box-office-report-mah-e-mir-makes-over-rs15m/|title=Box office report: ‘Mah-e-Mir’ makes over Rs15m - The Express Tribune|date=2016-05-16|work=The Express Tribune|access-date=2018-05-24|language=en-US}} The same year, critic Omair Alavi added the film in his list of Top Worst' Pakistani films that failed in 2016.{{Cite news|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/magazine/instep-today/165787-Films-that-have-failed-us-in-2016|title=Films that have failed us in 2016|last=Alavi|first=Omair|access-date=2018-05-24|language=en}} == Plot == The film's story resolves around an Indian Girl "Kashika" ([[Meera (actress)|Meera]]) who is expecting her first child. Kashika's husband Naresh (Humayun Gillani) wants to have a son while Kashika wishes to have a daughter. She stays in a Hotel, on the advice of her doctor. There she meets her Sister (from another Mister), who was never Born. Mysterious and incredible events took place at the Hotel, during which Kashika finds out the truth behind the whole scenario. == Cast == * [[Meera (actress)|Meera]] as Kashika * Humayun Gillani as Naresh * Neha Anil * Anees Raja * Nisa Jabie * Nasreen Jaan * Baila Naz * [[Wiam Dahmani]] as Dancer in a Song '''''''Lakshmi Hoon Main''''''' * [[Jasmine Sandlas]] Special Appearance in the Song '''''''Mombatti''''''' * Tariq Jamal * Sadiq A. Khan as Dr. Shaam * Muskaan Jay == Soundtrack == {{empty section|date=June 2018}} == Release == === Critical reception === Omer Bashir of ''[[Dawn.com]]'' gave the film 2 out of 5 stars and wrote ''In the end, while ''Hotal''’s plot failed to connect on any emotional level, it raises important questions about our film industry, the mainly homogenous titles it brings forth and the lack of diversity in roles offered to female leads.''{{Cite news|url=https://images.dawn.com/news/1175361|title=Review: My trip to Hotal with Meera Jee raised serious questions about Pakistani cinema|last=Bashir|first=Omer|date=2016-05-17|work=Images|access-date=2018-05-19|language=en-US}} Tanveer Khadim, blogger of ''[[The Express Tribune]]'' wrote ''''Hotal'', the work of a New York Film Academy graduate, Khalid Hasan Khan, offered nothing surreal and nothing to write home about.'' She concluded her review with upshot ''All in all, ''Hotal'' is a freaky product, not a spine-chilling psycho-thriller.''{{Cite news|url=https://blogs.tribune.com.pk/story/34753/hotal-is-entirley-an-intolerable-piece-of-hogwash/|title=Hotal is entirely an intolerable piece of hogwash|access-date=2018-05-24|language=en-US}} Omair Alavi from ''HIPinPakistan.com'' also criticized the film's plot and states ''''Hotal'' has all the ingredients of a psycho thriller except that the plot is beyond any logic whatsoever.''{{Cite news|url=https://www.hipinpakistan.com/news/1149706|title=In Review: 'Hotal' is a thriller for psychos|last=Alavi|first=Omair|date=2016-05-17|work=HIP|access-date=2018-05-19|language=en-US}} He also disliked the film's music and commented ''One cannot end the movie review without reflecting on the three items numbers – two songs and a dance sequence – that beat even the ''gandasa''-endorsing cinema of the mid ’90s in vulgarity. It’s ironic that the Pakistani censor board didn't chop off these numbers, which were there only to add "colour" to the film.''{{Cite news|url=https://www.hipinpakistan.com/news/1149706|title=In Review: 'Hotal' is a thriller for psychos|last=Alavi|first=Omair|date=2016-05-17|work=HIP|access-date=2018-05-19|language=en-US}} In his review on ‘Stupid Six’ Pakistani movies of 2016, Omair Alavi placed the film on second number.{{Cite news|url=https://www.thenews.com.pk/magazine/instep-today/165787-Films-that-have-failed-us-in-2016|title=Films that have failed us in 2016|last=Alavi|first=Omair|access-date=2018-05-24|language=en}} === Box office === The film was released on 13 May 2016.
