Nordaas | distributor = | released = {{film date|2012|02|17}} | runtime = 76 minutes | country = Norway | language = Norwegian | budget = $10,000 | gross = }} '''''Thale''''' is a 2012 Norwegian [[supernatural horror film]] directed and written by [[Aleksander L. Nordaas]].{{cite web|title=AleksanderNordaas.com|url=http://www.aleksandernordaas.com|publisher=Homepage and projects}} The film was released in Norway on February 17, 2012, with a following worldwide release, sold to over 50 countries. ==Synopsis== Elvis and Leo run a [[crime scene cleanup]] business and are hired to clean up after a death, when they discover Thale, a female humanoid creature with a cow's tail that appears to be incapable of human speech, hidden in a basement. Playing a tape left behind by her captor, Elvis and Leo learn of her life in captivity, and that she has been the subject of medical experimentation. Later, paramilitary soldiers come to recapture Thale. Thale's fellow creatures, [[hulder]]s that are more [[satyrs|satyr]]-like, come to the rescue and leave Elvis and Leo alive. Thale later rejoins the hulders in the wild. == Cast== * [[Silje Reinåmo]] as Thale * Erlend Nervold as Elvis * [[Jon Sigve Skard]] as Leo * [[Morten Andresen]] as Hvittkledd * [[Sunniva Lien]] as Young Thale == Production == ''Thale'' was shot on a budget of $10,000.{{cite web|title=Interview: Thale|url=http://www.fangoria.com/new/qa-thale-director-aleksander-nordaas/|work=[[Fangoria]]|accessdate=12 July 2014}} Aleksander L. Nordaas served as the film's writer, director, co-producer, cinematographer, editor, and the set designer. He built the sets and shooting most of the film in his father's basement. Nordaas has published a clip on YouTube where he talks a bit about the process.{{cite video|title=Video interview: Thale|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LdW6zwnGft8|publisher=YouTube|accessdate=12 July 2014}} Silje Reinåmo commented that she found the role challenging and that the character's nudity made her "very vulnerable and naked, in the true sense".{{cite web|title=Spilte inn huldrefilm i iskald kjeller|url=http://www.nrk.no/nyheter/distrikt/nordland/1.7973724|work=NRK|accessdate=17 January 2013}} == Reception == [[Rotten Tomatoes]], a [[review aggregator]], reports that 55% of eleven critics gave the film a positive review; the average rating was 6.2/10.{{cite web|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/thale_2012/|title=Thale (2013)|work=[[Rotten Tomatoes]]|accessdate=12 July 2014}} Michael Calore of [[Wired (website)|Wired.com]] called it "a little film" that is "large in scope". Calore wrote, "And while some of the gentler, emotional material falls flat, ''Thale'' (pronounced 'tall-eh') does prod the brain and the heart enough for me to recommend it."{{cite web|url=https://www.wired.com/underwire/2012/03/thale-review-sxsw|title=Review: Thale|last=Calore|first=Michael|work=[[Wired (website)|Wired]]|date=10 March 2012|accessdate=12 July 2014}} In a mixed review, John Anderson of ''[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]'' called it creepy but "too wordy by half, saying what it should be showing."{{cite web|title=Review: Thale|url=http://www.variety.com/review/VE1117948280/?refCatId=31|last=Anderson|first=John|work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|date=10 September 2012|accessdate=12 July 2014}} Owen Williams of ''[[Empire (film magazine)|Empire]]'' wrote that the film is "beautifully enigmatic and eerie"{{cite web|title=Review: Thale|url=http://www.empireonline.com/empireblogs/words-from-the-wise/post/p1314|last=Williams|first=Owen|work=Empire|date=4 November 2012|accessdate=4 November 2012|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131003031818/http://www.empireonline.com/empireblogs/words-from-the-wise/post/p1314|archivedate=3 October 2013}} and Mark L. Miller of [[Ain't It Cool News]] said that it is "a truly unique and amazing film".{{cite web|title=Review: Thale|url=http://www.aintitcool.com/node/59318|last=Miller|first=Mark L.|work=[[Ain't It Cool News]]|date=26 October 2012|accessdate=26 October 2012}} John DeFore of ''[[The Hollywood Reporter]]'' wrote that film is "intriguing to a point but unable to exploit its mysteries in a satisfying way".{{cite web|url=http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/thale-film-review-433205|title=Thale: Film Review|last=DeFore|first=John|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=5 April 2013|accessdate=12 July 2014}} Serena Whitney of [[Dread Central]] rated it 2.5/5 stars and wrote, "Although Nordaas does a competent job directing an ambitious low-budget indie creature feature with very little money, the laughable social commentary, failed boo scares and unbelievably cheesy ending make Thale a mediocre, yawn-worthy disappointment."{{cite web|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/reviews/thale-2012|title=Thale (2012)|last=Whitney|first=Serena|work=[[Dread Central]]|date=7 September 2012|accessdate=12 July 2014}} Noel Murray of ''[[The A.V.
