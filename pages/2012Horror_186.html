[[Mackenzie Firgens]] | music = [[KK (composer, producer)|KK (Kevin Kerrigan)]] | cinematography = | editing = | distributor = | released = | runtime = 82 minutes | country = | language = English | budget = }} '''''The Thompsons''''' is an [[Independent film|independent]] 2012 [[horror film]] directed by [[the Butcher Brothers]] ([[Mitchell Altieri]] and [[Phil Flores]]) and a sequel to their previous film ''[[The Hamiltons]]''. It premiered at the [[London FrightFest Film Festival]] on August 26, 2012. Filmed largely in the UK, several scenes feature The [[Ringlestone Inn]] Near [[Harrietsham]], in [[Kent]], south-east of London.[http://theringlestoneinn.co.uk/filmset.php] Ringlestone Inn website ''(retrieved 23 December 2013'') ==Plot== On the run from the law, the vampire family the Hamiltons (now known as the Thompsons) heads to England to find an ancient vampire clan known as the Stuarts.
