The film is based on the 1977 novel ''[[The Amityville Horror]]''. The film follows the trend of films based on [[The Amityville Horror]] having little to nothing to do with the events in the book and only using the location and the DeFeo murders and the [[112 Ocean Avenue]] house as a plot point. The film was directed by Mark Polonia, one of the [[Polonia brothers]].
