Flatmates Jemma (Jemma Dallender), Matt (Joshua Dickinson), and Steve (Nate Fallows) have decided to purchase a haunted mirror off of [[eBay]] in the hopes of winning the [[One Million Dollar Paranormal Challenge|James Randi Foundation’s Paranormal Challenge]].{{cite web|last1=Squires|first1=John|title=High Point Gazes into Haunted Mirror|url=http://www.dreadcentral.com/news/51094/high-point-gazes-into-haunted-mirror/#axzz3EOBGmuxn|publisher=Dread Central|accessdate=26 September 2014}} If they can show proof of supernatural or paranormal activity that can hold up under scientific testing, they will win a million [[U.S. dollar|dollars]].
