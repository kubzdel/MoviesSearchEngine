[[Indian Rupees]] 5 Million | gross = RM 6,314,526http://gengmovie.blogspot.com/2009/04/geng-jelajah-malaysia-habis.html }} '''''Geng: The Adventure Begins''''' ({{lang-ms|'''Geng: Pengembaraan Bermula'''}}, originally '''''Geng: Misteri Hantu Durian''''') is a [[2011 in film|2011]] Malaysian 3D [[computer animated film]]. The film is produced by [[Les' Copaque Production|Les' Copaque]] and released in Malaysian cinemas beginning 12 February 2009. ''Geng'' was launched in a ceremony held on 11 September 2007 together with short animated series ''[[Upin & Ipin]]'' that have connections with the film. Planning for the film began in late-2005 as Les Copaque commenced operations. It received financial support from ICT-related agencies such as [[Multimedia Development Corporation]] and [[MIMOS]]. ==Plot== Bored with nothing to do for their school holidays, Badrol invites his best friend Lim to go on a camping trip at his old [[Kampung]] where he grew up, with intentions to experience some long-forgotten true [[Malaysia]]n life in his Grandfather’s [[durian]] orchard. However their simple holiday trip is cut short by the discovery of a clue that leads them closer to the rumours of the mystery house deep in the forest. Together with their newly found friends, Raju the animal-whispering boy, Ros the village beauty, and her two cute twin brothers, Upin and Ipin, they work together to unravel the secrets surrounding the small Kampung.[http://www.sinemamalaysia.com.my/main/index.php?mod=film&p=info&id=445 Sinema Malaysia] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080331112727/http://www.sinemamalaysia.com.my/main/index.php?mod=film&p=info&id=445 |date=2008-03-31 }} ==Cast== * [[Amir Izwan]] as Badrol * [[Kee Yong Pin]] as Lim * [[Nur Fathiah Diaz]] as Upin & Ipin * [[Balqis Fadhullah Lee]] as Ros * [[Kannan Rajan]] as Raju ==Production== It took two years, RM 4.7 million{{cite news |url=http://rage.com.my/writeups/story.asp?file=/2009/2/18/mustread/20090218110354&sec=mustread |title=Daring adventures |first=Yam |last=Phui Yee |publisher=R.AGE (the Star) |date=18 February 2009 |accessdate=21 February 2009 |deadurl=yes |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090219221727/http://rage.com.my/writeups/story.asp?file=%2F2009%2F2%2F18%2Fmustread%2F20090218110354&sec=mustread |archivedate=19 February 2009 |df= }} and 40 local [[animator]]s to complete ''Geng'', which director Mohd Nizam Abdul Razak compares as cheap against Western animation budgets worth "around RM15 million to RM40 million" and "about 130 to 150 animators".{{cite news|title=Filem animasi Geng sasar RM5 juta |first=Ramlah |last=Zainal |publisher=Harian Metro |date=2 February 2009 |url=https://www.hmetro.com.my/Monday/Rap/20090202090132 |accessdate=14 February 2009 }}{{dead link|date=January 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Les'Copaque Production's Managing Director, Burhanuddin Md Radzi, told that planning the film began when Mohd Nizam, Muhammad Usamah Zaid and Mohd Safwan Ab Karim met him to read out their plan. [[Maya (software)|Maya]] and [[mental ray|MentalRay]] were employed as the main 3D animation software. Nizam added that supply from Mimos worth millions of [[ringgit]] allowed Les' Copaque to perform [[Rendering (computer graphics)|rendering]] in only six to eight months compared to the usual over-one-year period, as well as saving costs.{{cite news|title=Animasi 3D pertama telan RM4j |first=Afiq |last=Hanif |publisher=Harian Metro |date=14 January 2009 |url=http://www.hmetro.com.my/Current_News/myMetro/Wednesday/itMetro/20090114101216/Article/index_html |accessdate=18 February 2009}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}} ''Geng'' was completed in August 2008 at 108 minutes, and was previewed to invited guests from MDeC, Finas, Mimos, [[Radio Televisyen Malaysia|RTM]], [[Media Prima]], [[Astro (satellite TV)|Astro]] and the press. Les' Copaque was also busy completing the ''[[Upin & Ipin]]'' shorts for television which somewhat disrupted post-production of ''Geng''.{{cite web|url=http://gengmovie.blogspot.com/2008/10/gengthe-advanture-begins-status-quo.html |title="Geng: The Adventure Begins" Status Quo |publisher=Les' Copaque Production |date=5 October 2008 |accessdate=18 February 2009}} Les' Copaque prepared five original ''Geng'' reels, three of which were sent to [[Kantana Group]] in [[Bangkok]], [[Thailand]] for audio mixing.{{cite web|url=http://gengmovie.blogspot.com/2008/11/geng-in-film-reel.html |title=Geng in Film Reel |publisher=Les' Copaque Production |date=14 November 2008 |accessdate=18 February 2009}} On November 2008, Les' Copaque announced the [[Film Censorship Board of Malaysia|Censorship Board]]'s approval of their film without cuts and permission to screen by Finas.{{cite web|url=http://gengmovie.blogspot.com/2008/11/gengpengembaraan-bermula-is-coming-to.html |title="Geng: Pengembaraan Bermula" Is Coming To Town |publisher=Les' Copaque Production |date=26 November 2008 |accessdate=18 February 2009}} The music for ''Geng'' is mainly composed by [[Yuri Wong]], who also composed music for the related animated series ''[[Upin & Ipin]]'', along with Mohamed Azfaren Aznam, who contributed music as a second composer. ==Reception== Les' Copaque has been reported to place targets ranging from RM5 million to RM10 million, and even RM100 million after considering international distribution plans.{{cite news |title=Geng sasar kutipan RM100 juta |publisher=Utusan Malaysia |first=Nurulhisham |last=Abdul Latiff |date=25 August 2008 |url=http://photos-g.pe.facebook.com/photos-pe-snc1/v2066/233/120/41946404445/n41946404445_1214838_2473.jpg |accessdate=18 August 2011 }}{{dead link|date=October 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} ''Geng'' opened on 12 August 2011 to incredible public response and rave reviews.
