Austria | language = English | budget = | gross = }} '''''Cam2Cam''''' (also known under the working titles of ''Darknet'' and ''c2c'') is a 2014 [[horror film]] that was directed by [[Joel Soisson]]{{cite web|last1=Donato|first1=Matt|title=Cam2Cam (review)|url=http://wegotthiscovered.com/movies/cam2cam-review/|publisher=WGTC|accessdate=7 September 2014}} and stars [[Tammin Sursok]] as a tourist that becomes the target of a serial killer after logging on to a cam website.{{cite web|last1=Turek|first1=Ryan|title=Trailer Debut, Release Details for IFC Cam2Cam|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/361465-trailer-debut-release-details-for-ifc-cam2cam/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=7 September 2014}} The film was given a simultaneous limited theatrical and [[video on demand|VOD]] release on August 22, 2014. The movie was based on a short film that was directed by Davy Sihali, which Soisson worked into the first portion of the film.{{cite web|last1=Gingold|first1=Michael|title=One Fright in Bangkok: Horror Veteran Joel Soisson Talks His On-Line Chiller "CAM2CAM"|url=http://www.fangoria.com/new/one-fright-in-bangkok-horror-veteran-joel-soisson-talks-his-on-line-chiller-cam2cam/|publisher=Fangoria|accessdate=7 September 2014}} ==Synopsis== Allie ([[Tammin Sursok]]) is a young American staying in a hostel apartment building while travelling through Thailand. A friendly boarder introduces her to Cam2Cam, a popular online chat program that allows people to interact with one another. Curious, Allie logs on and soon discovers that there was a serial killer that used Cam2Cam to find his female victims, one of whom was staying in Allie's current room. After fellow boarder Marit (Sarah Bonrepaux) is murdered, Allie begins to worry that she is next on the serial killer's list. ==Cast== *[[Tammin Sursok]] as Allie Westbrook *Ben Wiggins as Michael *Sarah Bonrepaux as Marit *[[Jade Tailor]] as Lucy *[[Russell Geoffrey Banks]] *Alessa Aichingeras Webcam Performer *Ludovic Coutaud as Creepy nerd *Brett Olsen as Web Performer *Fabian Starr as Web Cam Performance Artist *Kevin Sumethasorn as Inspector (voice) *[[Vincent van Ommen]] as Voyeur *Jenna Z.
