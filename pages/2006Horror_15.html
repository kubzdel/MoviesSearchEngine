It stars Carlee Avers, [[Brad Ashten]], and Soren Bowie. An unrelated sequel, called ''[[Are You Scared 2]]'', was released in 2009. ==Plot== Six young people wake up in an abandoned building, with no idea of what is going on or how they got there. A mysterious figure appears to them over a [[PA system]] and tells them that they are on a game show called ''Are You Scared?'' They will have to face their deepest fears in order to win the contest.
