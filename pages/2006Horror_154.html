It is a [[Cinema of Hong Kong|Hong Kong]]/[[Cinema of Thailand|Thai]] co-production. ==Plot== Ting-yin, a young [[novelist]], is struggling to come up with a followup to her best-selling trilogy of romance novels. She has not even started on the book yet and her agent has already announced that the next title, ''The Recycle'', will deal with the [[supernatural]]. After drafting her first chapter, she stops and deletes the file from her computer.
