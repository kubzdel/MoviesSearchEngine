Brown. == Plot summary == During a horror film convention, several people are invited to an exclusive party. After they consume spiked drinks, they wake up to find themselves involuntary participants in a recreation of [[George A. Romero]]'s 1968 film ''[[Night of the Living Dead]]''. After being attacked, the participants flee to an abandoned farmhouse, which subsequently undergoes a zombie siege. == Cast == * [[Allen Maldonado]] as Duane * Lauren Mae Shafer as Judith * Taylor Piedmonte as Russell * David G.B.
