A group of vampire enthusiasts become convinced that their subculture has been infiltrated by real vampires. == Plot == M inducts Carmila, a journalism student, into the world of [[Goth subculture]] and [[live action role-playing]]. As Carmila becomes more immersed, M begins to have increasing doubts about her safety. Eventually, M comes to believe that real vampires have infiltrated the subculture and feeding on the enthusiasts. M desperately works to stop the vampires, whom he thinks are led by a man named Dahmer, though the others worry about his sanity. == Cast == * [[Blanca Lewin]] as Carmila * [[Juan Pablo Ogalde]] as M * [[Pascale Litvak]] as Pancha * [[Patricia López]] as Elizabeth * [[Claudio Espinoza]] as Martin * [[Carlos Borquez]] as Dahmer The model [[Ximena Huilipán]] has a non-speaking role.
