The movie had its world premiere on July 23, 2014 at the [[Fantasia Festival]] and stars Jon Wachter as a hotdog vendor who finds himself becoming the muse of a manipulative photographer, played by Theodore Bouloukos.{{cite web|title=Bag Boy Lover Boy|url=http://www.fantasiafestival.com/2014/en/films-schedule/64/bag-boy-lover-boy|publisher=Fantasia Festival|accessdate=10 August 2014}} ==Plot== {{more plot|date=April 2017}} Albert (Jon Wachter) works the night shift as a hot-dog vendor on the [[Lower East Side]], where he meets photographer Ivan (Theodore Bouloukos) after having a confrontation with two drunk patrons. Ivan is fascinated by the slow-witted Albert and decides to give him a job working with him at his studio, where he takes fetish photos. Albert is reluctant to participate in Ivan's photos but shows interest in the photography job and lessons that Ivan offers. When Ivan decides to go to a fashion shoot in Milan, he accidentally leaves the studio's keys with Albert- who uses this as an opportunity to lure unsuspecting women to their deaths by enticing them with promises of a modeling shoot. ==Cast== *[[Theodore Bouloukos]] as Ivan Worthington *[[Jon Wachter]] as Albert *[[Kathy Biehl]] as Jackie *[[Karah Serine]] as Nancy (as Kara Peterson) *[[Adrienne Gori]] as Lexy *[[Tina Tanzer]] as Sarah *[[Marseille Morillo]] as Pig Model *[[Teena Byrd]] as Prostitute *[[Sarah O'Sullivan]] as Drunk Girl *[[Saoko Okano]] as Miyuki *[[James Elwood (actor)|James Elwood]] as Officer Rhodes *[[Norman Outlaw]] as Officer Timmons *[[Peter Trojgaard]] as Camera Shop Employee *[[Ryan Preimesberger]] as Drunk Customer *[[Amanda Charles]] as Drunk Customer's Girlfriend ==Reception== Critical reception for ''Bag Boy Lover Boy'' has been mostly positive, with ''Sound on Sight'' and ''Cult MTL'' both listing it among their favorites of the 2014 Fantasia Film Festival.{{cite web|last1=Smith|first1=Justine|title=Fantasia 2014: Justineâ€™s Top 5 Movies Fantasia Film Festival 2014|url=http://www.soundonsight.org/fantasia-2014-justines-top-5-movies/|publisher=Sound on Sight|accessdate=11 September 2014}} ''[[Cult MTL]]'''s Katie Ferrar states that ''Bag Boy Lover Boy'' "makes ''[[Taxi Driver]]'' look like a Disney production with its necrophilia and cannibalism,"{{cite web|last1=Ferrar|first1=Katie|title=The best of Fantasia 2014|url=http://cultmontreal.com/2014/08/the-best-of-fantasia-2014/|publisher=Cult MTL|accessdate=11 September 2014}} while Justine Smith of ''Sound on Sight'' describes the film as "teeming with youthful energy."{{cite web|last1=Smith|first1=Justine|title=Fantasia 2014: 'Bag Boy Lover Boy' is teeming with youthful energy -
