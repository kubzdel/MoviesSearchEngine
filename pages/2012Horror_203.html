It stars [[Patrick Kilpatrick]] and Joseph Aviel. Aviel plays a zombie hunter who tries to protect a group of college students from a mercenary (Kilpatrick) and the zombies that his employer has created. == Plot == Nina, a fashion blogger, goes to [[Youngstown, Ohio]] to shoot a documentary but is caught up in a zombie apocalypse. An ex-soldier and zombie hunter, the Zombinator, appears in the city and tries to protect the citizens from the machinations of an evil corporation and their mercenary, the Colonel. == Cast == * [[Patrick Kilpatrick]] as The Colonel * Joseph Aviel as the Zombinator * Lucia Brizzi as Nina == Production == ''The Zombinator'' was shot in four and a half days in Youngstown, Ohio.{{cite web|url=http://www.wfmj.com/story/19311469/zombie-movie-shot-in-youngstown-to-premiere-on-the-big-screen-this-fall?clienttype=printable|title=Zombie movie shot in Youngstown to premiere on the big screen this fall|work=[[WFMJ-TV]]|date=August 23, 2012|accessdate=November 29, 2013}} Director [[Sergio Myers]] was in Youngstown to film a documentary for fashion website frockOn.com when he was inspired to make a [[zombie film]].
