Richmond]] |editing =Andrew Cohen |budget = |gross = }} '''''Autopsy''''' is a 2008 American [[horror film]] directed by [[Adam Gierasch]].{{cite web|title=First Photos From Adam Gierasch’s ‘Autopsy’|url=http://bloody-disgusting.com/news/8919/|publisher=Bloody Disgusting|accessdate=3 May 2014}} It premiered on August 24, 2008 in the [[United Kingdom]] at the [[London FrightFest Film Festival]] and was selected as one of [[After Dark Horrorfest|After Dark Horrorfest's]] "Eight Films to Die For". The films stars [[Michael Bowen (actor)|Michael Bowen]], [[Jessica Lowndes]], Ashley Schneider, [[Robert Patrick]] and [[Jenette Goldstein]].{{cite web|last=Turek|first=Ryan|title=Exclusive Interview: Adam Gierasch of Autopsy|url=http://www.shocktillyoudrop.com/news/9049-exclusive-interview-adam-gierasch-of-autopsy/|publisher=STYD|accessdate=3 May 2014}} Filming took place in [[Louisiana]].{{cite web|last=Scott|first=Mike|title=Local horror film 'Autopsy' selected for national film festival|url=http://blog.nola.com/mikescott/2008/11/local_horror_film_autopsy_sele.html|publisher=NOLA|accessdate=3 May 2014}} ==Plot== Five friends go to [[New Orleans]] [[Mardi Gras]]. On their way home, they get into a car accident and realize they have hit a man. Before anyone can call for help, an [[ambulance]] arrives. They are taken to a strange hospital where their injuries can be examined. Upon arrival, the five friends begin disappearing one by one. The plot centers on Mercy Hospital where inhumane experiments are being done on the living patients by an insane staff. The "experiments" are brutal, often killing the victim. In one set piece, the internal organs of a patient are hung like a garland over the autopsy table. The experiments are designed to save the head doctor's wife, who has a terminal disease. ==Cast== * [[Jessica Lowndes]] as Emily * Ross Kohn as Bobby * [[Ross McCall]] as Jude * Ashley Schneider as Clare * [[Arcadiy Golubovich]] as Dimitrly * [[Robert LaSardo]] as Scott * [[Michael Bowen (actor)|Michael Bowen]] as Travis * [[Jenette Goldstein]] as Nurse Marian * [[Robert Patrick]] as Dr. Benway * Elijah Hardy as Gregory * Tatyana Kanavka as Gretchen * Eric Adams as Officer Jacobs * Janine Venable as Lisette ==Reception== Critical reception for ''Autopsy'' has been mostly positive.{{cite web|title=Autopsy|url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/1209035-autopsy/|publisher=Rotten Tomatoes|accessdate=3 May 2014}}{{cite web|last=Johnson|first=David|title=AUTOPSY (review)|url=http://www.dvdverdict.com/printer/autopsy2009.php|publisher=DVD Verdict|accessdate=3 May 2014}} ''[[Fearnet]]'' commented that the film took a while to get started, but that "once Autopsy gets moving with its strange sense of humor and its admirable devotion to old-fashioned, over-the-top, Fango-friendly gore-gasms ...
