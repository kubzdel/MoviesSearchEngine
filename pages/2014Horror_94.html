His hopes are dashed, however, when his boss Mr. Whinny ([[Boyd Banks]]) has forced Charlie to take on a job at another cemetery and postpone his retirement plans for the indefinite future. While traveling to the cemetery Charlie meets Faye (Siobhan Murphy), a beautiful [[hitchhiker]].
