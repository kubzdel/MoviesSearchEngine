Daniel McCabe | music = Kristian Day | cinematography = Ron Atkins | editing = Ron Atkins | studio = Atkins Entertainment | distributor = Atkins Entertainment | released = {{Film date|2009|07|24}} | runtime = 82 minutes | country = United States | language = English }} '''''Mutilation Mile''''' is a 2009 American [[horror film]] shot, edited, written, produced, and directed by Ron Atkins. The film stars Lawrence Bucher and Daniel McCabe as Jack and Jimmy DeGrasso, who, on a mission to find the killer of their beloved uncle Sal, murder everyone who crosses their path.
